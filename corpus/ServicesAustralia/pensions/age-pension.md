# Age Pension

The main income support payment for people who are Age Pension age, which is 67 years or older.

To get Age Pension you must be all of the following:

- 67 years or older
- under the income and assets test limits
- an Australian resident, normally for at least 10 years.

Read the full conditions under [who can get it](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-age-pension?context=22526).
