# Pensioner Education Supplement

Pensioner Education Supplement (PES) is a regular extra payment to help with your study costs if you get certain payments.

To get PES you must be doing all the following:

- getting a payment from us or the Department of Veterans’ Affairs that has this benefit as an add on
- studying an approved course
- studying full time or in special circumstances, as a part time student.

Read the full conditions for [who can get it](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-pensioner-education-supplement?context=22201).

[Who can get it\\
\\
\\
You can get the supplement if you meet study rules and get certain payments from us or the Department of Veterans’ Affairs.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-pensioner-education-supplement?context=22201)

[How much you can get\\
\\
\\
Depending on your circumstances, there are 2 different rates of Pensioner Education Supplement you can get.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-much-pensioner-education-supplement-you-can-get?context=22201)

[How to claim\\
\\
\\
Complete the steps on this page to claim Pensioner Education Supplement. Before you start, check if you can get it.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-claim-pensioner-education-supplement?context=22201)

[How to manage your payment\\
\\
\\
If you get the Pensioner Education Supplement, you need to tell us if your circumstances change, including going overseas or changing your study.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-manage-your-pensioner-education-supplement?context=22201)
