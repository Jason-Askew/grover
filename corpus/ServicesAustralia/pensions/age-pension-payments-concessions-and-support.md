# Payments, concessions and support

There are other payments, concessions and help available for people who get Age Pension.

## on this page

-   [Payments](#a1)
-   [Concessions](#a2)
-   [Support](#a3)

## Payments

If you get Age Pension, there are other payments and supplements you may be eligible for.

### Pension Supplement

To help with your bills and medicine costs, you may get an extra [Pension Supplement](/pension-supplement) with your Age Pension.

### Energy Supplement

If you get Age Pension you may get an extra payment to help with your energy costs.

### Carer Allowance

If you give daily support to someone, you may get [Carer Allowance](/carer-allowance) each fortnight. The person you care for must meet all of the following:

-   need care for at least 12 months, or have a terminal medical condition
-   be assessed as needing care by a medical professional
-   be able to receive care in their home or your home
-   meet the [residence rules](/residence-rules-for-carer-allowance?context=21811).

### Essential Medical Equipment Payment

If you need help with energy costs, you may get an [Essential Medical Equipment Payment](/essential-medical-equipment-payment) each year. You can get this payment if you or the person you care for meet all of the following:

-   need heating, cooling or certain [eligible equipment](/eligible-equipment-for-essential-medical-equipment-payment) for [medical needs](/heating-or-cooling-for-medical-needs)
-   have a [Commonwealth Concession Card](/concession-and-health-care-cards)
-   pay for the energy running costs
-   be in Australia when you claim.

### Home Equity Access Scheme

If you need a little extra to live on each fortnight, you can apply for a voluntary non-taxable loan. You must qualify for Age Pension and own a property in Australia to be eligible. Under the [Home Equity Access Scheme](/home-equity-access-scheme) you’ll get a payment each fortnight and you can pay it back at any time. The loan accrues compound interest each fortnight until you repay the loan in full.

### Remote Area Allowance

If you live in an identified remote area you may get [Remote Area Allowance](/remote-area-allowance) each fortnight.

### Rent Assistance

If you pay rent, you may get [Rent Assistance](/rent-assistance) with your Age Pension to help with this cost.

## Concessions

If you get Age Pension, you’ll also get a [Pensioner Concession Card](/pensioner-concession-card) which gives you:

-   cheaper health care
-   cheaper medicine
-   some other discounts in your community.

Read more about the [benefits of this card](/benefits-pensioner-concession-card).

## Support

We have support services you may be interested in.

### Centrepay

[Centrepay](/centrepay) is a free and voluntary service to help you pay bills and expenses as regular deductions from your Age Pension.

### Financial Information Service

Our free [Financial Information Service](/financial-information-service) can inform and educate you about financial matters.

### Advance payment

You may be able to get part of your Age Pension as an [advance payment](/advance-payment?context=22526). You pay it back later out of your payments from us.

### Rent Deduction Scheme

Use the [Rent Deduction Scheme](/rent-deduction-scheme) to pay your public housing rent straight from your Age Pension.

### Deduct tax from your payment

Age Pension is a taxable payment. You can ask us to [deduct tax](/paying-tax-centrelink-payment?context=22526) from your Age Pension to help reduce how much tax you may have to pay at the end of the tax year.

### Grandparent, Foster and Kinship Carer Advisers

Our [Grandparent, Foster and Kinship Carer Advisers](/grandparent-foster-and-kinship-carer-advisers) help grandparents and non parent carers who provide ongoing care for children.

### Centrelink Indigenous Call Centre

Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander Australians can call the [Centrelink Indigenous Call Centre](/centrelink-indigenous-call-centre) about our payments and services.

### Multicultural Service Officers

Our [Multicultural Service Officers](/multicultural-service-officers) help migrant and refugee communities connect with our services.

### Social work services

If you’re going through a difficult time, our [social workers](/social-work-services) can help you.

### Deduct tax from your payment

Age Pension is a taxable payment. You can ask us to [deduct tax](/deduct-tax-from-your-payment?context=22526) from your Age Pension. This may help to reduce how much tax you may have to pay at the end of the financial year. 

### Work Bonus

You can earn more income from working, if you’re an eligible pensioner, without reducing your Age Pension. We call this the [Work Bonus](/work-bonus).
