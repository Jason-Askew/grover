# Pension Bonus Top Up

An extra payment if you've already got a Pension Bonus payment.

[Who can get it\\
\\
\\
You may get a Pension Bonus Top Up if you get both Age Pension and Pension Bonus payments.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-pension-bonus-top-up?context=22541)

[How much you can get\\
\\
\\
How much Pension Bonus top up you can get depends on how much Age Pension we paid you.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-much-pension-bonus-top-up-you-can-get?context=22541)
