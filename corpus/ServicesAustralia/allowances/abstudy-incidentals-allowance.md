# ABSTUDY Incidentals Allowance

A payment to help Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Australians with the cost of starting or continuing study or training.

Incidentals Allowance is a once a year payment. We pay it:

-   when you start a course, apprenticeship or traineeship
-   up to 4 weeks before you return to study in the new year, if you’re continuing a course
-   at the start of each year you do your apprenticeship or traineeship.
