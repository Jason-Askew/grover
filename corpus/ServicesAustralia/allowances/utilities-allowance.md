# Utilities Allowance

A quarterly payment to help with household bills if you get certain payments from us.

[Who can get it\\
\\
\\
You get this allowance if you get certain payments from us. You don’t need to apply for it.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-utilities-allowance?context=22631)

[How much you can get\\
\\
\\
We’ll pay the allowance with your regular payment. We pay you on or after 20 March, 20 June, 20 September and 20 December each year.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-much-utilities-allowance-you-can-get?context=22631)

[How to manage your allowance\\
\\
\\
Your options and obligations when you get this payment.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-manage-your-utilities-allowance?context=22631)
