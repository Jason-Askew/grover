# Temporary illness or medical condition

You may get Pharmaceutical Allowance if you have a temporary illness or medical condition.

To get the allowance you must also get one of the following payments:

-   [ABSTUDY](/abstudy)
-   [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment) partnered
-   [Special Benefit](/special-benefit)
-   [Youth Allowance](/youth-allowance)
-   [Farm Household Allowance](/farm-household-allowance).

If you have a partial capacity to work, you can get it if you get any of the following payments:

-   [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment) partnered
-   [Youth Allowance](/youth-allowance) as a jobseeker.
