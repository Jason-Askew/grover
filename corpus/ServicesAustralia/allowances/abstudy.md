# ABSTUDY

Financial help for Australian Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander students or apprentices.

Depending on a student’s circumstances, ABSTUDY can help with:

- school fees
- boarding fees
- living costs
- travel costs
- buying school materials.

To get ABSTUDY you must:

- be an Australian citizen
- be an Australian Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander person
- normally live in Australia
- be an Australian Apprentice or a student enrolled in an approved course or undertaking a Testing and Assessment activity
- not be in receipt of any other government or other financial assistance to study or complete your apprenticeship or traineeship.
