# Fares Allowance

Helps with travel costs between your permanent home and your place of tertiary study in Australia.

If you get ABSTUDY, read about [ABSTUDY Fares Allowance](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/abstudy-fares-allowance) instead.

[Who can get it\\
\\
\\
You must be a tertiary student living away from home to study to get Fares Allowance.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-fares-allowance?context=22156)

[What we pay it for\\
\\
\\
If you’re eligible, you can get Fares Allowance for the following situations.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/what-we-pay-fares-allowance-for?context=22156)

[When to claim\\
\\
\\
You can get Fares Allowance after your journey or we can pre-book your travel for you.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/when-to-claim-fares-allowance?context=22156)
