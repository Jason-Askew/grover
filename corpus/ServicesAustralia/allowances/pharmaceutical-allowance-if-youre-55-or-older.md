# 55 and older

You may get Pharmaceutical Allowance when you are 55 or older and get certain payments from us.

You can get it if you’re 55 and get [ABSTUDY](/abstudy).

If you’re between 55 and Age Pension age, you can also get it. But you must have been on 1 of the following payments for at least 9 months:

-   [Austudy](/austudy)
-   [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment) partnered
-   [Special Benefit](/special-benefit).
