# Youth Allowance for students and Australian Apprentices

Financial help if you’re 24 or younger and a student or Australian Apprentice.

For Centrelink payments and services, you can call our [multilingual phone service](/phone-us#languages).

To get this you must be one of the following:

-   18 to 24 and studying full time
-   16 to 17, studying full time and either independent or needing to live away from home to study
-   16 to 17, studying full time and have completed year 12 or equivalent
-   16 to 24 and doing a full time Australian Apprenticeship.

Read the full conditions under [who can get it](/who-can-get-youth-allowance-for-students-and-apprentices?context=43916).

3.  Report and [keep your payment](/how-to-report-and-manage-your-youth-allowance-for-students-and-apprentices-payment?context=43916).
