# ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance higher rate

Helps pay school and boarding fees for Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander Australian students in high school.

This allowance helps students who can’t attend high school while living at home. For example, they may live in a remote community and need to go to a boarding school.

Some students who live at home may also be able to get it in special circumstances.

What it's for

ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance higher rate is for fees at schools that aren’t public high schools. Often this is a boarding school.
