# Maximum rates for independent students and Australian Apprentices

These payment rates are only a guide. They're for full time and approved concessional study load students and apprentices.

Use our [Payment Finder](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/apps/clkonline_cof/payment-service-finder/payments-finder) to work out how much you might get.

We update the rates each year on the following dates:

-   1 January for people younger than 22
-   20 March and 20 September for people 22 or older.

The tables are a guide only.
