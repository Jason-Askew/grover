# Youth Allowance for job seekers

Financial help if you’re 21 or younger and looking for work. It’s also for when you can’t work while you’re sick or injured.

For Centrelink payments and services, you can call our [multilingual phone service](/phone-us#languages).

To get this payment, you must be 16 to 21 and one of the following:

-   looking for full time work
-   temporarily unable to work or study while sick or injured.

Read the full conditions under [who can get it](/who-can-get-youth-allowance-for-job-seekers?context=43921).

Read more about [Youth Allowance for students and Australian Apprentices](/youth-allowance-for-students-and-australian-apprentices) if you’re both:

-   aged 24 or younger
-   a full time student or Australian Apprentice.

This may be better for your situation.
