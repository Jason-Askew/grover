# Mobility Allowance

A payment to help with travel costs for work, study or looking for work.

You may be eligible if you have a disability, illness or injury that means you canâ€™t use public transport without a lot of help.

To get this you must:

- be 16 years or older
- be unable to use public transport without a lot of help
- have a disability, illness, or injury
- need to travel for study, training, work or to look for work.

Read the full conditions under [who can get it](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-mobility-allowance?context=22501).
