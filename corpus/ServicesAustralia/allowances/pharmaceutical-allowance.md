# Pharmaceutical Allowance

A regular extra payment to help with medicine costs if you get certain payments from us.

[Who can get it\\
\\
\\
You can get the Pharmaceutical Allowance if you meet certain conditions.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-pharmaceutical-allowance?context=22011)

[How much you can get\\
\\
\\
You’ll get the allowance with your regular payment each fortnight.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-much-pharmaceutical-allowance-you-can-get?context=22011)

[How to apply\\
\\
\\
We’ll assess if you can get Pharmaceutical Allowance. You don’t need to apply unless you want an advance payment.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-apply-for-pharmaceutical-allowance?context=22011)

[How to manage your allowance\\

Your options and obligations for Pharmaceutical Allowance.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-manage-your-pharmaceutical-allowance?context=22011)
