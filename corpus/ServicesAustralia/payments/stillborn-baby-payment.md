# Stillborn Baby Payment

A one off payment if you have a stillborn baby.

To get this you must:

- have recently had a stillborn baby
- be under the income test limits or eligible for Family Tax Benefit Part A
- not be getting Parental Leave Pay for the same baby.

Read the full conditions under [who can get it](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-stillborn-baby-payment?context=22221).

[Who can get it\\
\\
\\
You may be able to get Stillborn Baby Payment if you recently had a baby who was stillborn.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-stillborn-baby-payment?context=22221)

[How much you can get\\
\\
\\
The Stillborn Baby Payment is currently $4,326.57.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-much-stillborn-baby-payment-you-can-get?context=22221)

[How to claim\\
\\

Complete the following steps to claim Stillborn Baby Payment. Before you start, check if you can get it.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-claim-stillborn-baby-payment?context=22221)
