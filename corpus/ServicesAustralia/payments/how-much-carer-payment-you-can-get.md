# How much you can get

There are different rates of Carer Payment for single and partnered people.

## on this page

- [Tax on your payment](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-much-carer-payment-you-can-get?context=21816#a1)
- [Carer Supplement](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-much-carer-payment-you-can-get?context=21816#a2)

The Department of Social Services adjusts these rates every 20 March and 20 September.

| Pension rates per fortnight | Single | Couple each | Couple combined | Couple each separated due to ill health |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Maximum basic rate | $1,079.70 | $813.90 | $1,627.80 | $1,079.70 |
| Maximum [Pension Supplement](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/pension-supplement) | $84.90 | $64.00 | $128.00 | $84.90 |
| [Energy Supplement](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/energy-supplement) | $14.10 | $10.60 | $21.20 | $14.10 |
| TOTAL | $1,178.70 | $888.50 | $1,777.00 | $1,178.70 |

Read about how your [relationship changes](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/relationship-changes) can affect your payment rate.

## Tax on your payment

If you or the person you care for are Age Pension age or above, your Carer Payment is a taxable Centrelink payment. We don’t automatically deduct tax from your payment, but you can ask us to do this for you. This can help you avoid a debt at tax time.

Find out how to [deduct tax from your payment](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/deduct-tax-from-your-payment?context=21816).

## Carer Supplement

If you get Carer Payment for a period that includes 1 July, we’ll automatically pay you Carer Supplement each year. Find out more about [Carer Supplement](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/carer-supplement).
