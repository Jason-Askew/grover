# Who can get it

You must be a tertiary student living away from home to study to get Fares Allowance.

You must also get one of these payments:

-   [Youth Allowance](/youth-allowance)
-   [Austudy](/austudy)
-   [Pensioner Education Supplement](/pensioner-education-supplement).

You must also be one of these:

-   dependent and get a [higher rate of Youth Allowance when living away from home](/higher-rates-youth-allowance-when-living-away-from-home-students-and-australian-apprentices)
-   independent and living away from your partner to study
-   a parent living away from your dependent children to study
-   studying a [distance or online course](/distance-and-online-education) and need to travel to your education provider to meet a course requirement.

You can also get Fares Allowance if both of the following applied to you during the study year:

-   you were getting a [higher rate of Youth Allowance when living away from home](/higher-rates-youth-allowance-when-living-away-from-home-students-and-australian-apprentices) as a dependent student
-   you have now turned 22 or become [independent](/dependent-or-independent-for-youth-allowance-student-or-australian-apprentice?context=43916) by supporting yourself through work.
