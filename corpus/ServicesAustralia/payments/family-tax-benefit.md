# Family Tax Benefit

A 2 part payment that helps with the cost of raising children.

To get this you must:

- have a dependent child or full time secondary student aged 16 to 19 who isn’t getting a pension, payment or benefit like Youth Allowance
- care for the child for at least 35% of the time
- meet an income test.

Read the full conditions under [who can get it](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-family-tax-benefit?context=22151).
