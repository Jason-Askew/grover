# Who can get it

You’ll get this supplement if you’re in the Skills for Education and Employment program.

You must also get one of these:

-   [Disability Support Pension](/disability-support-pension)
-   [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment)
-   [Youth Allowance](/youth-allowance).

## Who can’t get it

You can’t get it if you’re getting any of these with the income support payments above:

-   [Pensioner Education Supplement](/pensioner-education-supplement)
-   ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement
-   another supplement, such as the Approved Program of Work Supplement.
