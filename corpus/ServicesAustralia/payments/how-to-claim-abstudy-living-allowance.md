How you claim depends on your circumstances.

There are 2 ways to claim ABSTUDY Living Allowance. You can claim as a [secondary or primary student](/claiming-abstudy-living-allowance-secondary-or-primary-student?context=45541) or as a [tertiary student or an Australian Apprentice](/claiming-abstudy-living-allowance-tertiary-student-or-australian-apprentice?context=45541).
