# Who can get it

You can get a Commonwealth Seniors Health Card if you meet certain conditions.

To get a card you must meet all these:

-   be [Age Pension age](/who-can-get-age-pension)
-   meet [residence rules](/residence-rules-for-commonwealth-seniors-health-card?context=21966)
-   not be getting an [income support payment](/income-support-payment) from Centrelink or the Department of Veterans’ Affairs
-   give us a Tax File Number or be exempt from doing so
-   meet [identity requirements](/how-to-prove-your-identity-with-centrelink?context=21966)
-   meet the [income test](/income-test-for-commonwealth-seniors-health-card?context=21966).

You must tell us your and your partner’s tax file numbers, unless you’re exempt.

If you can’t get this card, you could be eligible for other [concession or health care cards](/concession-and-health-care-cards).
