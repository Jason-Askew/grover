# How to claim medical expenses

Up until 20 June 2019 you can claim your out of pocket expenses for some medical items through the Australian Taxation Office (ATO).

These include:

-   disability aids
-   attendant care
-   aged care expenses.

You can only claim the offset from the ATO.

Read about how to claim [medical expenses tax offset](https://www.ato.gov.au/individuals-and-families/income-deductions-offsets-and-records/tax-offsets/medical-expenses-tax-offset) on the ATO website.
