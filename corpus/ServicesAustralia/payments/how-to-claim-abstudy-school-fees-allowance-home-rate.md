# How to claim

Complete the following steps to claim ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance at home rate for your child. Before you start, check if they can get it.

If you’ve already claimed ABSTUDY School Term Allowance for your child, you don’t need to claim this allowance separately. We’ll assess you for School Fees Allowance at the same time.

To claim School Fees Allowance at home rate for your child, you can either:

-   call the [ABSTUDY line](#toolbar-phone) and we’ll help you
-   fill in the [claim for ABSTUDY Schooling A](/sy018) form.

To get School Fees Allowance for the year, you must apply by 31 December of that year.

## Steps to claim ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance at home rate

1.  ### 1\. Get your documents ready to claim
    
    You’ll need to get some [supporting documents](/supporting-documents-for-your-centrelink-claim) ready to help answer some of the questions in the claim.
    
2.  ### 2\. Make your claim
    
    You can claim by calling the [ABSTUDY line](#toolbar-phone).
    
    Or, to submit your claim form, follow these steps:
    
    1.  Check you’ve answered all questions.
    2.  Sign and date the claim form.
    3.  Attach any supporting documents.
    4.  Submit your claim to us.
    
    You can submit your claim form in one of the following ways:
    
    -   online by [uploading your documents online](/upload-your-centrelink-documents-online)
    -   by post to:
        
        Services Australia  
        Student Services  
        Reply Paid 7804  
        Canberra BC ACT 2610
        
    -   in person at a [service centre](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/).
    
3.  ### 3\. Wait for the result of your claim
    
    If we need more details we’ll contact you.
    
    We'll let you know the result of your application. If your claim is accepted, we’ll let you know:
    
    -   when you’ll get your first payment
    -   how much you’ll get.
    
    You can ask us to [review our decision](/explanations-and-formal-reviews) if you think we’ve made a mistake.
