# How to claim

You can claim ABSTUDY Fares Allowance before or after your trip.

If you claim before, we’ll book the travel for you. Otherwise, claim after you travel and we’ll pay you back.

[

Claiming before your trip

How you claim before your trip depends on what type of student you are.


](/claiming-abstudy-fares-allowance-before-your-trip?context=22701)

[

Claiming after your trip

Claiming ABSTUDY Fares Allowance after your trip is the same for all students.


](/claiming-abstudy-fares-allowance-after-your-trip?context=22701)

[

Providing proof to support your claim

You may need to give us documents to explain the reason you need to travel.


](/proof-to-support-your-abstudy-fares-allowance-claim?context=22701)
