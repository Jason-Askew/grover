# Who can get it

You can get Mobility Allowance if you meet certain criteria.

You may be eligible if you meet all the following conditions. You:

-   are 16 years or older
-   meet [residence rules](/residence-rules-for-mobility-allowance?context=22501)
-   need help using public transport for at least 12 months because of disability
-   have a medical report from your doctor stating you can’t use public transport without a lot of help
-   need to travel from home for paid work, voluntary work, training, or to look for work.

You can’t get the allowance if you have either:

-   funded support from the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS)
-   a car from the Department of Veterans’ Affairs under their [Vehicle Assistance Scheme](https://www.dva.gov.au/what-we-help-with/vehicle-costs-and-benefits/vehicle-assistance-scheme) or [Motor Vehicle Compensation Scheme](https://www.dva.gov.au/what-we-help-with/vehicle-costs-and-benefits/motor-vehicle-compensation-scheme).
