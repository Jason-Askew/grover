# Crisis Payment for National Health Emergency

There are no current health emergencies that you can claim this payment for in Australia.

The Crisis Payment for National Health Emergency (NHE) is a one-off payment for people affected by a major health crisis in Australia.

How much you can get, and when you can get it, depends on the type of health emergency. Eligibility rules may also vary.

## Past payments

The Crisis Payment for National Health Emergency (COVID-19) ended on 1 October 2022.

If you need another payment or service, read more about [getting a Centrelink payment](/get-centrelink-payment).
