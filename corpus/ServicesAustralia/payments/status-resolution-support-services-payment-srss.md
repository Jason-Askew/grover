# Status Resolution Support Services payment (SRSS)

A regular payment to help with basic living costs while you’re waiting to hear about your immigration status.

[Who can get it\\
\\
\\
You may be able to get this payment if you’re unable to support yourself while resolving your immigration status.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-status-resolution-support-services-payment?context=22626)

[When we’ll pay you\\
\\
\\
We’ll pay you the SRSS payment once the Department of Home Affairs tell us you’re eligible.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/when-well-pay-status-resolution-support-services-payment?context=22626)

[How to apply\\
\\
\\
Contact a SRSS service provider in your state or territory. They’ll help you apply for the SRSS payment.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-apply-for-status-resolution-support-services-payment?context=22626)

[How to manage your payment\\
\\
\\
Your options and obligations for the Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) payment.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-manage-your-status-resolution-support-services-payment?context=22626)
