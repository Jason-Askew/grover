# Who can get it

We pay ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance at home rate to parents and guardians of students who meet certain requirements.

## on this page

-   [Requirements for students](#a1)
-   [Requirements for parents and guardians](#a2)

## Requirements for students

To get School Fees Allowance at home rate, students must be all of the following:

-   Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander Australian
-   aged 15 or younger
-   living at home or not approved to live away from home.

The must also be either:

-   a full-time student in high school, including those approved for home schooling or [distance education](/distance-and-online-education?context=38071)
-   a primary school student aged 14 or older on 1 January in the year of study.

### Students in state care

If a student you’re caring for is in state care they can still get the allowance. They must meet all the requirements listed above.

### Students with special circumstances

You can get the allowance for a student you’re caring for if we consider them independent in any of these situations:

-   they’re an [orphan](/independent-for-abstudy-if-youre-orphan?context=38071)
-   their [parents can’t take care of them](/independent-for-abstudy-if-your-parents-cant-take-care-you?context=38071)
-   it’s [unreasonable for them to live at home](/independent-for-abstudy-if-its-unreasonable-for-you-to-live-home?context=38071).

## Requirements for parents and guardians

To get School Term Allowance at home rate, you or your partner must be one of the following:

-   getting an [income support payment](/income-support-payment), including periods of nil rate
-   getting [Farm Household Allowance](/farm-household-allowance)
-   a student getting [ABSTUDY Living Allowance](/abstudy-living-allowance)
-   a current [Health Care Card](/health-care-card) or [Low Income Health Care Card](/low-income-health-care-card) holder.
