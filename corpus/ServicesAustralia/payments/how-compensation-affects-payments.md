# How compensation affects payments

Compensation can affect your Centrelink payments. Some similar types of payments can affect you too.

## on this page

- [Compensation payments](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-compensation-affects-payments?context=22516#a1)
- [Payments under schemes and benefits](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-compensation-affects-payments?context=22516#a2)

There are different types of compensation or payments made under schemes or benefits that can affect payments you get from us.

## Compensation payments

Compensation payments can affect payments you get from us. These include all the following:

- workers compensation
- motor vehicle accident compensation
- public liability compensation
- medical negligence compensation
- personal injury or nervous shock compensation.

You need to tell us if you or your partner make a claim for any of these types of compensation.

## Payments under schemes and benefits

The following payments are not considered compensation but may affect your payments from us under the income and assets test:

- victims of crime schemes, including criminal injury
- death benefits
- payments made under an income replacement policy attached to a superannuation policy
- sickness and accident policy for sporting injuries
- total permanent disability from your superannuation scheme
- compensation for unlawful dismissal, sexual harassment or racial discrimination
- disability pension from the Department of Veterans’ Affairs.

You can [call us](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/phone-us?context=64107) on your main payment line or ask a [Financial Information Service](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/financial-information-service) Officer for more information about this.
