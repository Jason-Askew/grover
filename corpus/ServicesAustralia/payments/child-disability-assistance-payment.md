# Child Disability Assistance Payment

An annual payment if you get Carer Allowance when looking after a child with disability or medical condition.

The Child Disability Assistance Payment is an automatic annual payment of up to $1,000.

You don’t need to claim this payment. You just need to be getting Carer Allowance for a child younger than 16 for a period that includes 1 July.

[Who can get it\\
\\
\\
To get this payment, you need to be getting Carer Allowance for a child younger than 16 for a period that includes 1 July.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-child-disability-assistance-payment?context=21871)

[How much you can get\\
\\
\\
You can get up to $1,000 each year for each child who qualifies you for Carer Allowance.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-much-child-disability-assistance-payment-you-can-get?context=21871)

[How to get it\\
\\
\\
You don’t need to claim for Child Disability Assistance Payment. If you’re eligible, we’ll pay it straight into your bank account.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-get-child-disability-assistance-payment?context=21871)
