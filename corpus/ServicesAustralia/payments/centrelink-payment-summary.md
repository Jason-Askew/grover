# Centrelink payment summary

Your payment summary shows most taxable and non-taxable payments you got from us within a tax year that the Australian Taxation Office need to know about.

Family Tax Benefit is a non-taxable payment. If you only got payments of Family Tax Benefit from us, you won’t get a payment summary.

[What’s on it\\
\\
\\
Your payment summary shows most of your taxable and non-taxable payments the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) need to know about.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/whats-your-centrelink-payment-summary?context=22781)

[How to get it\\
\\
\\
You can use a self service option to get your payment summary.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-get-your-centrelink-payment-summary?context=22781)

[What happens if your payment summary changes\\
\\
\\
If there’s a change to your payment summary details, we’ll send you a letter to let you know. The letter will tell you what payment summary detail changed and the tax year it’s for.](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/what-happens-if-your-centrelink-payment-summary-changes?context=22781)
