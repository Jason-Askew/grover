# Who can get it

You need to meet eligibility requirements to get ABSTUDY Fares Allowance.

You can get the allowance if you’re getting ABSTUDY or ABSTUDY [Pensioner Education Supplement](/pensioner-education-supplement) and meet 1 of the following:

-   secondary student
-   tertiary student
-   master’s or doctorate student who doesn’t get Relocation Allowance.

You must also be studying full time and either:

-   dependent and getting the [higher rate of ABSTUDY when living away from home](/higher-rates-abstudy-living-allowance-when-living-away-from-home)
-   independent and living away from home to study because of [travel time or access](/your-travel-time-or-access-criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy).

Sometimes, someone other than you can get the allowance to travel.

## Who can’t get it

You can’t get the allowance if you’re either:

-   an Australian Apprentice
-   getting ABSTUDY as a student, but don’t meet the criteria above.
