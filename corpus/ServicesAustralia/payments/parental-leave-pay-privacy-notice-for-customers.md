# Parental Leave Pay privacy notice for customers

This privacy notice outlines how we manage personal information in relation to Parental Leave Pay and the Paid Parental Leave Superannuation Contribution (PPLSC).

## What we collect

We collect information about:

-   you, your partner and your children
-   your employer.

This includes:

-   your name, date of birth, tax file number (TFN) and other identity details
-   contact details such as your address
-   your eligibility for Parental Leave Pay and the PPLSC.

## Why we collect this information

We collect your personal information to assess your eligibility for Parental Leave Pay and the PPLSC.

## How we’re authorised to collect your information

We collect, use and disclose your personal information in line with the:

-   _Paid Parental Leave Act 2010_
-   _Privacy Act 1988_.

## Who we may share your information with

If you receive Parental Leave Pay, we will provide the following information to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO):

-   your name, your date of birth and TFN
-   your address
-   your Centrelink Reference Number (CRN)
-   your first Parental Leave Pay payment date for the financial year
-   the gross amount of Parental Leave Pay you received in the financial year.

This is so the ATO can locate your record to calculate and pay your PPLSC.

If you can’t resolve an issue with the person you’ve been talking to, ask to speak to their supervisor.

Our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy?context=22) outlines how to complain about a breach of the Australian Privacy Principles in the _Privacy Act 1988_. It also outlines how we deal with such a complaint.
