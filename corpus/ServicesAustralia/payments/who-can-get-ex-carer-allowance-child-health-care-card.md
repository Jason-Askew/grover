# Who can get it

You can get an Ex-Carer Allowance (Child) Health Care Card if you meet certain criteria.

You must meet all of these. You:

-   are between 16 and 25 years of age
-   are a full time student
-   had a Carer Allowance Health Care Card on the day before you turned 16
-   meet residence rules.

This card is not means tested.
