# Who can get it

ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance higher rate is for students who need to live away from home or are doing an approved special course.

## on this page

-   [Dependent and living away from home](#awayfromhome)
-   [Dependent and living at home](#livingathome)
-   [Independent](#independent)
-   [In state care](#instatecare)

To get School Fees Allowance higher rate, students must be Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander Australian and either:

-   a full-time high school student, or
-   a full-time student who needs to live away from home to repeat the final year of primary school before high school.

They can be one of the following:

-   dependent and living away from home
-   dependent and living at home
-   independent
-   in state care.

Students may also get a higher rate if they live away from home to do an [approved special course](/approved-special-courses-for-abstudy-away-from-home-rate?context=38071).

## Dependent and living away from home

If a student is [dependent](/dependent-or-independent-for-abstudy?context=38071) we must approve them for one of the [higher rates when living away from home](/higher-rates-abstudy-living-allowance-when-living-away-from-home).

## Dependent and living at home

We’ll pay School Fees Allowance higher rate if a student is [dependent](/dependent-or-independent-for-abstudy?context=38071) and needs to attend a private school. They must not be able to attend a government school because of either:

-   [limited local school facilities](/criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy-if-your-local-government-school-has-limited-program-or)
-   [travel time and access](/your-travel-time-or-access-criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy?context=38071).

## Independent

We’ll pay School Fees Allowance higher rate if you’re independent because of one of the following reasons:

-   you’re an [orphan](/independent-for-abstudy-if-youre-orphan?context=38071)
-   your [parents can’t take care of you](/independent-for-abstudy-if-your-parents-cant-take-care-you?context=38071)
-   it’s [unreasonable to live at home](/independent-for-abstudy-if-its-unreasonable-for-you-to-live-home?context=38071).

If you're independent for any other reason, you must also be getting the [ABSTUDY Living Allowance higher rate](/higher-rates-abstudy-living-allowance-when-living-away-from-home).

## In state care

If a student you're caring for is in state care, you can get the School Fees Allowance higher rate if both of the following apply:

-   you don’t get a regular foster care allowance for them
-   we pay the [ABSTUDY Living Allowance higher rate](/higher-rates-abstudy-living-allowance-when-living-away-from-home) for them.
