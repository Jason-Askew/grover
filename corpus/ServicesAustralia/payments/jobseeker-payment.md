# JobSeeker Payment

Financial help if you’re between 22 and Age Pension age and looking for work. It’s also for when you’re sick or injured and can’t do your usual work or study for a short time.

For Centrelink payments and services, you can call our [multilingual phone service](/phone-us#languages).

Call the [Centrelink Indigenous Call Centre](/centrelink-indigenous-call-centre?context=64107) to speak to us about Centrelink payments and services for free.

To get this payment you need to meet some rules. These include:

-   you’re between 22 and Age Pension age
-   you meet the income and assets tests
-   you meet residence rules.

JobSeeker Payment may affect the [child support](/managing-child-support) payments you make or receive. You need to tell us [if your income changes](/change-circumstances-for-child-support?context=21911).
