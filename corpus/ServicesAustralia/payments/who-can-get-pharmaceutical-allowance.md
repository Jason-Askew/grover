# Who can get it

You can get the Pharmaceutical Allowance if you meet certain conditions.

You may get it if you get any of the following payments:

-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment) single if you're younger than Age Pension age
-   [Disability Support Pension](/disability-support-pension) if you're younger than 21 with no dependent children.

You may also be eligible if you get certain payments and you meet certain conditions.
