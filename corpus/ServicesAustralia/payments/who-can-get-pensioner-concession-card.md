# Who can get a card

We'll automatically send you a Pensioner Concession Card if you get certain payments from us.

## on this page

-   [Eligible payments](#a1)
-   [If you have a Social Security Agreement with New Zealand](#a2)

You don’t need to apply for a Pensioner Concession Card.

## Eligible payments

We’ll automatically send you a Pensioner Concession Card if you get one of the following payments:

-   [Age Pension](/age-pension)
-   [Carer Payment](/carer-payment)
-   [Disability Support Pension](/disability-support-pension)
-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment) single.

Your Parenting Payment single ends when your youngest child turns 14. You can keep your card for 12 weeks after your Parenting Payment ends.

### If you're 55 or older

We’ll also automatically send you a Pensioner Concession Card if you’re 55 or older and get one of the following payments for more than 9 months:

-   [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment) partnered
-   [Special Benefit](/special-benefit) and you’re an Australian resident.

### If you have a partial capacity to work

We’ll automatically send you a Pensioner Concession Card if you have a partial capacity to work and you're getting any of the following payments:

-   [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment) partnered
-   [Youth Allowance](/youth-allowance) as a job seeker.

If you find a job, you may be able to keep your card for a short time.

### If you’re a single principal carer

We’ll automatically send you a Pensioner Concession Card if you’re a single principal carer and getting one of the following payments:

-   [Jobseeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Youth Allowance](/youth-allowance) as a job seeker.

## If you have a Social Security Agreement with New Zealand

You can get a Pensioner Concession Card if you get an eligible payment under a Social Security Agreement with New Zealand.

If you don’t automatically get a Pensioner Concession Card, you should call us on your regular [Centrelink payment line](/phone-us).
