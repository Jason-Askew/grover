# Carer Adjustment Payment

A one-off payment to support families in financial hardship after a catastrophic event affecting a child younger than 7.

This payment is for you if you give full time care to a child younger than 7 who has been affected by a catastrophic event. Catastrophic events include things like:

- childhood stroke
- diagnosis of a severe medical condition, such as childhood cancer
- car accident or other serious accidents.

To get this payment you will need to provide suitable evidence, such as a doctor’s report.

The event must have caused a significant change to your caring circumstances and resulted in financial hardship.

Read more about [who can get Carer Adjustment Payment](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/who-can-get-carer-adjustment-payment?context=21866).
