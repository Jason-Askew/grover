# Centrelink online account help - Update your banking details

Instructions on how to update your banking details, including cash, cheques, loans, savings, deeming accounts and term deposit accounts.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: view banking details](#a2)
-   [Step 3: edit banking details](#step3)
-   [Step 4: delete banking details](#step4)
-   [Step 5: add new banking details](#step5)
-   [Step 6: review and submit](#step6)
-   [Step 7: sign out](#a7)

You need to tell us when your circumstances change. Then we can assess your eligibility for payments and services using the correct details. Read more about [changes to your circumstances](/change-circumstances-if-you-get-centrelink-payment) and how it may affect your payment.

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

From your homepage, select **MENU**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step1a.png)

Select **Income and assets**, then **Income and assets details** and **Manage income and assets**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step1b.png)

Read **What you need to know before you start**. Then select **Continue** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-11/coa-update-banking-details-step1c-111121.png)

## Step 2: view banking details

On the Income and assets summary page, select **View/Edit details in Banking**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-banking-step2a-251022.png)

Read **Banking explained**, then select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-07/update-og-update-banking-details-step2b-150722.png)

The banking details you’ve already told us about will show. They could be details about your savings, loans, deeming accounts, or term deposit accounts. To protect your personal information, we’ll only show the last 4 digits of your account number. If you don’t recognise these details, [contact us](/phone-us).

Select any of the following:

-   **Home** if you don’t need to make any changes
-   **Edit** to update your banking details and go to [Step 3](#step3) for instructions
-   **Delete** to remove your banking details and go to [Step 4](#step4) for instructions
-   **Add** to add new banking details and go to [Step 5](#step5) for instructions.

Some of your banking details may not have an edit or delete option. You’ll need to [contact us](/contact-us) to make changes to these items.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step2c.png)

## Step 3: edit banking details

Select **Begin** to edit your banking details. In this example, we’ll edit savings account details.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step2a.png)

If you need help, read the information with the **question mark** icon on each page.

Enter your new savings account balance. The amount you enter needs to be a whole number. You need to round it down to the nearest whole number. For example, if the new balance amount is $100.91, you need to round it down to $100.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step2b.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date of the change. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step2c.png)

Select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step2d.png)

The details you changed will show as **Edited** on the Banking summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

If you’ve entered the wrong details, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To delete banking details, go to [Step 4](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-banking-details#step4).

To add new banking details, go to [Step 5](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-banking-details#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step3e.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions on how to review and submit your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step2f.png)

## Step 4: delete banking details

If you need to remove any of your banking details, select **Delete**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step4a.png)

Select either:

-   **OK** to continue
-   **Cancel** if you no longer want to delete your banking details.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step3b.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step3c.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date you deleted your account. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step3d.png)

Select **Delete** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step3e.png)

The details you deleted will show as **Removed** on the Banking summary page.

If you’ve deleted the wrong details, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To add new banking details, go to [Step 5](#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step4f.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions on reviewing and submitting your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step3g.png)

## Step 5: add new banking details

To add new banking details, select **Add**. In this example, we’ll add a new savings account.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step5a.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4b.png)

Select the type of account or investment from the **dropdown** menu. In this example, we’ll select **Savings account**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4c.png)

Select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4d.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if the account is outside Australia. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4e.png)

Enter the Bank State Branch (BSB) number, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4f.png)

Enter the account number, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4g.png)

Enter your savings account balance. The amount you enter needs to be a whole number. You need to round it down to the nearest whole number. For example, if the new balance amount is $100.91, you need to round it down to $100.

Select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4h.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the purchase, start or acquisition date. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4i.png)

Enter your **share of ownership** in whole numbers.

If you have a partner, enter their share of ownership in whole dollars.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-banking-step5j-251022.png)

Select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4l.png)

The details you added will show as **New** on the Banking summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

On this page, you can select any of the following:

-   **Edit** if you need to make changes
-   **Delete** to remove your update
-   **Add** to add more banking details.

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step5l.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step4n.png)

## Step 6: review and submit

The Income and assets summary page shows updates that are ready to review and submit.

We’ll cancel your changes if you don’t submit them by the date we tell you.

Select either:

-   **Cancel updates** if you no longer want to update your details or need to start again
-   **View/Edit details** in another category to view or make changes.

To submit your changes, select **Review and submit updates**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step6a.png)

Select **Begin** to check your updates are correct.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step5b.png)

We’ll give you a summary of the details you’ve given us.

Check each section to make sure your details are correct. If you need to make changes, select **Edit Banking**.

If all the details are correct, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step6c.png)

Read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I declare and understand that:**.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step5d.png)

Select **Confirm information** to save the information you’ve given us and continue. You won’t be able to make any changes to your answers after this.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-banking-step5e.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a receipt when you submit your update. Make a note of the **Receipt Number** for your records.

You won’t be able to make changes to your income and asset details until we finalise your updates. We may contact you to do this.

On this page, you can select any of the following:

-   **Information you provided** to view a summary of the details you’ve given us
-   **Return home** to go back to your homepage
-   **Save receipt** to keep a copy of your receipt.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step6f.png)

If you need to change other types of [income and assets](/centrelink-online-account-help-manage-your-income-and-assets-details), we’ll ask you to update these next.

-   [update your managed investments and superannuation](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-managed-investments-and-superannuation-details)
-   [update your real estate information](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-real-estate-details)
-   [update your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-home-contents-personal-effects-vehicles-and-other-assets)
-   [update your other income details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-income-and-assets-details-your-other-income-details).

## Step 7: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/coa-update-your-banking-details-step7a.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.

View other [online account and Express Plus mobile app guides](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps) and video demonstrations about using your online account.
