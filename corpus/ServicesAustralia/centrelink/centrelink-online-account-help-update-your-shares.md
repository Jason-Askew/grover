# Centrelink online account help - Update your shares

Instructions on how to update your Australian listed shares using your Centrelink online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#step1)
-   [Step 2: view shares details](#step2)
-   [Step 3: edit shares details](#step3)
-   [Step 4: delete shares details](#step4)
-   [Step 5: add new shares details](#step5)
-   [Step 6: review and submit](#step6)
-   [Step 7: sign out](#step7)

You need to tell us when your circumstances change. Then we can assess your eligibility for payments and services using the correct details. This includes changes to Australian listed shares for you and your partner.

Read about [deeming](/deeming) rules for shares and other financial investments that may affect your payment.

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

Select **MENU** from your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/update-your-shares-step1a-040425.png)

Select **Income and assets**, then **Income and assets details** and **Manage income and assets**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/update-your-shares-step1b-040425.png)

Read **What you need to know before you start**. Then select **Continue** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-02/coa-update-shares-step1c-020221.png)

## Step 2: view shares details

On the Income and assets summary page, select **View/Edit details** in **Shares**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step2a-241022.png)

Read **Shares explained**, then select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step2b-241022.png)

The shares details you’ve already told us about will show.

Select any of the following:

-   **Home** if you don’t need to make any changes
-   **Edit** to update your shares details and go to [Step 3](#step3) for instructions
-   **Delete** to remove your shares details and go to [Step 4](#step4) for instructions
-   **Add** to add new shares details and go to [Step 5](#step5) for instructions.

Some of your shares details may not have an edit or delete option. You’ll need to [contact us](/contact-us) to make changes to these items.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step2c-241022.png)

## Step 3: edit shares details

If you need to update any of your shares, select **Edit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step3a-241022.png)

Select **Begin** to edit your shares details.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step2b.png)

Enter the new **Total number of shares**.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step3c-241022.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the purchase, start or acquisition date. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-02/coa-update-shares-step3d-020221.png)

Select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step2e.png)

The details you changed will show as **Edited** on the Shares summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

If you’ve entered the wrong details, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To delete home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details, go to [Step 4](#step4).

To add new home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details, go to [Step 5](#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step3f-241022.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions on how to review and submit your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step2g.png)

## Step 4: delete shares details

If you need to remove any of your shares details, select **Delete**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step4a-241022.png)

Select either:

-   **OK** to continue
-   **Cancel** if you no longer want to delete your shares details.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step3b.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step3c.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date you deleted your shares. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step3d.png)

Select **Delete** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step3e.png)

The details you deleted will show as **Removed** on the Shares summary page.

If you’ve deleted the wrong details, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To add new shares details, go to [Step 5](#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step4f-241022.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions on how to review and submit your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step3g.png)

## Step 5: add new shares details

To add new shares details, select **Add**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step5a-241022.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step4b.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if your shares are listed on any Australian stock exchange.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step4c.png)

If your shares are listed on any Australian stock exchange, enter the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX) code or security code. Then select **Next**.

If you need help, read the information with the **question mark** icon on each page.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step4d.png)

Enter the **Total number of shares**, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step4e.png)

Enter your **share of ownership** in whole numbers.

If you have a partner, enter their share of ownership in whole dollars.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step5f-241022.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the purchase, start or acquisition date. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step4h.png)

Select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step4i.png)

The details you added will show as **New** on the Shares summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

On this page, you can select any of the following:

-   **Edit** if you need to make changes
-   **Delete** to remove your update
-   **Add** to add more shares details.

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step5i-241022.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step4k.png)

## Step 6: review and submit

The Income and assets summary page shows updates that are ready to review and submit.

We’ll cancel your changes if you don’t submit them by the date we tell you.

Select either:

-   **Cancel updates** if you no longer want to update your details or need to start again
-   **View/Edit details** in another category to view or make changes.

To submit your changes, select **Review and submit updates**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step6a-241022.png)

Select **Begin** to check your updates are correct.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step5b.png)

We’ll give you a summary of the details you’ve given us.

Review each section to check your details are correct. If you need to make changes, select **Edit Shares**.

If all the details are correct, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step6c-241022.png)

Read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I declare and understand that:**.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step6d-241022.png)

Select **Confirm information** to save the information you’ve given us and continue. You won’t be able to make any changes to your answers after this.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-02/coa-update-shares-step6e-020221.png)

Before you submit your update, you may need to complete some extra tasks. These will be listed in **Next steps**.

Select **Begin** to complete the extra tasks for your update.

If you don’t need to complete extra tasks, go to [Get your receipt](#getyourreceipt).

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step5f.png)

These tasks will show as **Required**.

When a task is **Required** you must complete it before you can submit your update.

Select **Upload** for the task you need to complete. You’ll need to do this for each **Required** task.

Read our online guides on how to do any of the following

-   [upload documents using your Centrelink online account](/upload-your-centrelink-documents-online)
-   [upload documents using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app-help-upload-documents).

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step6g-241022.png)

When you’ve completed the task, the status changes from Required to **Done**.

If you’ve completed a task and you need to change it, select **Edit**.

When you’ve completed all Required tasks, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step6h-241022.png)

If you’re ready to submit your update, select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-09/coa-update-shares-step5i.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a receipt when you submit your update. Make a note of the **Receipt Number** for your records.

You won’t be able make changes to any of your income and assets details until we finalise your updates. We may contact you to do this.

On this page, you can select any of the following:

-   **Information you provided** to view a summary of the details you’ve given us
-   **Return home** to go back to your homepage
-   **Save receipt** to keep a copy of your receipt.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-shares-step6j-241022.png)

Read our online guides on how to do any of the following:

-   [update your banking details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-banking-details)
-   [update your managed investments and superannuation](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-managed-investments-and-superannuation-details)
-   [update your real estate information](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-real-estate-details)
-   [update your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-home-contents-personal-effects-vehicles-and-other-assets)
-   [update your other income details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-income-and-assets-details-your-other-income-details).

## Step 7: sign out

From your homepage you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/update-your-shares-step7-040425.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.
