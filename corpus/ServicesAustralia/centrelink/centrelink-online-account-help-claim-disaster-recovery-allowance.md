# Centrelink online account help - Claim the Disaster Recovery Allowance

Instructions on how to claim the Disaster Recovery Allowance or New Zealand Disaster Recovery Allowance for an active disaster event using your Centrelink online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#step1)
-   [Step 2: check eligibility](#step2)
-   [Step 3: start your claim and answer questions](#step3)
-   [Step 4: review and confirm your claim](#step4)
-   [Step 5: submit claim](#step5)
-   [Step 6: sign out](#step6)

This guide is for Australian residents, [New Zealand citizens living in Australia](/new-zealand-citizens-claiming-payments-australia) or [those who meet other residence rules](/residence-rules-for-natural-disaster-support) who need to claim the [Disaster Recovery Allowance](/natural-disaster-support) or New Zealand Disaster Recovery Allowance for a natural disaster event.

Information about these and other payments is detailed on the [disaster event](/natural-disaster-support) pages.

To claim online, you need a myGov account with Centrelink linked to it.

If you’re a nominee you won’t be able to claim this payment online for customers you’re a nominee for.

If you can’t use your Centrelink online account, you can call us on the [Emergency information line](#toolbar-phone) for help claiming this payment.

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

From your homepage, select **Make a claim or view claim status**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step1a.png)

On the My online claims page, select **Make a claim** to begin a new claim.

If you’re not making a new claim, you can select any of the following:

-   **Continue claim** to continue a claim you’ve started, which could be for a different disaster or payment
-   **Cancel claim** to cancel a claim and start again
-   **View claim** to see the details of a claim you’ve submitted
-   **Withdraw claim** to withdraw a claim you’ve submitted.

If you withdraw your claim twice, you won’t be able to submit another claim online. If you reach the limit, you’ll need to call us on the [Emergency information line](#toolbar-phone) for help with submitting your claim.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-03/coa-claim-dra-step1b.png)

Select **Get started** from the **Help in an emergency** category.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-10/og-coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step1c.png)

Read the information about Disaster Recovery Allowance. This claim is for those who’ve lost income as a direct result of the disaster. Then select **Apply for Disaster Recovery Allowance**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-07/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step1d-080722.png)

## Step 2: check eligibility

We’ll ask you questions to check if you’re eligible for this payment. Answer all the questions about your current situation by entering the information we ask for. To check your eligibility, select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-03/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step2a.png)

Read the Privacy Notice about how Services Australia uses your personal information.

Select **Yes** to agree with the privacy agreement, then **Next** to continue.

If you select **No**, you won’t be able to continue your claim.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-03/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step2b-160322.png)

Read the acknowledgement about providing accurate information, then select **Next**.

Read the information with the **question mark** icon on each page to help you answer the questions correctly.

Select **Launch the digital assistant** to use our digital assistant for more help.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-03/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step2c-160322.png)

Read the information about family and domestic violence, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-03/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step2d.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if you’re an Australian resident, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-03/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step2e.png)

Select the disaster event you want to claim for. Then select **Next**.

In this example, we’ll select **Flood Event**. Images in this guide about a particular event are examples only.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-03/coa-claim-dra-step2f.png)

Select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-03/update-og-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step2g-080322.png)

We’ll ask more questions to check if you’re eligible.

Select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-03/coa-claim-dra-step2h.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if you’re getting a Department of Veterans’ Affairs income support payment. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-03/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step2i.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if you’re getting Self-Employment Allowance. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-12/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step2j-011222.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if you hold a New Zealand passport with a non-protected Special Category visa. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-03/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step2k.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if your usual place of residence is in an affected area. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-03/coa-claim-dra-step2l.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if your usual place of work is in an affected area. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-03/coa-claim-dra-step2m.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if you lost income or your income reduced because of the disaster. Then select **Next**.

Based on your answers, we’ll let you know if the Disaster Recovery Allowance is suitable in your circumstances.

There are different instructions to follow if you’re:

-   [Not suitable](#noteligible)
-   [Suitable](#eligible).

### Not suitable

We’ll confirm a Disaster Recovery Allowance is not suitable for your current circumstances. We’ll also suggest next steps.

Select **Return home** and go to [Step 6](#step6) to sign out.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-03/coa-claim-dra-step2p.png)

### Suitable

We’ll let you know to submit your claim as soon as possible so we can pay you from the earliest date.

Select **Start claim**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-03/coa-claim-dra-step2q.png)

## Step 4: review and confirm your claim

Select **Begin** to review the details you gave us.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-10/update-og-claim-dra-step4a.png)

Select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-10/update-og-claim-dra-step4b.png)

We’ll give you a summary of your claim details. Review each section to check your details are correct. If you need to make changes, select the relevant Update button. For example, select **Update Contact requirements** to change those details.

If all the details are correct, select **Next** to read your obligations and terms of the claim.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-10/update-og-claim-dra-step4c.png)

Read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the terms in the Declaration, **I understand and agree to the above terms**. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-10/update-og-claim-dra-step4d.png)

Read the Acknowledgement. If you understand and agree with the terms in the Acknowledgement, select **I understand and agree to the above terms**. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-10/update-og-claim-dra-step4e.png)

Select **Confirm Information** to save all the information you gave us and go back to your claim page. You won’t be able to make any changes to your answers after this point.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-10/update-og-claim-dra-step4f.png)

## Step 5: submit claim

Select **Start** when you’re ready to do the next steps and submit your claim.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step5a.png)

Select **Begin** to complete extra tasks for your claim.

**Note**: If you’re claiming New Zealand Disaster Recovery Allowance, we’ll ask you for evidence to show you’ve participated in the Australian income tax system.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-10/update-og-claim-dra-step5b.png)

Select **Upload** for the task you need to complete. You’ll need to do this for each Supplementary task you choose to complete.

If the task is Supplementary, you don’t need to give us these documents with your claim. But, if you do, it’ll help us to assess your claim quicker. We may contact you if we need more information to help us assess your claim.

Read more about how to [upload documents with your Centrelink online account](/upload-your-centrelink-documents-online) or the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app-help-upload-documents).

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step5c.png)

When you’ve completed the task, the status will change from Supplementary to **Done**.

If you’ve completed a task and you need to change it, select **Edit**.

When you’ve completed all tasks, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step5d.png)

If you’ve completed all tasks and you’re ready to submit your claim, select **Submit**.

If you need to make changes after submitting your claim, you’ll need to withdraw that claim and start a new one. If you withdraw your claim twice, you won’t be able to submit another claim online. If you reach the limit, you’ll need to call us on the [Emergency information line](#toolbar-phone) for help with submitting your claim.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-10/update-og-claim-dra-step5e.png)

We’ll let you know if you’ve successfully submitted your claim.

You can then select any of the following:

-   **Home** to go back to your homepage
-   **View claim history** to check the history of your claim
-   **View claim answers** to check the information you gave us.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-03/coa-claim-dra-step5f.png)

You can track the progress of your claim from your homepage. To check the status of your claim, select **Make a claim or view claim status**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/coa-claim-disaster-recovery-allowance-step5g.png)
