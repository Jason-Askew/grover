# Centrelink online account help - Update income and assets details - your other income details

Instructions on how to update your other income details using your Centrelink online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#step1)
-   [Step 2: view other income details](#step2)
-   [Step 3: edit other income details](#step3)
-   [Step 4: delete other income details](#step4)
-   [Step 5: add other income details](#step5)
-   [Step 6: review and submit](#step6)
-   [Step 7: sign out](#a7)

You need to tell us when your circumstances change. We can then assess your eligibility for payments and services using the correct details. This includes changes to your and your partner’s income. You can make changes to your partner’s other income if they permit you to access their information.

Read more about [income](/income) and how it may affect your payment.

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

Select **MENU** from your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-09/update-og-coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step1a-200922.png)

Select **Income and assets**, then **Income and assets details** and **Manage income and assets**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-09/update-og-coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step1b-200922.png)

Read **What you need to know before you start**. Then select **Continue** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step1c-050121.png)

## Step 2: view other income details

On the Income and assets summary page, select **View/Edit details** in **Other income**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-other-income-step1a.png)

Read **Other income explained**, then select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-09/update-og-coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step2b-200922.png)

The other income details you’ve already told us about will show.

Select any of the following:

-   **Home** if you don’t need to make any changes
-   **Edit** to update your other income details and go to [Step 2](#step2) for instructions
-   **Delete** to remove your other income details and go to [Step 3](#step3) for instructions
-   **Add** to add new other income details and go to [Step 4](#step4) for instructions.

Some of your other income types may not have an edit or delete option. You’ll need to [contact us](/contact-us) on your regular Centrelink payment line to update these items.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step2c-300822.png)

## Step 3: edit other income details

If you need to update any of your other income, select **Edit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step3a-300822.png)

Select **Begin** to edit your other income details.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-09/update-og-coa-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step3b-200922.png)

Enter how much the payment was and select its frequency from the **dropdown** menu. The amount you enter needs to be a whole number.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step3c-190121.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date of the change. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step3d-050121.png)

Select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step3e-050121.png)

The details you changed will show as **Edited** on the Other income summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

If you’ve entered the wrong details, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To delete other income details, go to [Step 4](#step4).

To add new other income details, go to [Step 5](#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step3f-300822.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions on how to review and submit your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step3g-050121.png)

## Step 4: delete other income details

If you need to remove any of your other income details, select **Delete**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step4a-300822.png)

Select either:

-   **OK** to continue
-   **Cancel** if you no longer want to delete your other income details.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step4b-050121.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step4c-050121.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date your other income stopped. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step4d-120121.png)

Select **Delete** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-12/coa-update-other-income-step4e.png)

The details you deleted will show as **Removed** on the Other income summary page.

If you’ve deleted the wrong details, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To add new other income details, go to [Step 5](#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step4f-300822.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions on how to review and submit your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/update-other-income-details-step4g-190121.png)

## Step 5: add other income details

To add new other income details, select **Add**.

You can’t use this service to record one-off employment income. If you’ve been paid one-off employment income, you can report this when you [report employment income](/employment-income-reporting).

Select **Add**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step5a-300822.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step5b-050121.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if your other income is paid from a source outside Australia.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step5c-300822.png)

Select the type of income from the **dropdown** menu.

In this example we’ll select **Sales commission**.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-update-other-income-step5d-241022.png)

Enter a short description of your other income, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step5e-050121.png)

Enter the full name of all persons or organisations who pay you.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step5f-050121.png)

Enter the **payment amount**, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step5g-300822.png)

We’ll ask you some more questions about your other income. We may ask you to give details about the date you were paid.

The questions we ask you depend on the other income you’re adding. Answer all the questions, then select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step5i-050121.png)

When you’ve answered all of the questions, select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step5j-050121.png)

The details you added show as **New** on the Other income summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

On this page, you can select any of the following:

-   **Edit** if you need to make changes
-   **Delete** to remove your update
-   **Add** to add more other income details.

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step5j-300822.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step5l-050121.png)

## Step 6: review and submit

The Income and assets summary page shows updates that are ready to review and submit.

We’ll cancel your changes if you don’t submit them by the date we tell you.

Select either:

-   **Cancel updates** if you no longer want to update your details or need to start again
-   **View/Edit details** in another category to view or make changes.

To submit your changes, select **Review and submit updates**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step6a-300822.png)

Select **Begin** to check your updates are correct.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step6b-050121.png)

We’ll give you a summary of the details you’ve given us.

Review each section to check your details are correct. If you need to make changes, select **Edit other income**.

If all the details are correct, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step6c-300822.png)

Read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I declare and understand that:**.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-update-other-income-step6d.241022.png)

Select **Confirm information** to save all the information you’ve given us and continue. You won’t be able to make any changes to your answers after this.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step6e-050121.png)

Before you submit your update, you may need to complete some extra tasks. These will be listed in **Next steps**.

Select **Begin** to complete the extra tasks for your update.

If you don’t need to complete extra tasks, go to [Get your receipt](#receipt).

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step6f-050121.png)

These tasks will show as **Required**.

When a task is **Required**, you must complete it before you can submit your update.

Select **Upload** for the task you need to complete. You’ll need to do this for each **Required** task.

Read more about how to:

-   [upload documents using your Centrelink online account](/centrelink-online-account-help-upload-documents)
-   [upload documents using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app-help-upload-documents).

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step6g-300822.png)

When you’ve completed the task, the status changes from Required to **Done**.

If you’ve completed a task and you need to change it, select **Edit**.

When you’ve completed all Required tasks, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-08/coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step6h-300822.png)

If you’re ready to submit your update, select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-01/coa-update-other-income-step6i-050121.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a receipt when you submit your update. Make a note of the **Receipt Number** for your records.

You won’t be able make changes to any of your income and assets details until we finalise your updates. We may contact you to do this.

On this page, you can select any of the following:

-   **Information you provided** to view a summary of the details you’ve given us
-   **Return home** to go back to your homepage
-   **Save receipt** to keep a copy of your receipt.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-update-other-income-step6j-241022.png)

## Step 7: sign out

From your homepage you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-09/update-og-coa-update-income-and-assets-details-other-income-step7-200922.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.
