# Centrelink online account help - Update your child’s education details for Family Tax Benefit

Instructions on how to view or update your child’s education details for Family Tax Benefit (FTB).

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#step1)
-   [Step 2: view, add or update your child’s education details](#step2)
-   [Step 3: review and submit](#step3)
-   [Step 4: sign out](#step4)

If your child’s study details change, you need to let us know. For example, tell us study details when your child either:

-   turns 16
-   stops or changes study after they turn 16.

Find out more about [FTB when your child is 16 to 19 or stops studying](/family-tax-benefit-when-your-child-16-to-19-and-stops-full-time-study-or-school).

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

From your homepage, select **MENU**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-02/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step1a.png)

Select **My Family**, then **Family assistance**, and **Manage child education details**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-02/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step1b.png)

## Step 2: view, add or update your child’s education details

Select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2a.png)

**Select** the child you wish to view or update.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2b.png)

The next page will show a summary of your child’s study details. If there’s nothing recorded, you’ll be asked to start an update.

Select **Edit** to update your child’s existing study details.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2c.png)

Select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2d.png)

Select your child’s study load, then **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2e.png)

The next page will show the level of study we have recorded. Select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2f.png)

Select the type of schooling your child is enrolled in from dropdown menu, then **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2g.png)

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2h.png)

You’ll need to confirm if your child started the course after they turned 16. Select **Yes** or **No**, then **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2i.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and choose the date your child started the course of study, then select **Next**. You can also enter the date manually as dd/mm/yyyy.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2j.png)

You’ll need to confirm if your child is studying outside Australia. Select **Yes** or **No**, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2k.png)

When you’ve answered all questions, select **Continue**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2l.png)

The next page will show a summary of your child’s updated study details.

Select either:

-   **Edit** if you need to change any details
-   **Next** if the details are correct.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2m.png)

Select **Continue** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step2n.png)

## Step 3: review and submit

Select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step3a.png)

The next page will show a summary of your child’s updated education details. Make sure the details are correct, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step3b.png)

Read the Declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I have read and agree with the above conditions**, then **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step3c.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a **Receipt ID** to confirm your update. Make a note of this number for your records, then select one of the following:

-   **Information you provided** to view a summary of the details you’ve given us
-   **Return home** to go back to your homepage
-   **Save receipt** to keep a copy of your receipt.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-01/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step3d.png)

## Step 4: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2026-02/coa-update-child-education-details-ftb-step4a.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.

View other [online account and Express Plus mobile app guides](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps) for more step-by-step information and some video demonstrations.
