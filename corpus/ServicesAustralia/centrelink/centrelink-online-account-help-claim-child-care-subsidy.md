# Centrelink online account help - Claim Child Care Subsidy

Instructions on how to claim Child Care Subsidy.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: claim for Child Care Subsidy](#a2)
-   [Step 3: tell us about your situation and finances](#a3)
-   [Step 4: review claim](#a4)
-   [Step 5: submit supporting documents](#a5)
-   [Step 6: submit claim](#a6)
-   [Step 7: sign out](#a7)

[Child Care Subsidy](/child-care-subsidy) provides help with the cost of child care.

You can claim this subsidy using your Centrelink online account through myGov.

We’ll prefill any information you’ve already given us into your Child Care Subsidy claim.

If you need to stop your claim, you can save your progress and come back later. You should finish the step you’re on first. When you come back you can complete the remaining steps.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

Select **MENU** from your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-11/update-og-claim-ccs-step1a.png)

Select **Payments and Claims**, then **Claims** and **Make a claim**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-11/update-og-claim-ccs-step1b.png)

On the Make a Claim page, select **Get started** in the **Families** category.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/claim-child-care-subsidy-step1c-040425.png)

Select **Apply for Family Assistance (including Paid Parental Leave)**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/update-claim-step-1d.png)

We’ll ask you some questions to check if you’re eligible for the payment you’ve chosen.

This helps you decide if you should claim this type of payment.

To check your eligibility, select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/update-claim-step-1e.png)

Answer all the questions, then select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed. You may need to work through more than one page.

If you need help, select the **question mark** icon.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/update-claim-step-1f.png)

## Step 2: claim for Child Care Subsidy

On the next page, we’ll ask about your family. Select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/update-claim-step-2a.png)

We’ll ask you some questions about your children.

Answer all the questions, then select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed. You may need to work through more than one page.

If you need help, select the **question mark** icon.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/update-claim-step-2b.png)

On the Claim options page, we’ll show the different types of family assistance you can claim.

Select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/update-claim-step-2c.png)

Select **Child Care Subsidy**, then **Next**.

You can claim this subsidy and other family assistance payments at the same time. To do this, select any additional payments you want to claim then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/update-claim-step-2d.png)

## Step 3: tell us about your situation and finances

To begin your claim, select **Start** in Your personal details.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ccs-how-to-claim-step3a-280923.png)

When you’ve completed a step, it will show as **Completed**. You can also select **Edit** to update the information you gave us.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ccs-how-to-claim-step3b-280923.png)

Continue to work through the remaining steps until they all show as **Completed**.

If you haven’t completed all the required steps, an **Updates required** message will appear. You must complete all steps before moving on to Review and confirm.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ccs-how-to-claim-step3c-280923.png)

## Step 4: review claim

To review your claim, select **Start** in Review and confirm.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ccs-how-to-claim-step4a-280923.png)

On the Confirm details page, we’ll give you a summary of your claim answers.

Select **Begin** to review your answers.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ccs-how-to-claim-step4b-280923.png)

You can update your details by selecting any of the update buttons. For example, select **Update About Me** to change your contact details, or **Update Relationship** to change your relationship status.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ccs-how-to-claim-step4c-280923.png)

If all the details are correct, select and read the **declaration and acknowledgement** text. If you understand and agree with your obligations, select **I understand and agree to the above terms**. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ccs-how-to-claim-step4d-280923.png)

Select **Confirm information** to save all the information you've provided.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ccs-how-to-claim-step4e-280923.png)

## Step 5: submit supporting documents

Before you can submit your claim, there are tasks you must complete.

Tasks may include giving us documents to support your claim. If you don’t have your documents ready, you can submit them later.

Find out how to upload documents using:

-   your [Centrelink online account](/centrelink-online-account-help-upload-documents)
-   the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app-help-upload-documents).

On the Next steps page select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ccs-how-to-claim-step5a-280923.png)

Complete any outstanding tasks, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-11/update-og-claim-ccs-step5a.png)

## Step 6: submit claim

If you’ve completed all required tasks and you’re ready to submit your claim, select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-11/update-og-claim-ccs-step6a.png)

We’ll give you a **Claim ID** to let you know your claim was submitted. Make a note of this number for your records. Use your **Claim ID** if you need to talk to us about your claim.

We’ll also give you an estimated completion date range of when we’ll process your claim.

You don’t need to contact us about your claim unless we ask you to.

Select:

-   **Withdraw claim** if you need to withdraw your claim
-   **View claim** to view the information you gave us.

Select **Home** to go back to your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-11/update-og-claim-ccs-step6b.png)

You can track the progress of your claim from your homepage. To check what stage your claim is at, select **View**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-11/update-og-claim-ccs-step6c.png)

## Step 7: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-11/update-og-claim-ccs-step7.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.
