# Centrelink online account help - Add new study details

Instructions on how to add new study details for Youth Allowance, Austudy, ABSTUDY, ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement and Pensioner Education Supplement using your Centrelink online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: add new course when you don’t know the full details](#step2)
-   [Step 3: add new course when you know the full details](#step3)
-   [Step 4: review and submit](#step4)
-   [Step 5: sign out](#step5)

If you need to update or remove your study details, there’s a different way to do this. For help, read [Centrelink online account help - update your study details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-study-details).

Read the following information about when you can add new study details online:

-   ABSTUDY, ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement and Youth Allowance job seeker customers can add new study details any time
-   Youth Allowance student, Austudy and Pensioner Education Supplement customers can add new study details if their study end date is within the next 13 weeks
-   Customers who only get Tertiary Access Payment can’t add new study details.

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

From your homepage, select **MENU**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step1a.png)

Select **My details**, then **Personal and contact details** and **Manage study details**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step1b-150424.png)

Your study details will show on the Study details page.

Select **Add a new course**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step2a-150424.png)

Select **Next** to tell us about your new course.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-06/update-og-coa-add-new-study-details-step1e-240624.png)

If you get ABSTUDY, ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement or Pensioner Education Supplement, or Youth Allowance as a job seeker, go to [Step 3](#step3).

If you get Austudy or Youth Allowance as a student, and you:

-   only know the start date, select **I only know the start date**, then select **Next**. Go to [Step 2](#step2).
-   know all the details of your new course, select **I know all the information**. Then select **Next**. Go to [Step 3](#step3).

![](/sites/default/files/2024-06/update-og-coa-add-new-study-details-step1f-240624.png)

## Step 2: add new course when you don’t know the full details

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date you’ll start studying. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step2d-150424.png)

Select **Continue** to proceed.

Go to [Step 4](#step4).

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step2e-150424.png)

## Step 3: add new course when you know the full details

Enter the **name of your education provider**, then select **Search**. If you can’t find your provider, enter the name you know them by.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3a-150424.png)

Select your **education provider** from the **dropdown** menu, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3b-150424.png)

Select the **campus you’re studying at**, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3c-150424.png)

Enter the **name of your course**, then select **Search**. If you can’t find your course, enter the name you know the course by.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3d-150424.png)

Select your course from the **dropdown** menu, then **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3e-150424.png)

Select your **course code** from the **dropdown** menu, then **Next**.

If your course code doesn’t show, select either:

-   **Other** to enter it
-   **Unknown** if you don’t know it.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3f-150424.png)

Select how you’re completing your study from the **dropdown** menu, then **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3g-150424.png)

Select the **year or stage of the course** you’ll be studying this study period from the **dropdown** menu. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3h-150424.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date you started or will start studying. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3i-150424.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the start date of your course. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3j-150424_1.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date you expect to complete your course. This will be the date of your final exam at the end of your course. If your course end date changes before then, you’ll need to [tell us](/contact-us).

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3k-150424_1.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the end date of your course. Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3l-150424_1.png)

Select whether you’re studying Full time or Part time, then **Next**. If you’re studying part time, we’ll ask some more questions.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3m-150424_1.png)

Enter your **student identification (ID) number**, then select **Next**. If you don’t know it, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3n-150424_1.png)

Select **Continue** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3o-150424_1.png)

If you’re eligible, we’ll ask if you want to apply for a [Student Start-up Loan](/student-start-up-loan). Read the information about Student Start-up Loan, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-06/update-og-coa-add-new-study-details-step3p-240624.png)

Select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3q-150424_1.png)

If you want to apply for a [Student Start-up Loan](/student-start-up-loan), select the box next to the period you want to apply for.

If you don’t want to apply for a [Student Start-up Loan](/student-start-up-loan), don’t select any boxes under your **Claim Decision**.

In this example, we’ll apply for one loan period of Jul 2024 to Dec 2024.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-06/update-og-coa-add-new-study-details-step3r-240624.png)

If you apply for a Student Start-up Loan, we’ll ask you to accept the Student Start-up Loan Terms and Conditions.

If you understand and accept the Terms and Conditions, select **Yes**, then **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-06/update-og-coa-add-new-study-details-step3s-240624.png)

Select **Continue** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step3s-150424_1.png)

## Step 4: review and submit

Select **Next** to review and submit your updates.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-06/update-og-coa-add-new-study-details-step4a-240624.png)

We’ll give you a summary of the details you’ve given us. Check each section to make sure it’s correct.

If the details are wrong, select the **Edit** button. For example, select **Edit new course details** to change those details.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step4e-150424.png)

If all the details are correct, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-06/update-og-coa-add-new-study-details-step4c-240624.png)

Read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I have read and agree with the above conditions**, then **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-06/update-og-coa-add-new-study-details-step4d-240624.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a receipt when you submit your update. Make a note of the **Receipt ID** for your records.

You won’t need to do anything else unless we ask you to.

Select any of the following:

-   **Information you provided** to view a summary of the details you’ve given us
-   **Return to Study details summary** to go back to the Study details page
-   **Return home** to go back to your online account homepage
-   **Save your receipt** to keep a copy of your receipt.

In this example, we’ll select **Return to Study details summary** to go back to the Study details page.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step4h-150424.png)

On the Study details page, we’ll tell you the following:

-   your update is complete or is waiting to be processed
-   if your update is waiting to be processed, you won’t be able to update your study details again until we complete this update.

Select **Home** to go back to your online account homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step4i-150424.png)

## Step 5: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-04/coa-add-new-study-details-step5a.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.
