# Centrelink online account help guides

If you need help with your Centrelink online account, read the online account guides or watch our video demonstrations.

To access your Centrelink online account you need to have it linked to myGov.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

You can also use the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app).

If you don’t have a myGov account or a Centrelink online account you’ll need to [create them](/create-online-account?context=64107). If you need help with linking your account, read the [myGov online guides or watch our video demonstrations](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps?context=64107).
