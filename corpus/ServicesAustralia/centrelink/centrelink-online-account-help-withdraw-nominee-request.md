# Centrelink online account help - Withdraw a nominee request

Instructions on how to withdraw a nominee request made in your Centrelink online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#step1)
-   [Step 2: withdraw request](#step2)
-   [Step 3: review and submit](#step3)
-   [Step 4: get your receipt](#step4)
-   [Step 5: sign out](#step5)

You can withdraw your online request for a nominee while it’s still pending.

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer view. If you’re using a mobile device, the page layout will look different.

## Step 1: get started

[Sign into myGov](https://my.gov.au/) and select Centrelink.

Select **MENU** from your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/coa-withdraw-nominee-request-step1a-270323.png)

Select **My details**, followed by **Nominee arrangements** and **Manage nominee arrangements**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/coa-withdraw-nominee-request-step1b-270323.png)

## Step 2: withdraw request

On the Nominee arrangements page, select **Pending requests**.

You’ll need to check the details. You can then select **Withdraw** to cancel a pending request.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/coa-withdraw-nominee-request-step2a-270323.png)

## Step 3: review and submit

We’ll give you a summary of the nominee request you want to withdraw.

You’ll need to select either **Yes** or **No** to the question, Do you want to withdraw this nominee arrangement request? then select **Next**.

If you need to change any details, select **Previous**.

If you don’t want to continue or to start again, select **Home**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/coa-withdraw-nominee-request-step3a-270323.png)

If all the details are correct, read the declaration.

If you understand and accept the declaration, select **I have read and agree with the above conditions**, then **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/coa-withdraw-nominee-request-step3b-270323.png)

## Step 4: get your receipt

We’ll give you a Receipt ID to confirm your update. You can make a note of this number for your records.

If you’d like check the details of your withdrawal, select **Information you provided**.

You can also **Return to nominee arrangements** page.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/coa-withdraw-nominee-request-step3c-270323.png)

Select the **home icon** to go back to homepage, then go to [Step 5](#step5).

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/coa-withdraw-nominee-request-step3d-270323.png)

## Step 5: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2023-03/coa-withdraw-nominee-request-step5a-270323.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you have finished using your myGov account.

Get more [help for myGov, online accounts and mobile apps](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps).
