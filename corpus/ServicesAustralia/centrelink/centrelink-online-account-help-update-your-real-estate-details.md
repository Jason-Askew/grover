# Centrelink online account help - Update your real estate details

Instructions on how to update your real estate details using your Centrelink online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: view real estate details](#step2)
-   [Step 3: edit real estate details](#step3)
-   [Step 4: delete real estate details](#step4)
-   [Step 5: add new real estate details](#step5)
-   [Step 6: review and submit](#step6)
-   [Step 7: sign out](#step7)

You need to tell us when your circumstances change. Then we can assess your eligibility for payments and services using the correct details. This includes changes to real estate assets for you and your partner.

Read more about [real estate assets](/real-estate-assets) and how they can affect your payment.

The screenshots in this guide are from a desktop computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the myGov app and select **Centrelink**.

Select **MENU** from your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/update-real-estate-details-step1a-040425.png)

Select **Income and assets**, then **Income and assets details** and **Manage income and assets**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/update-real-estate-details-step1b-040425.png)

Read **What you need to know before you start**. Then select **Continue** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-11/coa-update-real-estate-step1c-151121.png)

## Step 2: view real estate details

On the Income and assets summary page, select **View/Edit details** in **Real estate**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step2a-241022.png)

Read **Real estate explained**, then select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step1b.png)

The properties you’ve already told us about will show. This includes your real estate assets in Australia or outside Australia.

Select any of the following:

-   **Home** if you don’t need to make any changes
-   **View more** to access more details about each property
-   **Edit** to update your real estate details and go to [Step 3](#step3) for instructions
-   **Delete** to remove your real estate details and go to [Step 4](#step4) for instructions
-   **Add** to add new real estate details and go to [Step 5](#step5) for instructions.

Some of your real estate details may not have an edit or delete option. You’ll need to [contact us](/contact-us) to make changes to these items.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step2c-241022.png)

## Step 3: edit real estate details

If you need to update any of your properties, select **Edit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step3a-241022.png)

Select **Begin** to edit your property details.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step2b.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date of the change. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step2c.png)

Select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step2d.png)

Select **Begin** to tell us about your property.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step2e.png)

We’ll ask you questions about your property changes.

Answer all the questions, then select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step2f.png)

When you’ve answered all of the questions, select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step2g.png)

The details you changed will show as **Edited** on the Real estate summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

If you’ve entered the wrong details, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To delete real estate details, go to [Step 4](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-real-estate-details#step4).

To add new real estate details, go to [Step 5](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-real-estate-details#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step3h-241022.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions on how to review and submit your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step2i.png)

## Step 4: delete real estate details

If you need to remove any of your properties, select **Delete**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step4a-241022.png)

Select either:

-   **OK** to continue
-   **Cancel** if you no longer want to delete the property.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step3b.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step3c.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date this occurred. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step3d.png)

Select **Yes** or **No** to tell us if the property sold or gifted was for less than its market value.

If you select **Yes**, you’ll need to tell us the sale price or consideration.

Select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step3e.png)

Enter the **sale price or consideration**. The amount you enter needs to be a whole number.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step3f.png)

Select **Delete** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step3g.png)

The property you deleted will show as **Removed** on the Real estate summary page.

If you’ve deleted the wrong property, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To add new real estate details, go to [Step 5](#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step4h-241022.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step3i.png)

## Step 5: add new real estate details

To add new real estate details, select **Add**.

In this example, we’ll add a new residential property.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step5a-241022.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step4b.png)

We’ll ask you some questions about your new property.

Answer all the questions by either:

-   selecting **Yes** or **No**
-   entering information we ask for.

After you answer each question, select **Next** to move to the next one.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step4c.png)

When you’ve answered all of the questions, select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step4d.png)

The property you added will show as **New** on the Real estate summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

On this page, you can select any of these:

-   **Edit** if you need to make changes
-   **Delete** to remove your update
-   **Add** to add more real estate details.

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step5e-241022.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step4f.png)

## Step 6: review and submit

The Income and assets summary page shows updates that are ready to review and submit.

We’ll cancel your changes if you don’t submit them by the date we tell you.

Select either:

-   **Cancel updates** if you no longer want to update your details or need to start again
-   **View/Edit details** in another category to view or make changes.

To submit your changes, select **Review and submit updates**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step6a-241022.png)

Select **Begin** to check your updates are correct.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step5b.png)

We’ll give you a summary of the details you’ve given us.

Review each section to check your details are correct. If you need to make changes, select **Edit Real estate**.

If all the details are correct, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step6c-241022.png)

Read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I declare and understand that:**.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step6d-241022.png)

Select **Confirm information** to save all the information you’ve given us and continue. You won’t be able to make any changes to your answers after this.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step5e.png)

Before you submit your update, you may need to complete some extra tasks. These will be listed in **Next steps**.

Select **Begin** to complete the extra tasks for your update.

If you don’t need to complete extra tasks, go to [Get your receipt](#getyourreceipt).

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step5f.png)

These tasks will show as **Required**.

You must complete any **Required** tasks before you can submit your update.

Select **Upload** for the task you need to complete. You’ll need to do this for each **Required** task.

Read more about how to:

-   [upload documents using your Centrelink online account](/centrelink-online-account-help-upload-documents)
-   [upload documents using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app-help-upload-documents).

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step6g-241022.png)

When you’ve completed the task, the status changes from Required to **Done**.

If you’ve completed a task and you need to change it, select **Edit**.

When you’ve completed all Required tasks, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step6h-241022.png)

If you’re ready to submit your update, select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-realestate-step5i.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a receipt when you submit your update. Make a note of the **Receipt Number** for your records.

You won’t be able make changes to any of your income and assets details until we finalise your updates. We may contact you to do this.

On this page, you can select any of the following:

-   **Information you provided** to view a summary of the details you’ve given us
-   **Return home** to go back to your homepage
-   **Save receipt** to keep a copy of your receipt.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-coa-real-estate-step6j-241022.png)

Read our online guides on how to do any of the following:

-   [update your banking details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-banking-details)
-   [update your managed investments and superannuation](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-managed-investments-and-superannuation-details)
-   [update your shares](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-shares)
-   [update your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-home-contents-personal-effects-vehicles-and-other-assets)
-   [update your income details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-income-and-assets-details-your-other-income-details).

## Step 7: sign out

From your homepage you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/update-real-estate-details-step7-040425.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.
