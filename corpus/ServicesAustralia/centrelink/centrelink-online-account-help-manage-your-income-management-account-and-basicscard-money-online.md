# Centrelink online account help - Manage your Income Management account and BasicsCard money online

Instructions to help you manage your money in your Income Management account and your BasicsCard, using your Centrelink online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: view and search your transaction history](#a2)
-   [Step 3: move money between accounts](#a3)
-   [Step 4: review and submit](#a4)
-   [Step 5: sign out](#step5)

The BasicsCard gives you access to your income managed money through EFTPOS at approved stores and businesses. You can get a BasicsCard if your payments are income managed. Read more about [Income Management and BasicsCard](/income-management-and-basicscard).

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you're using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

[Sign in to myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

Select **MENU** from your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step1a-120625.png)

Select **Payments and claims**, then **Income management and BasicsCard** and **Account details and funds transfer**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step1b-120625.png)

Your Income Management account and BasicsCard balance will show. You’ll be able to:

-   view how much you have left on your BasicsCard daily spend limit
-   search your transaction history.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step1c-120625.png)

## Step 2: view and search your transaction history

To view your past transactions, select **Income Management transactions** or **BasicsCard transactions**.

In this example, we’ll select Income Management transactions.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step2a-120625.png)

To search for a transaction that you know the name of, use the **Search** field.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step2b-120625.png)

To search for transactions within a certain timeframe, select **Advanced search**. We’ll ask you to choose a time period and enter dollar amounts. You can choose to do either or both.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step2c-120625.png)

## Step 3: move money between accounts

To move money between your Income Management account and BasicsCard you must:

-   have an active BasicsCard
-   not have any transfers pending to either your Income Management account or BasicsCard
-   not have a payment nominee.

Select **Transfer funds** to move money between your Income Management account and BasicsCard.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step3a-130625.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step3b-120625.png)

Your account summary will show. Select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step3c-120625.png)

Select the **dropdown** menu to choose what type of transfer you want to do. Then, select either:

-   **From Income Management account to BasicsCard**
-   **From BasicsCard to Income Management account**.

Then, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step3d-120625.png)

Enter the **Amount** you want to transfer, then select **Next**.

The amount can’t be more than the available money in the From account.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step3e-120625.png)

Select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step3f-120625.png)

## Step 4: review and submit

Select **Begin** to review and submit your updates.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step4a-120625.png)

We’ll give you a summary of the details you’ve provided.

Read the details on the page and make sure they’re correct.

If the details are wrong, select **Edit Transfer details** to make changes.

If all the details are correct, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step4b-120625.png)

Read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I declare and understand**, then **Confirm information**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step4c-120625.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a receipt to let you know your transfer was successful. Make a note of the **Receipt ID** for your records.

Select any of the following:

-   **Information you provided** to view a summary of the details you’ve given us
-   **Return home** to go back to your online account homepage
-   **Save your receipt** to keep a copy of your receipt.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step4d-120625.png)

## Step 5: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/manage-inc-management-basicscard-online-step5a-120625.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you've finished using your myGov account.
