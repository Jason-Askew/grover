# Centrelink online account help - Update your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details

Instructions on how to update your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other asset details using your Centrelink online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#step1)
-   [Step 2: view home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details](#step2)
-   [Step 3: edit home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details](#step3)
-   [Step 4: delete home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details](#step4)
-   [Step 5: add new home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other asset details](#step5)
-   [Step 6: review and submit](#step6)
-   [Step 7: sign out](#step7)

You need to tell us when your circumstances change. Then we can assess your eligibility for payments and services using the correct details. This includes changes to assets for you and your partner.

Read more about [assets](/asset-types) and how they may affect your payment.

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

Select **MENU** from your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/update-home-personal-vehicles-assets-step1a-040425.png)

Select **Income and assets**, then **Income and assets details** and **Manage income and assets**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/update-home-personal-vehicles-assets-step1b-040425.png)

Read **What you need to know before you start**. Select **Continue** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-11/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step1c-121121.png)

## Step 2: view home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details

On the Income and assets summary page, select **View/Edit details** in **Home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step2a-241022.png)

Read **Home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets explained**. Then select **Begin**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step1b.png)

The home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details you’ve already told us about will show.

Select any of the following:

-   **Home** if you don’t need to make any changes
-   **Edit** to update home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details and go to [Step 3](#step3) for instructions
-   **Delete** to remove home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details and go to [Step 4](#step4) for instructions
-   **Add** to add new home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details and go to [Step 5](#step5) for instructions.

Some of your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets may not have an edit or delete option. You’ll need to [contact us](/contact-us) to make changes to these items.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step2c-241022.png)

## Step 3: edit home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details

If you need to update your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other asset details, select **Edit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step3a-241022.png)

Select **Begin** to edit your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details. In this example, we’ll edit household and personal effects.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step2b.png)

If you need help, read the information with the **question mark** icon on each page.

Enter the new net value of your household and personal effects. The amount you enter needs to be a whole number.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step2c.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date of the change. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step2d.png)

Select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step2e.png)

The details you changed will show as **Edited** on the Home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

If you’ve entered the wrong details, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To delete home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details, go to [Step 4](#step4)

To add new home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details, go to [Step 5](#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step3f-241022.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions on how to review and submit your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step2g.png)

## Step 4: delete home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details

If you need to remove any of your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details, select **Delete**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step4a-241022.png)

Select either:

-   **OK** to continue
-   **Cancel** if you no longer want to delete your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step3b.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step3c.png)

Select the **calendar** icon and enter the date you deleted your home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets. Or, enter it manually in the format of dd/mm/yyyy.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step3d.png)

Select **Delete** to proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step3e.png)

The details you deleted will show as **Removed** on the Home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets summary page.

If you’ve deleted the wrong details, select **Restore** to bring back your previous details.

To add new home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other asset details, go to [Step 5](#step5).

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step4f-241022.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

Go to [Step 6](#step6) for instructions on how to review and submit your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step3g.png)

## Step 5: add new home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other asset details

To add new home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details, select **Add**. In this example, we’ll add a new asset.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step5a-241022.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step4b.png)

We’ll ask you some more questions about your asset. These will be different depending on what asset you’re adding.

Answer all the questions, then select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step4c.png)

When you’ve answered all of the questions, select **Continue** to save your changes and proceed.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step4d.png)

The details you added will show as **New** on the Home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets summary page. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

On this page, you can select any of the following:

-   **Edit** if you need to make changes
-   **Delete** to remove your update
-   **Add** to add more home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets details.

If the details are correct and you’ve finished making changes, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step5e-241022.png)

If you’re ready to review and submit your changes, select **Continue**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step4f.png)

## Step 6: review and submit

The Income and assets summary page shows updates that are ready to review and submit.

We’ll cancel your changes if you don’t submit them by the date we tell you.

Select either:

-   **Cancel updates** if you no longer want to update your details or need to start again
-   **View/Edit details** in another category to view or make changes.

To submit your changes, select **Review and submit updates**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step6a-241022.png)

Select **Begin** to check your updates are correct.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step5b.png)

We’ll give you a summary of the details you’ve given us.

Review each section to check your details are correct. If you need to make changes, select **Edit Home contents, personal effects, vehicles and other assets**.

If all the details are correct, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step6c-241022.png)

Read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I declare and understand that:**.

Then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step6d-241022.png)

Select **Confirm information** to save the information you’ve given us and continue. You won’t be able to make any changes to your answers after this.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step6e-241022.png)

Before you submit your update, you may need to complete some extra tasks. These will be listed in **Next steps**.

Select **Begin** to complete the extra tasks for your update.

If you don’t need to complete extra tasks, go to [Get your receipt](#getyourreceipt).

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step5f.png)

These tasks will show as **Required**.

When a task is **Required** you must complete it before you can submit your update.

Select **Upload** for the task you need to complete. You’ll need to do this for each Required task.

Read our online guides on how to do any of the following

-   [Upload documents using your Centrelink online account](/centrelink-online-account-help-upload-documents)
-   [Upload documents using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app-help-upload-documents).

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step6g-241022.png)

When you’ve completed the task, the status changes from Required to **Done**.

If you’ve completed a task and you need to change it, select **Edit**.

When you’ve completed all Required tasks, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step6h-241022.png)

If you’re ready to submit your update, select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2020-10/coa-update-home-pers-other-assets-step5i.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a receipt when you submit your update. Make a note of the **Receipt Number** for your records.

You won’t be able make changes to your income and assets details until we finalise your updates. We may contact you to do this.

On this page, you can select any of the following:

-   **Information you provided** to view a summary of the details you’ve given us
-   **Return home** to go back to your homepage
-   **Save receipt** to keep a copy of your receipt.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/coa-home-pers-other-assets-step6j-241022.png)

Read our online guides on how to do any of the following:

-   [update your banking details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-banking-details)
-   [update your shares](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-shares)
-   [update your managed investments and superannuation](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-managed-investments-and-superannuation-details)
-   [update your real estate information](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-real-estate-details)
-   [update your other income details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-income-and-assets-details-your-other-income-details).

## Step 7: sign out

From your homepage you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-04/update-home-personal-vehicles-assets-step7-040425.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.
