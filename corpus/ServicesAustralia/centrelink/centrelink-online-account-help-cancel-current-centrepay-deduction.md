# Centrelink online account help - Cancel a current Centrepay deduction

How to use your Centrelink online account to cancel a current Centrepay deduction.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: view your current Centrepay deductions](#a2)
-   [Step 3: cancel a current Centrepay deduction](#a3)
-   [Step 4: review and submit](#a4)
-   [Step 5: sign out](#a5)

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

[Sign in to myGov](https://my.gov.au/) and select **Centrelink**.

Select **MENU** from your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step1-090622.png)

## Step 2: view your current Centrepay deductions

Select **Payments and claims**, then **Manage payments**, then **Manage deductions**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step2a-090622.png)

The Deductions Summary tab displays a summary of any current Centrepay arrangements that you have in place.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step2b-090622.png)

## Step 3: cancel a current Centrepay deduction

Select the **rubbish bin** icon next to the deduction you want to cancel.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step3a-090622.png)

Select either:

-   **OK** to cancel the deduction
-   **Back** if the details are not correct and you need to make updates.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step3b-090622.png)

Select **OK** to continue.

## Step 4: review and submit

We’ll show you a summary of the deduction you’ve selected to cancel.

If all the details are correct, read the declaration. If you understand and accept the declaration, select **I accept this declaration**, then **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step4a-090622.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a **Receipt ID** for the deduction you have cancelled. Make a note of this number for your records.

You don’t need to contact us about your cancellation unless we ask you to.

Select:

-   **Information you have provided** to view the full details of the deduction you have cancelled
-   **Return to Summary** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step4b-090622.png)

If you selected **Return to Summary**, your current Centrepay deductions will display.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step4c-090622.png)

Select **Back** to go back to the homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step4d-090622.png)

## Step 5: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-06/update-centrepay-cancel-a-current-deduction-step5-090622.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you have finished using your myGov account.
