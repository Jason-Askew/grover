# Centrelink online account help - Update your Family Tax Benefit (FTB) payment choices

To help avoid being overpaid or underpaid your Family Tax Benefit, it’s important to make sure your family income estimate is accurate and your payment choice is right for you.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: update your FTB payment choice](#a2)
-   [Step 3: review and submit](#a3)
-   [Step 4: receipt and next steps](#a4)
-   [Step 5: sign out](#a5)

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

[Sign into myGov](https://my.gov.au/) and select **Centrelink**.

Select **MENU** from your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step1a.png)

To update your [payment choice for Family Tax Benefit](/payment-choices-for-ftb) select **My family**, then **Family assistance** and **Payment choices for Family Tax Benefit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step1b.png)

## Step 2: update your FTB payment choice

Select the **Update** icon for the financial year you wish to update. From April to June you can update both the current and next financial year payment choices.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step2a.png)

Select the **Edit** icon for the **Payment type** you wish to update. Read more about [payment choices for Family Tax Benefit](/payment-choices-for-ftb).

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step2b.png)

Select your new payment choice from the options, then select **Next**. You may be overpaid if you don’t choose the recommended payment choice. If you’re paid too much, you’ll have to pay any excess amount back. We’ll let you know if this happens.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step2c.png)

Select **Continue**

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step2d.png)

The status of **Updated** will display on the left hand side of the payment choice that has been updated. You can select **Edit** to change your payment choice for another **Payment type** or select **Next** to continue the update.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step2e.png)

Select **Continue**

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step2f.png)

## Step 3: review and submit

Select **Begin** to review your changes.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step3a.png)

Confirm your changes. Read the information and if all the details are correct select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step3b.png)

Before you submit the update, you must read the declaration. If you understand and accept the declaration, select **I have read and agree with the above conditions**, then **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step3c.png)

## Step 4: receipt and next steps

We’ll give you a **Receipt ID** when you submit your update. You can select **Save your receipt** to save a copy.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step4a.png)

You can select [**family income estimate**](/your-family-income-estimate-for-family-assistance-payments) to update your estimate or select **Return home** to go back to your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step4b.png)

If you get Child Care Subsidy, you may need to update your recognised participation.

## Step 5: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-09/coa-update-ftb-payment-choices-step5a.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.
