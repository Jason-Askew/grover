# Centrelink online account help - Complete your income stream review

Instructions to complete your income stream review using your Centrelink online account .

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: income stream review](#step2)
-   [Step 3: review and submit](#a3)
-   [Step 4: sign out](#a4)

There are 2 ways to complete your income stream review. You can use your Centrelink online account or a secure One Time Access Code (OTAC). This guide shows the process for using Centrelink online account. For instructions on how to use OTAC, go to [Income Stream Reviews](/income-stream-reviews).

Each financial year we review certain types of income streams. We do this to ensure we assess your payments and concession cards correctly.

Read more about [Income Stream Reviews](/income-stream-reviews).

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The page layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

## Step 1: get started

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Centrelink**.

From your homepage, select the **Complete your income stream review** task. Then go to [Step 2](#step2) to start your income stream review.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-online-guide-coa-complete-your-income-stream-review-step1-111022.png)

## Step 2: income stream review

We’ll show the income stream review you need to complete for you and your partner, if you have one.

Select **Start review** to begin your review.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-2a-291124.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-2b-291124.png)

Select **Update** to give us information about your income stream products.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-2c-291124.png)

Select **Begin** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-2d-291124.png)

The income stream details we ask for will depend on either:

-   the details you’ve given us before
-   the details you need to update.

Enter your **current account balance**, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-2e-291124.png)

Select **Continue** to go back to the Products Summary page.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-2f-291124.png)

Select **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-2g-291124.png)

Select **Continue** to review and submit your update.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-2h-291124.png)

## Step 3: review and submit

Select **Begin** to review and submit your update.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-3a-291124.png)

We’ll give you a summary of the details you’ve given us. Check the information to make sure it’s correct.

If the details are wrong, select **Edit Product details** to make changes.

If the details are correct, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-3b-291124.png)

Read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I have read and agree with the above conditions**.

Then select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-3c-291124.png)

### Get your receipt

We’ll give you a receipt when you submit your update. Make a note of the **Receipt ID** for your records.

You won’t need to do anything else unless we ask you to.

Select either:

-   **Information you have provided** to view a summary of the details you’ve given us
-   **Return home** to go back to your online account homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/complete-income-stream-review-step-3d-291124.png)

## Step 4: sign out

From your homepage you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-online-guide-coa-complete-your-income-stream-review-step4-111022.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you've finished using your myGov account.
