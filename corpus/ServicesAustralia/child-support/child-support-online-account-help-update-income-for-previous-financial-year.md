# Child Support online account help - Update income for a previous financial year

Instructions on how to update your income for a previous financial year using your Child Support online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: update your income](#a2)
-   [Step 3: review and submit](#a3)
-   [Step 4: receipt](#a4)
-   [Step 5: sign out](#a5)

It’s important to keep your income details up to date. This makes sure your child support assessment and child support payments are accurate.

We assess your adjusted taxable income from a previous year to determine how much child support you need to pay.

Read more about [how your income affects your payment](/how-your-income-affects-your-child-support?context=21911).

## Step 1: get started

From your homepage, select **Declare income for a previous financial year** from **Quick links**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-11/update-cs-og-update-income-previous-year-step1a-071122.png)

Or select **MENU**, then **Income**, then **Declare income for a previous financial year**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-11/update-cs-og-update-income-previous-year-step1b-071122.png)

## Step 2: update your income

You’ll start with the Information details page. Read the information to help you update your income.

Select:

-   **Next** to continue
-   **Exit form** if you don’t want to continue, or need to start again.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-11/update-cs-og-update-income-previous-year-step2a-071122.png)

On the Financial year page, use the dropdown menu to select a previous financial year.

Answer all the questions, then select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-11/update-cs-og-update-income-previous-year-step2b-071122.png)

On the Income details page, answer the questions about your gross income details. The gross income is the amount your employer paid you before tax.

Read the information in the alert boxes before you start.

You’ll need to enter $0 in any field that doesn’t apply to you.

If you’re not sure what we’re asking for, select the link above the field for details.

When you finish answering all the questions, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-11/update-cs-og-update-income-previous-year-step2c-071122.png)

## Step 3: review and submit

We’ll give you a summary of your updates.

Select:

-   **Financial year details** or **Income details** to display the information you gave us
-   **Back** if you need to make changes

Select **Exit** if you:

-   no longer want to update your previous income
-   want to start again
-   want to save and come back later.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-11/update-cs-og-update-income-previous-year-step3a-071122.png)

If all the details are correct, read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I have read, understood and accept the declaration**.

Select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-11/update-cs-og-update-income-previous-year-step3b-071122.png)

## Step 4: receipt

We’ll give you a receipt to confirm your update. Make a note of the **Receipt ID** for your records.

Select **Finish** to go back to your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-11/update-cs-og-update-income-previous-year-step4a-071122.png)

## Step 5: sign out

From your homepage you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-11/update-cs-og-update-income-previous-year-step5a-071122.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you've finished using your myGov account.

We have other guides to help you. Read about [help for myGov, online accounts and mobile apps](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps?context=64107).
