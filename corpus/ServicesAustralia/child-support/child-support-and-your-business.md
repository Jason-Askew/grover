# Child Support and your business

Information for employers who need to make child support deductions and information for legal, medical and financial professionals that are involved in child support matters.

-   [Legal Professionals](#a2)
-   [Medical Professionals](#a3)

## Employers

We provide [child support information for employers](/child-support-information-for-employers) who are involved in the collection of child support.

[Single Touch Payroll](/single-touch-payroll-for-employers?context=60075) and [Child Support Business Online Services](/child-support-business-online-services) provide you with a secure way to manage employee child support deductions.

If we ask you, you are legally required to deduct child support payments from your employees and sub-contractors’ salaries or wages.

If your business is in administration or liquidation and you have an outstanding child support debt, you should tell us [whether your debt is likely to be paid](/cs4456?context=60075).
