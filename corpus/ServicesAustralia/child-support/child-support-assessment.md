For help with Child Support, you can call the [Child Support Enquiry Line](/phone-us#csgeneralenquiries). Let us know if you need an interpreter and we'll arrange one for free.

Call the [Centrelink Indigenous Call Centre](/centrelink-indigenous-call-centre?context=64107) to speak to us about Centrelink payments and services for free.

The formula we use to work it out is in Australian law.

If you disagree with a decision, find out when you can object to a [Child Support decision](/objections-to-child-support-decisions).

We’re reviewing assessments based on provisional income from 2008 onwards. You don’t need to contact us about this unless you’ve received a letter. Read more about [what to do if you got a letter about a system issue with your child support case](/how-your-income-affects-your-child-support?context=21911#letter).
