# Child Support online account help - Advise of extraordinary payment

Instructions on how to tell us when an extraordinary payment was made using your Child Support online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#a1)
-   [Step 2: add payment details](#a2)
-   [Step 3: review and submit](#a3)
-   [Step 4: receipt](#a4)
-   [Step 5: sign out](#a5)

When we collect and transfer your payments, you may want to tell us about a private child support payment that was made. We call these payments extraordinary payments or non-agency payments (NAPs).

Read more about [how to pay child support](/choose-how-to-pay-child-support).

## Step 1: get started

From your homepage, select **Advise an extraordinary payment** from **Quick links**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step1a-271022.png)

Or select **MENU**, then **Payments**, then **Advise an extraordinary payment**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step1b-271022.png)

## Step 2: add payment details

You’ll start on the Information page. Read this page to help you complete your update, then select **Next**.

Select **Exit** if you:

-   no longer want to advise of an extraordinary payment
-   want to start again
-   want to save your progress and come back later.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step2a-271022.png)

If you select **Exit**, we’ll ask if you want to save your progress.

If you want to save your progress, select **Yes, save for later**, then **OK**.

If you don’t want to save your progress, select **No thanks**, **delete it**, then **OK**.

![](/sites/default/files/2018/12/advise-of-extraordinary-payments2b.png)

The Select other party page won’t display if you only have one case. Select the person who received the extraordinary payment, followed by **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step2c-271022.png)

On the Payment details page, select **Add** to tell us about your payment.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step2d-271022.png)

Enter the details and answer the questions, then select **Add**.

The questions we ask may vary depending on your answers.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step2e-271022.png)

We’ll display the details you’ve given us. Check the information is correct.

Select:

-   the **pencil** icon to correct the payment
-   the **bin** icon to remove the payment
-   **Next** to continue.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step2f-271022.png)

## Step 3: review and submit

We’ll give you a summary of your updates.

Select:

-   **Select other party** and **Payment details** to display the information you gave us
-   **Back** if you need to make changes.

Or, select **Exit**:

-   if you no longer want to advise of extraordinary payment
-   to start again
-   to save your progress and come back later.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step3a-271022.png)

If all the details are correct, read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I have read, understood and accept the declaration.**

Select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step3b-271022.png)

## Step 4: receipt

We’ll give you a receipt to confirm your update. Make a note of the **Receipt ID** for your records.

We’ll also tell you if we need more information.

For more information read [Upload your Child Support documents online](/upload-your-child-support-documents-online).

Select **Finish** to go to the Extraordinary payments history page.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step4a-271022.png)

On the Extraordinary payments history page, we’ll give you a list of all previously processed extraordinary payments.

Select the **Child Support** icon to go back to your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step4b-271022.png)

## Step 5: sign out

From your homepage you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/update-og-cs-extraordinary-payments-step5a-271022.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you've finished using your myGov account.

We have other guides to help you. Read about [help for myGov, online accounts and mobile apps](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps?context=64107).
