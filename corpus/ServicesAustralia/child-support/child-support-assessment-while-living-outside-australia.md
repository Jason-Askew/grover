# While living outside Australia

In some cases we can help separated parents with child support arrangements for their children when one parent lives in another country.

Different countries have a range of child support schemes. How we can help depends on your case and where each parent lives.

Read more information about [child support when parents and children live overseas](/child-support-when-parents-and-children-live-outside-australia).
