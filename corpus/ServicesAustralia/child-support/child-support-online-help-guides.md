# Child Support online help guides

If you need help with your Child Support online account, read our online account and Express Plus mobile app guides or watch our video demonstrations.

To access your [Child Support online account](/child-support-online-accounts), you need to create a [myGov account and link to Child Support](https://my.gov.au/). You can then download and use the [Express Plus Child Support mobile app](/express-plus-child-support-mobile-app).
