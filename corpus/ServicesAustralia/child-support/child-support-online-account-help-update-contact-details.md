# Child Support online account help - Update contact details

Instructions on how to update your contact details using your Child Support online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#step1)
-   [Step 2: update contact details](#step2)
-   [Step 3: update address details](#step3)
-   [Step 4: review and submit](#step4)
-   [Step 5: receipt](#step5)
-   [Step 6: sign out](#step6)

## Step 1: get started

From your homepage, select **Update contact details** or **Update my contact details** from **My profile**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step1a-211022.png)

Or, select **MENU** then **My profile** then **Update my contact details**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step1b-211022.png)

## Step 2: update contact details

On the **Contact details** page, we’ll display the details you’ve given us before.

If you don’t need to update your phone or email details, select **Next**. Then go to [Step 3](#step3) to update your address details.

If you need to update your contact details, delete the incorrect information. Then enter your new details. If you are entering a landline phone number, include the area code and make sure there are no spaces.

Once you finish updating your details, select **Next**.

If you no longer want to update your contact details, or need to start again, select **Exit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step2a-211022.png)

## Step 3: update address details

The **Address details** page will display the postal and residential address details you’ve given us before.

If you don’t need to update your address details, select **Next**. Then go to [Step 4](#step4) to review and submit your details.

If you need to update your **Postal address**, delete the current address and start typing your new address.

If your address does not appear, select **I can’t find my address** to manually enter it.

If your **Residential address** is the same as your **Postal address**, leave the **checkbox** ticked. This will copy your postal address details to the residential address section.

If your details are up to date, select **Next**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step3a-211022.png)

If your **Residential address** is different to your **Postal address**, select the **checkbox** to remove the tick. Then delete the current address and start typing your new address.

If your address does not appear, select **I can’t find my address** to manually enter it.

Once you finish entering your new address details, select **Next**.

If you no longer want to update your address details, or need to start again, select **Exit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step3b-211022.png)

## Step 4: review and submit

We’ll give you a summary of your new details.

To display the full summary, select **Contact details** and **Address details**. The information will expand.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step4a-211022.png)

Read the information on this page to make sure it’s correct.

Select:

-   **Back**, if you need to make any changes
-   **Exit**, if you don’t want to continue, or need to start again.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step4b-211022.png)

If all of the details are correct, read the declaration. If you understand and agree with the declaration, select **I have read, understood and accept the declaration**, then select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step4c-211022.png)

## Step 5: receipt

We’ll give you a receipt to let you know you have submitted your update. Please make a note of the **Receipt ID** for your records.

The updated information may not appear in your online account straight away.

Select **Finish** to go back to your **My profile** page.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step5a-211022.png)

From your **My profile** page, select the **ChildSupport** icon to return to your online account homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step5b-211022.png)

## Step 6: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-update-contact-details-step6a-211022.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you have finished using your myGov account.

We have other guides to help you. Read about [help for myGov, online accounts and mobile apps](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps?context=64107).
