# Child Support online account help - Upload documents

Instructions on how to upload and submit documents using your Child Support online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: get started](#step1)
-   [Step 2: upload your document](#step2)
-   [Step 3: review and submit](#a3)
-   [Step 4: view upload history](#step4)
-   [Step 5: sign out](#a5)

Uploading documents to us online is safe and convenient.

You can upload and submit most documents with your online account through [myGov](https://my.gov.au/). Read about what kind of Child Support [documents you can submit online](/upload-your-child-support-documents-online).

The screenshots in this guide are from a computer. The screen layout will look different if you’re using a mobile device.

Before you upload a document, remove any password protection on the document.

Please make sure all images you upload are appropriate. Don’t upload any images that:

-   are distressing, intimidating or offensive
-   are of the human body as evidence of physical injuries or to support a claim
-   could be considered illegal, as we may report them to the relevant authorities.

## Step 1: get started

[Sign in to myGov](https://my.gov.au/) and select **Child Support**.

On your homepage, select **Send supporting documents** from **Quick links**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step1a-201022.png)

Or, from the homepage, select **MENU**, then **Send supporting documents**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step1b-201022.png)

## Step 2: upload your document

You can include a maximum of 10 files for each upload.

We accept jpg, png, pdf or tiff file formats. Each file must be less than 5 MB in size.

Choose **Select a document type**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step2a-201022.png)

Select the document type you want to upload from **Select a document type**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step2b-201022.png)

Select **Choose files** to find the file or files you want to upload, then select **Open**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step2c-201022.png)

Select:

-   **Add file** if you have more files to upload for this document type
-   **Upload** if you don’t have any more files to upload for the document type
-   **Cancel** if you no longer want to upload a document or need to start again.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step2d-201022.png)

## Step 3: review and submit

Documents you’ve uploaded will show in PDF format.

Review the information to make sure it’s correct. If you need to delete a document, select the **bin** icon.

If you have more documents to upload, select **Choose files** and repeat [Step 2](#step2)

If you don’t have any more documents to upload, select **Submit**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step3a-201022.png)

### Get receipt

We’ll give you a receipt when you submit your documents. Make a note of the **Receipt ID** for your records.

After uploading your documents, you can select:

-   **online history** to view your document upload history and go to [Step 4](#step4).
-   **Finish** to go back to your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step3b-201022.png)

## Step 4: view upload history

You can view documents you’ve uploaded in Online history. You don’t need to upload these again unless we ask you to.

From the Online history page, select **Upload history**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step4a-201022.png)

You can also view your Upload history from your homepage.

Select **MENU**, followed by **Online history**.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step4b-201022.png)

A list of uploaded documents will display.

In Upload history, your documents will be in PDF format. To view your files, you’ll need PDF reader software.

Select a document to open or save it to your files.

You can’t delete any documents in your Upload history.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step4c-201022.png)

Select **ChildSupport** to go back to your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step4d-201022.png)

## Step 5: sign out

From your homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2022-10/csoa-upload-document-step5a-201022.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.

We have other guides to help you. Read about [help for myGov, online accounts and mobile apps](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps).
