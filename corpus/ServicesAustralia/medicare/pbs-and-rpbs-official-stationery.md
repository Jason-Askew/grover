# PBS and RPBS official stationery

You can order Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) or Repatriation Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (RPBS) stationery through Health Professional Online Services (HPOS).

You can order different types of stationery to prescribe and supply PBS or RPBS medicines.

PBS and RPBS computer prescription pad

[![](/sites/default/files/2024-08/pb023-sample-tn.png)](/sites/default/files/2024-08/pb023-sample.png)

The PBS/RPBS computer prescription pad (PB023) is a green, A4 computer-generated prescription pad that can be ordered by:

-   medical practitioners
-   dentists
-   nurse practitioners
-   midwives
-   optometrists.

Order through [HPOS](/hpos?context=20).

Personalised PBS and RPBS standard prescription pad

[![](/sites/default/files/2020-05/pb021p-sample-tn.png)](/sites/default/files/2020-05/pb021p-sample.png)

The PBS/RPBS personalised standard prescription pad (PB021p) is a yellow pad that can be ordered by either medical practitioners or locums.

Use this form to prescribe up to 3 unrestricted, restricted or non-PBS items per prescription. You can get your details pre-printed on each prescription.

Order through [HPOS](/hpos?context=20).

[![](/sites/default/files/2020-05/pb027p-sample-tn.png)](/sites/default/files/2020-05/pb027p-sample.png)

The Dental Practitioner personalised standard prescription pad (PB027p) is a purple pad that can be ordered by dentists.

Order through [HPOS](/hpos?context=20).

[![](/sites/default/files/2020-05/pb037p-sample-tn.png)](/sites/default/files/2020-05/pb037p-sample.png)

The Nurse practitioners and midwives personalised standard prescription pad (PB037p) is an orange pad that can be ordered by either nurse practitioners or midwives.

Order through [HPOS](/hpos?context=20).

[![](/sites/default/files/2020-05/pb029p-sample-tn.png)](/sites/default/files/2020-05/pb029p-sample.png)

The PBS/RPBS optometrist personalised standard prescription pad (PB029p) is a green pad that can be ordered by optometrists.

Order through [HPOS](/hpos?context=20).

Pharmacists and Section 92 medical practitioners PBS Safety Net stationery

[![](/sites/default/files/2024-03/pb013-2008-sample-thumb.png)](/sites/default/files/2024-03/pb013-2008-sample-large.png)

The PBS Safety Net entitlement card (PB013) is an aqua card for customers who reach the PBS Safety Net concession threshold. You must account for each card issued to your pharmacy.

Order through [HPOS](/hpos?context=20).

[![](/sites/default/files/2024-03/pb014-2008-sample-thumb.png)](/sites/default/files/2024-03/pb014-2008-sample-large.png)

The PBS Safety Net concession card (PB014) is a blue card for customers who reach the PBS Safety Net general threshold. You must account for each card issued to your pharmacy.

Order through [HPOS](/hpos?context=20).

[![](/sites/default/files/2024-03/pb038-2008-sample-thumb.png)](/sites/default/files/2024-03/pb038-2008-sample-large.png)

The PBS/RPBS repeat authorisation form (PB038) is a yellow A5 form to attach to prescriptions to show number of repeats and times dispensed.

Order through [HPOS](/hpos?context=20).

[![](/sites/default/files/2024-08/en348-sample-fn.png)](/sites/default/files/2024-08/en348-sample.png)

Use the PBS Safety Net reply paid envelope (EN348) to submit PBS Safety Net claims. Don’t send prescription claims in this envelope.

Order through [HPOS](/hpos?context=20).
