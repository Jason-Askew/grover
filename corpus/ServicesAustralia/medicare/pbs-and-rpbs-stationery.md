# PBS and RPBS stationery

Official Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) prescription stationery to help you prescribe PBS and Repatriation Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (RPBS) medicines.

We provide Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) and Repatriation Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (RPBS) stationery for prescribers, hospitals and suppliers.

To order PBS or RPBS stationery you must have both:

-   a PBS prescriber number, a hospital provider number (HPN) or an approved PBS supplier number
-   a provider location registered with us.

There are different PBS and RPBS official stationery. The stationery you can get depends on your profession.

You can order online using your Health Professional Online Services (HPOS) account. You can also track your order for PBS or RPBS stationery and fix order errors.

If you need to dispose of unwanted and unused stationery there is a safe option.
