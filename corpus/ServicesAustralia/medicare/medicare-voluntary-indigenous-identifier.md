# Medicare Voluntary Indigenous Identifier

We will ask you if you want to identify when you apply for our payments or services.

You can also identify by filling in the form below. You don’t have to do this. It’s your choice.

![](/sites/default/files/2019-10/hs03091101-0034-vii.jpg)

The details you give us help to shape government plans and policies and see if the policies are making a difference. Your information will be kept safe. It is protected by privacy rules and laws.

Follow these steps to identify.

1.  complete your details below and print the page, or you can print the page and fill it in by hand
2.  sign the form
3.  send the completed form to:

Indigenous Access Program  
Reply Paid 9822  
in your capital city
