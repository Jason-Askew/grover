# Medicare card

You get a Medicare card when you’re enrolled in Medicare. Learn how to get, use and replace your card.

To get this you must meet one of the following:

- live in Australia or an Australian dependency island
- visit from certain countries.

Read the full conditions under [enrolling in Medicare](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/enrolling-medicare).
