# Medicare online account help - Update your bank account details

Instructions on how to update your bank account details using your Medicare online account.

## in this guide

-   [Step 1: sign in](#step1)
-   [Step 2: change your bank details](#step2)
-   [Step 3: sign out](#step3)

To update your bank details you need your:

-   Account name
-   BSB
-   Account number.

The screenshots and instructions in this guide are for using Medicare online account on a computer or mobile web browser. The screen layout will look different if you’re using the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app).

## Step 1: sign in

Sign in to [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) or the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) and select **Medicare**.

From your homepage, select **View and edit my details** in **My details**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/moa-update-your-bank-account-details-step1a.png)

## Step 2: change your bank details

The banking details you’ve already told us about will show. To protect your personal information, we’ll only show the last 4 digits of your account number. If you don’t recognise these details, [contact us](/phone-us).

These changes only apply to Medicare payments for you. They won’t apply to others on your Medicare card.

To update your bank details, select **Edit** in **Bank details**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/moa-update-your-bank-account-details-step2a.png)

The Edit bank details page will show your existing bank details.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/update-your-bank-account-details-step2b-050225.png)

To update your bank details, type over the existing details with your new ones including all of the following:

-   **Account name**
-   **BSB**
-   **Account number**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/update-your-bank-account-details-step2c-050225.png)

Check your new bank details are correct, then select **Save bank details**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/update-your-bank-account-details-step2d-050225.png)

We’ll confirm your updated bank details have been saved. Check the changes are correct, then select **Close**.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/moa-update-your-bank-account-details-step2c.png)

To delete your bank details, select **Delete bank details**.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/update-your-bank-account-details-step2f-050225.png)

We’ll tell you that if you delete your bank details, we’ll hold your payments until you give us your bank details. Select either:

-   **Yes** to continue
-   **Cancel** if you no longer want to delete your banking details.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-02/update-your-bank-account-details-step2g-050225.png)

Select **Home** to go back to your homepage.

![](/sites/default/files/2025-06/moa-update-your-bank-account-details-step2h.png)

## Step 3: sign out

From the homepage, you can complete other transactions or select **Return to myGov** to go back to your myGov account.

![](/sites/default/files/2024-11/moa-update-your-bank-account-details-step3a.png)

For your privacy and security, **sign out** when you’ve finished using your myGov account.

View other [online account guides](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps) and video demonstrations about using your online account.
