# Medicare online help guides

If you need help with your Medicare online account, read our online help guides.

To access your [Medicare online account](/medicare-online-account), you need to [create a myGov account](https://my.gov.au/en/create-account/) and [link to Medicare](/mygov-help-link-medicare-to-mygov-with-your-medicare-card?context=64107). If you need [help](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help), visit the myGov website.
