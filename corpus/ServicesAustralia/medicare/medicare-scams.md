# Medicare scams

There are different types of Medicare scams.

If you clicked on a link or gave personal information to a scammer, call our [Scams and Identity Theft Helpdesk](/phone-us#scams).

To report a scam related to myGov or Services Australia, including Centrelink, Medicare, or Child Support, you can email [reportascam@servicesaustralia.gov.au](mailto:reportascam@servicesaustralia.gov.au).

Read more about other types of [Active scams](/active-scams?context=60271).
