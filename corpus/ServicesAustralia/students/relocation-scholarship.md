# Relocation Scholarship

A once a year payment if you get ABSTUDY or Youth Allowance. You must also need to move to or from a regional or remote area for higher education study.

[

Who can get it

You might get Relocation Scholarship if you’re a student getting Youth Allowance or ABSTUDY and you meet these rules.


](/who-can-get-relocation-scholarship?context=22566)

[

What are the study rules and courses

You can get a Relocation Scholarship if you’re studying in a full time approved scholarship course.


](/what-are-relocation-scholarship-study-rules-and-courses?context=22566)

[

How much you can get

Your payment rate of Relocation Scholarship depends on your circumstances.


](/how-much-relocation-scholarship-you-can-get?context=22566)

[

How to get it

You don’t need to apply for the Relocation Scholarship unless you’re doing a regional placement. You may still need to fill out a form, or tell us about changes to your situation.
