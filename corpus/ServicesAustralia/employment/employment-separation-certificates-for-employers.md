# Employment Separation Certificates for employers

Employment Separation Certificates (ESC) help us pay people the right amount from the correct date. You need to complete the certificate within 14 days of the request.

We may release all the information contained in or attached to the certificate to any person under the _Freedom of Information Act 1982_.

If you want any of this information treated in confidence, please attach a statement indicating which information, and give your reasons. We will consider it if you make a request under the _Freedom of Information Act 1982_.
