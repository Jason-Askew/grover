## Workforce Australia

You can search for work in your area using the [Workforce Australia](https://www.workforceaustralia.gov.au/) website. They also have free [online learning modules](https://www.workforceaustralia.gov.au/individuals/coaching/online-learning) that can help you in your job search.

Workforce Australia employment services providers can also help you access a range of programs.

## Jobs and Skills Australia

Jobs and Skills Australia has a [Jobs and Skills Atlas](https://www.jobsandskills.gov.au/jobs-and-skills-atlas) to explore a range of key labour market datasets. It provides insights into the Australian labour market at national, state, and regional level.

## Inclusive Employment Australia

There are services that can help you prepare for, find and keep a job if you have a disability, injury or health condition.

Find out more about [Inclusive Employment Australia](https://www.dss.gov.au/inclusive-employment-australia) on the Department of Social Services website.

## Remote Australia Employment Service

The Remote Australia Employment Service (RAES) may help you if you live in a remote area. You can take part in RAES to get help building skills and getting a job.

RAES offers both:

-   training and development opportunities
-   opportunities to be part of community projects.

Find out more about [RAES for job seekers](https://www.niaa.gov.au/our-work/employment-and-economic-development/remote-jobs/job-seekers) on the National Indigenous Australians Agency website.

## Defence jobs

If you’re interested in a career in the Australian Defence Force, search for jobs on the [ADF Careers](https://www.adfcareers.gov.au/) website.

## Australian Public Service (APS) jobs

If you’re looking for work in the APS, search for jobs on the [APSjobs](https://www.apsjobs.gov.au/s/) website.

## Parent Pathways

Parent Pathways is a voluntary support service for parents and carers of children aged under 6. It can help you identify and achieve your personal, study and work goals. The service is flexible and tailored to your individual circumstances.

You can work with a mentor who will assist you during your time with Parent Pathways. You’ll also get tailored advice in a safe environment, where your children are always welcome.

Learn more about [Parent Pathways](/parent-pathways).
