# Employment income reporting

You must tell us the gross employment income you and your partner get so we can pay you the right amount.

Easter public holidays closures

Our service centres, agents, access points and most call centres close for the Easter public holidays.

This means you’ll need to do your business with us online or by phone self-service.

We’ll be closed on:

-   Good Friday, 3 April 2026
-   Easter Monday, 6 April 2026.
