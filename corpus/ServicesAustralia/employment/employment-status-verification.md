## Requests for employee information

We may ask you to complete a report verifying details about a past or present employee. This helps us to make sure they get the right payments or services from us.

Read about [requests for employee information](/requests-for-employee-information?context=23111).

## Employment Separation Certificates

[Employment Separation Certificates](/su001) help us pay people the right amount from the correct date. You need to complete the certificate within 14 days of the request. This is according to section 196 of the _Social Security (Administration) Act 1999_.
