# Aged Care Specialist Officer

An Aged Care Specialist Officer (ACSO) is part of My Aged Care face-to-face services. They can help with your aged care matters.

## on this page

-   [What an ACSO does](#a1)
-   [How to book an ACSO appointment](#a2)
-   [How to access your video chat appointment](#a3)
-   [Which service centres have ACSO appointments](#a4)

An ACSO can help you in more detail with your aged care matters.

## What an ACSO does

Our ACSOs can help with all of these:

-   providing in-depth information on the different types of aged care services
-   referring you to an assessment organisation for an aged care assessment
-   helping you appoint a registered supporter for My Aged Care
-   providing financial information about aged care services
-   connecting you to local support services.

Free face-to-face appointments are available with an ACSO:

-   through video chat if you have a myGov account
-   in person at some service centres.

## How to book an ACSO appointment

You, your family or nominee can call the [Aged Care line](#toolbar-phone) or visit a service centre to book an appointment with an ACSO. Appointments are available in-person or via video chat.

## How to access your video chat appointment

To access your video chat appointment, use the link we send to your myGov Inbox. The link will also appear in your Centrelink online account and your Express Plus Centrelink mobile app under Manage Appointments.

Once you’ve booked, we’ll send you details of your video chat appointment to your myGov account. If you don’t have a myGov account, you can [create one](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-website/create-mygov-account).

To access your appointment details, [sign in to myGov](https://my.gov.au/) and go to your myGov Inbox under Appointments.

To reschedule or cancel a video chat appointment call the [Aged Care line](#toolbar-phone).

Learn about how [video chat appointments](/video-chat-appointments) work and what you need to do on the day of your appointment.

### How to change your ACSO appointment

To reschedule or cancel an in person or video chat appointment call the [Aged Care line](#toolbar-phone).
