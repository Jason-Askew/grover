# Health Care Card

A concession card to get cheaper medicines and some discounts if you get a payment from us.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-10/health-care-card.jpg)

[

Who can get a card

You may get a Health Care Card if you get specific payments or supplements from us. You’ll get it if you’re on the maximum rate of Family Tax Benefit Part A.


](/who-can-get-health-care-card?context=21981)

[

Who needs to apply

You don’t need to apply for a Health Care Card. If you're eligible, we’ll post your card to you.


](/who-needs-to-apply-for-health-care-card?context=21981)
