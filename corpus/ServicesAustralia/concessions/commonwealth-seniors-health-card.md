# Commonwealth Seniors Health Card

A concession card to get cheaper health care and some discounts if you’ve reached Age Pension age.

To get this card you must meet all these conditions:

-   be Age Pension age or older
-   meet residence rules
-   not be getting an [income support payment](/income-support-payment) from Centrelink or the Department of Veterans’ Affairs
-   give us a Tax File Number or be exempt from doing so
-   meet identity requirements
-   meet the income test.

Find out the full conditions about [who can get it](/who-can-get-commonwealth-seniors-health-card?context=21966).

![](/sites/default/files/2021-10/cshc-front.jpg) ![](/sites/default/files/2021-10/cshc-rear.jpg)

The Commonwealth Seniors Health Card has changed to a laminated card. If you get a new, renewal or replacement card sent to you, it may be either the new laminated card or the older style. Both are a valid proof of concession. The laminated card may seem brighter than the older style card.
