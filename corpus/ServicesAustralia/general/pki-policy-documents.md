# PKI policy documents

Before you apply for a Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) certificate you need to read the common policies and terms and conditions.

You also need to read the specific policy terms and conditions of the certificate issued to you.

The common Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) policy documents apply to all types of certificates available.

Common PKI policy documents

If you have a Medicare Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) certificate read the following SHA-2 Certificate Policy and Certification Practice Statements:

-   [Root Certification Authority Certificate Policy v2.8 2017](/sites/default/files/2018/05/root-certification-authority-certificate-policy-v2.8-2017.docx "Word document")
-   [Root Certification Authority Certification Practice Statement v2.7 2017](/sites/default/files/2018/05/root-certification-authority-certification-practice-statement-v2.7-2017.docx "Word document")
-   [Organisation Certification Authority Certification Practice Statement v2.8 2018](/sites/default/files/2018/09/organisation-certification-authority-certificate-practice-statement-v2.8.docx "Word document").

You also need to read the Terms and Conditions:

-   [Public Key Infrastructure Site Certificates Terms and Conditions of Use](/sites/default/files/2018/08/20180725-public-key-infrastructure-certificate-site-certificates-terms-and-conditions-of-use.docx "Word document")
-   [Public Key Infrastructure Individual Certificate Terms and Conditions of Use](/sites/default/files/2018/08/20180725-public-key-infrastructure-individual-certificate-terms-and-conditions-of-use.docx "Word document").

You may also find it useful to read the [Department of Human Services Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) glossary](/sites/default/files/2018/10/department-of-human-services-pki-glossary.docx "Word document"), February 2018.
