# Estimate of income for use in Child Support Assessment form (CS1670)

Use this form to provide us with an estimate of your income where it is different from the income used for your current child support assessment.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Estimate of income for use in Child Support Assessment form](/sites/default/files/2025-12/cs1670-2512en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Use your [Child Support online account](/child-support-online-accounts) through myGov to submit this form online.
