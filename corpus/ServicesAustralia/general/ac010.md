# Consent to release an aged care client record form (AC010)

Use this form to prove that a care recipient or nominee has consented that an aged care service or Aged Care Assessment Team can request a copy of their electronic Aged Care Client Record.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Consent to release an aged care client record](/sites/default/files/2025-10/ac010-2511en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Aged care services include Residential care, Home care and Multi Purpose Services.
