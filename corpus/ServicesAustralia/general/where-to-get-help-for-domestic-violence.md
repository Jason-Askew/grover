For Centrelink payments and services, you can call our [multilingual phone service](/phone-us#languages).

To access 24/7 counselling and support, call 1800RESPECT on 1800 737 732.

Family and domestic violence can include, but isn’t limited to:

-   controlling behaviour
-   physical violence
-   sexual assault
-   emotional abuse
-   stalking
-   technology facilitated abuse
-   financial abuse.
