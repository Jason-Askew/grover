# Medicine export declaration form (PB302)

Use this form to declare any medicines you are taking or sending out of Australia.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Medicine export declaration form](/sites/default/files/pb302-2102en-f.pdf "PDF document").

You do not need to submit this form to us. You must include this declaration with the medicines you are taking or sending out of Australia.

To fill in this form digitally, you’ll need a computer and Adobe Acrobat Reader or a similar program. You can download Adobe Acrobat Reader for free. If you can’t complete the form digitally, you can print it and complete it by hand.
