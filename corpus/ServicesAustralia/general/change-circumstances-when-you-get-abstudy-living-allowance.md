# Change of circumstances

You need to tell us if your circumstances change when you get ABSTUDY Living Allowance.

## on this page

-   [What to tell us](#a1)
-   [How to tell us](#a2)
-   [Changing or correcting your name](#changename)

## What to tell us

You need to tell us if any of the following things change:

-   you [change or correct your name](#changename)
-   your personal and contact details
-   your bank details
-   your study, including any changes to your study load
-   your [relationship changes](/relationship-changes), for example if you become partnered or separated
-   care arrangements for anyone in your care, including your children
-   you’re [leaving the country](/travel-outside-australia-rules-for-abstudy?context=45541), temporarily or to live somewhere else
-   your or your partner’s income or assets change
-   you’re no longer getting a STARTUP-HELP loan for your Startup Year course.

You also need to tell us if there are changes to both your:

-   accommodation details
-   living arrangements if you’re single and start sharing your accommodation with a non family member who’s 16 or older.

You need to tell us about a change in your circumstances within 14 days. If you don’t, we may pay you too much. This means you’ll have a [debt](/centrelink-debts-and-overpayments) you need to pay back.

## How to tell us

To tell us about changes to living arrangements you’ll need to complete the [relationship details form](/ss284).

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov you can tell us about changes to your circumstances online. To do this, sign in to myGov and select **My details**.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

You can also tell us using either:

-   the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app)
-   [Centrelink phone self service](/centrelink-phone-self-service).

Read more about what you can do using [self service](/self-service).

If you don’t have access to a self service option please call the [ABSTUDY line](#toolbar-phone).
