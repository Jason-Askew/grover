# Adult Crohn’s Disease Activity Index form (PB393)

Use this form to calculate Crohn’s Disease Activity Index (CDAI).

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can upload this form in Online PBS Authorities (OPA) system.

Download and complete the [adult Crohn's Disease Activity Index form](/sites/default/files/2026-02/pb393-2602en-f.pdf "PDF document").
