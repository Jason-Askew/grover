For Centrelink payments and services, you can call our [multilingual phone service](/phone-us#languages).

Call the [Centrelink Indigenous Call Centre](/centrelink-indigenous-call-centre?context=64107) to speak to us about Centrelink payments and services for free.

Enhanced Income Management helps you budget your payments from us. It also helps you meet essential household needs and expenses, like food, rent, bills and education.

You can access your enhanced Income Management account with a SmartCard.

Watch this video to learn what enhanced Income Management is.

[Transcript](/sites/default/files/2025-04/what-is-enhanced-income-management-video-transcript.docx "Word document") | [Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3Q4ZkBHptDs)
