# How to get help

You can get help to manage your Child Support online account.

## on this page

-   [Online guides](#a1)
-   [Contact us](#a2)
-   [myGov](#a3)

## Online guides

We have [Child Support online guides](/child-support-online-help-guides) to help you with online accounts and mobile apps.

They explain each step you need to do to complete a task. You can find out how to:

-   get started
-   update your details
-   claim and manage your payments.

## Contact us

If you need help after reading our online guides you can call our [Child support enquiry line](/phone-us#csgeneralenquiries).

## myGov

If you need help with myGov, you can:

-   go to the [myGov help pages](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help) on the myGov website
-   ask the [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) digital assistant
-   read our [myGov online guides](/mygov-online-help-guides)
-   call the [Online services support hotline](/phone-us#mygovhelp).
