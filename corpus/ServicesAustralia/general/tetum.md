# Lia-Tetun / Tetum

We provide information in your language to help you learn more about our payments and services.

Ami fó informasaun iha Ita-nia lian hodi ajuda Ita aprende tan konaba ami-nia pagamentu no servisu sira.

Se Ita uza teknolojia hodi fó asisténsia no labele asesa ami-nia informasaun ne’ebé tradús ona, Ita bele kontakta ami [iha Ita-nia lian](/extra-help-when-calling-us).
