# Application for Approved Care Organisation status form (FA083)

Use this form if your organisation would like to apply for Approved Care Organisation status, for the purpose of claiming Family Tax Benefit for children in your care.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Application for approved care organisation status form](/sites/default/files/2025-04/fa083-2212en-f.pdf "PDF document").
