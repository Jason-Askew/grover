# What you can do

You can claim, manage your payments, details and money, give us documents and report your income online.

## on this page

-   [Claim payments](#a1)
-   [Manage documents](#a2)
-   [Report employment income](#a3)
-   [Manage money](#a4)
-   [View payments and overpayments](#a5)

You can also use your Centrelink online account to [view and update your details](/what-details-you-can-view-and-update-using-your-centrelink-online-account?context=22621).

## Claim payments

You can:

-   [claim a payment](/payments-you-can-claim-with-centrelink-online-account?context=22621)
-   apply for an [advance payment](/advance-payment)
-   track your claim
-   [request to transfer carer payments for a child to an adult](/centrelink-online-account-help-request-to-transfer-carer-payments-for-child-to-adult).

You can ask [someone to deal with us on your behalf](/someone-to-act-for-you-with-medicare-centrelink-aged-care-or-child-support) and authorise them to get payments for you.

## Manage documents

You can:

-   request replacement [concession and health care cards](/concession-and-health-care-cards)
-   view your payment and transaction history
-   [submit documents](/upload-your-centrelink-documents-online)
-   [request documents](/centrelink-online-account-help-request-document)
-   consent or decline to [release your Centrelink information](/personal-information-releases) to a third party organisation.

## Report employment income

You can:

-   [report your income](/employment-income-reporting) or confirm you’re [meeting your requirements](/mutual-obligation-requirements)
-   get [reporting reminders](/employment-income-reporting).

## Manage money

You can:

-   manage your [BasicsCard](/income-management-and-basicscard)
-   manage your [Centrepay](/centrepay) or tax deductions
-   view your [Working Credit](/working-credit) and [Work Bonus](/work-bonus) balance
-   view and manage your [Income Bank](/income-bank) details
-   view and manage the [money you owe](/how-to-repay-money-you-owe-to-centrelink).

## View payments and overpayments

You can view details about your payment under **My payments** if you get one of the following:

-   Youth Allowance
-   Age Pension
-   Disability Support Pension
-   Carer Payment.

You can view:

-   how we work out your payment
-   how much we’ll pay you.

You can view details about any overpayments you have in [**Money you owe**](/centrelink-online-account-help-manage-money-you-owe), including:

-   explanations about your overpayments
-   tips to prevent getting a debt.
