# Eating disorder treatment and management plans

Rules for billing Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS) eating disorder treatment and management plans and how to apply them.

Read the relevant item descriptions, fact sheets and explanatory notes on the [MBS Online](http://www.mbsonline.gov.au/internet/mbsonline/publishing.nsf/Content/Home) website.

You can bill and claim the following Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS) items for eating disorder plans.

These services can be performed and billed through telehealth. Read [Telehealth MBS items](/telehealth-billing-codes-for-mbs-items?context=20) to learn more.

## Consultation items and treatment and management plan items

Some eating disorder treatment and management plan items can’t be claimed with some other consultation items on the same day for the same patient.

The [MBS item and explanatory notes](https://www.mbsonline.gov.au/) on the MBS Online website provide more information about what consultation items you can’t claim with an eating disorder treatment and management plan item.
