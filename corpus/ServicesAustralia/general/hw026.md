# Application for late lodgement of a Simplified Billing claim for assigned Medicare benefits form (HW026)

Use this form to apply for late lodgement of a Simplified Billing claim for assigned Medicare benefits if you are a health professional, billing agent or private health insurer.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Application for late lodgement of a Simplified Billing claim for assigned Medicare benefits form](/sites/default/files/2025-09/hw026-2508en-f.pdf "PDF document").
