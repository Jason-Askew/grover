For Centrelink payments and services, you can call our [multilingual phone service](/phone-us#languages).

Call the [Centrelink Indigenous Call Centre](/centrelink-indigenous-call-centre?context=64107) to speak to us about Centrelink payments and services for free.

You had until 2 December 2025 to make a claim for the following Local Government Areas (LGAs):

-   Bellingen
-   Central Coast
-   Coffs Harbour
-   Dungog
-   Kempsey
-   Lake Macquarie
-   Maitland
-   Mid-Coast
-   Nambucca Valley
-   Newcastle
-   Port Macquarie-Hastings
-   Port Stephens.

Late claims will only be accepted in exceptional circumstances. To claim, call the [Emergency information line.](/phone-us?context=64107#emergency)

To find more emergency information about your LGA, you can enter your address into the New South Wales Government’s [find your local council tool](https://www.olg.nsw.gov.au/public/find-my-council/).
