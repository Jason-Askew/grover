# Help me find a form for health professionals

We can help you find a form based on your role and tasks.

Select one or more of the options that apply to you.

When you post or fax a form to us you need to sign the manual form. We can't accept digital signatures with paper forms.

When you send a form to us through HPOS, we can accept digital signatures attached to electronic forms.
