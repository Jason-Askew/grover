# Eligible equipment

You can only get the Essential Medical Equipment Payment (EMEP) if you use certain equipment in your home.

This equipment can be any of the following:

-   home dialysis machine
-   home ventilator
-   home respirator
-   home parenteral or enteral feeding device
-   oxygen concentrator
-   heart pump
-   suction pump.

You can also get the payment if you use any of the following:

-   infant apnoea monitor, medically prescribed
-   nebuliser, used daily
-   positive airways pressure device
-   phototherapy equipment
-   air bed vibrator
-   electric wheelchair
-   insulin pump.

You might know the equipment by a [different name](/what-are-alternative-names-for-essential-medical-equipment?context=21831).

You can’t get this payment for any other equipment. But you may get it for heating or cooling used for medical needs.

Equipment with non-rechargeable batteries is not eligible.

You can’t get this payment for a mobility or electric scooter. A mobility scooter is not essential medical equipment for EMEP and is not eligible.
