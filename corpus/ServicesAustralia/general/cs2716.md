# Child over 18 in full time secondary education form (CS2716)

Use this form to extend child support to the end of the school year before your child turns 18.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Child over 18 in full time secondary education form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/cs2716-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").

The easiest way to extend child support is online. Only use this form if you can’t apply online.

If your Child Support online account is linked to myGov, sign in now to apply to extend your child support.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.csaonline.gov.au/csos_site/csos-base/basePage.jsf?wec-appid=cssr_app&wec-locale=en_US)

If you have a child support agreement, you can’t apply to extend your child support online. You must use the form.

Use our guide to help you use your online account to [extend child support for your child who’s turning 18](/child-support-online-account-help-extend-child-support-for-child-turning-18).
