# How much you can get

The Stillborn Baby Payment is currently $4,326.57.

This payment is:

-   a one-off lump sum
-   tax free.

It can be part of [Income Management](/income-management-and-basicscard).

If you have a multiple birth, you’ll get either:

-   $4,326.57 for each stillborn baby
-   [Parental Leave Pay](/parental-leave-pay) for one and Stillborn Baby Payment of $4,326.57 for others.

The Stillborn Baby Payment Income limit for you and your partner must be $77,177.00 or less for the 6 months from the day of the child’s delivery.
