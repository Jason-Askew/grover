# Systemic lupus erythematosus - anifrolumab - initial authority application form (PB365)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment with anifrolumab for systemic lupus erythematosus.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Download and complete the [systemic lupus erythematosus - anifrolumab - initial authority application form](/sites/default/files/2025-05/pb365-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").
