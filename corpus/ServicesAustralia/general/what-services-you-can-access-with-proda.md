# What services you can access

You can use your PRODA account to access a range of services for providers.

## on this page

-   [Health provider services](#a1)
-   [Centrelink provider services](#a2)
-   [Department of Veterans’ Affairs provider services](#a3)
-   [Education provider services](#a4)
-   [NDIS provider services](#a5)
-   [Other provider services](#a6)

## Health provider services

You can access a number of services using PRODA. These include:

-   Health Professional Online Services (HPOS)
-   Medicare Online
-   Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme Online (PBS Online)
-   Disability Medical Assessment Online service
-   Aged Care Provider Portal
-   Australian Immunisation Register (AIR)
-   Practice Incentives Program (PIP)
-   Workforce Incentive Programs - Practice Stream
-   DVA Webclaim
-   My Health Record National Provider Portal
-   National Cancer Screening Register.

## Centrelink provider services

Access the Centrelink [Business Hub](/business-hub) using PRODA.

## Department of Veterans’ Affairs provider services

Access the following services using [PRODA](https://connect.dva.gov.au/index.htm) on the Department of Veterans’ Affairs website:

-   [Transport Providers Portal (TBIS)](https://connect.dva.gov.au/)
-   [Veterans’ Home Care Provider (VHC) - Provider Portal](https://connect.dva.gov.au/)
-   [Ex-Service Organisation Portal](https://connect.dva.gov.au/)
-   [RAPTOR Portal](https://connect.dva.gov.au/)

## Education provider services

Access the following services using PRODA:

-   Child Care Subsidy (CCS) Provider Entry Portal
-   Tertiary Collection of Student Information (TCSI).

You can find out more about TCSI on the [TCSI Support website](https://www.tcsisupport.gov.au/).

## NDIS provider services

Access the NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission Provider Portal using PRODA.

You can find out more about the [NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission Provider Portal](https://www.ndiscommission.gov.au/) on the NDIS Commission website.

## Other provider services

Access the National Redress Scheme using PRODA. You can read more about the [scheme](https://www.nationalredress.gov.au/) on their website.

If you need help with PRODA, you can [contact us](/business-and-employer-contact-information#proda).
