# Medicare Claim form (MS014)

Use this form to claim Medicare benefits by mail or in person at a service centre. This can be for a paid or unpaid account.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Medicare Claim form](/sites/default/files/2023-09/ms014-2309en-f.pdf "PDF document").

We pay Medicare benefits directly into your nominated bank account. Please include your bank details on the form if we don't have them or if they need [updating](/how-you-update-your-name-date-birth-or-gender-for-medicare).

Did you know you can claim Medicare benefits online? It’s the quickest way to get your benefit. You don’t need to mail us anything or visit a service centre. Find out how to [submit your claim online](/medicare-claims#claimmedicare).
