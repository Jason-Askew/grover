# Update your personal details online

You can update your personal details using your online account.

You can view or update:

-   [address, accommodation and contact details](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-address-accommodation-and-contact-details)
-   [nominee arrangements](/centrelink-online-account-help-add-nominee)
-   parental income
-   [where your payments are sent](/centrelink-online-account-help-manage-where-your-payments-are-sent)
-   [study details](/centrelink-online-account-help-add-new-study-details)
-   [travel details](/payments-while-outside-australia#howtotellus).

You can [view, update or add information using your Centrelink online account](/what-details-you-can-view-and-update-using-your-centrelink-online-account?context=22621).

You should tell us [when you break up or separate](/breaking-up-with-your-partner) from your partner. Update your relationship status online before you update your contact details. This will make sure all of your personal information is protected.

You can also subscribe to electronic email and SMS messaging using your online account.
