# Language, Literacy and Numeracy Supplement

An extra payment each fortnight while you’re improving your skills in the Skills for Education and Employment program.

[

Who can get it

You’ll get this supplement if you’re in the Skills for Education and Employment program.


](/who-can-get-language-literacy-and-numeracy-supplement?context=22216)

[

How much you can get

You’ll get an extra $20.80 with your main payment every fortnight while you’re in the Skills for Education and Employment (SEE) program.


](/how-much-language-literacy-and-numeracy-supplement-you-can-get?context=22216)

What you must do
