# Solid tumours with confirmed NTRK gene fusion - larotrectinib - initial authority application form (PB333)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment with larotrectinib for solid tumours with confirmed neurotrophic tropomyosin receptor kinase (NTRK) gene fusion.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.
