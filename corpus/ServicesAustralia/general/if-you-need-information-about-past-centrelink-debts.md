# If you need information about past Centrelink debts

You have a right to access your Centrelink debt history and the information we use to make decisions about debts.

## on this page

-   [Statement of Debt](#statement)
-   [Other information about past debts](#otherinfo)

## Statement of Debt

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, sign in now to request a Statement of Debt.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

If you need help, use the [request a document](/centrelink-online-account-help-request-document) online guide.

Your statement will show your debt information for up to 5 years. It will include:

-   when we raised your debt
-   your debt repayment and transaction history
-   any adjustments and changes to your debt
-   your debt identification number.

You can request a Statement of Debt for any 5 year period going back to 1998. You can make more than one request.
