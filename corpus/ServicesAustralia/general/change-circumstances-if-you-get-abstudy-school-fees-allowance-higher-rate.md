# Change of circumstances

You need to tell us if your circumstances change when you get ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance higher rate.

## on this page

-   [What to tell us](#a1)
-   [How to tell us](#a2)

## What to tell us

You need to tell us if any of the following things happen:

-   you [change or correct your name](#changename)
-   your personal and contact details change
-   your bank details change
-   you [leave the country](/payments-while-outside-australia), temporarily or to live somewhere else
-   your child changes school or lives with someone else.

You need to tell us about a change in your circumstances within 14 days. If you don’t, we may pay you too much. This means you’ll have [debt](/centrelink-debts-and-overpayments) you need to pay back.

## How to tell us

For changes to your circumstances you can tell us by calling the [ABSTUDY line](#toolbar-phone).

### Changing or correcting your name

It’s important to tell us if you [change your legal name](/how-to-update-your-name-with-centrelink).
