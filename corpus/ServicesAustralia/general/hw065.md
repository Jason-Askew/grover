# Application for remote area exemption for R-type diagnostic imaging services for a medical practitioner form (HW065)

Use this form to apply for Remote Area Exemption for R Type Diagnostic Imaging Services for a medical practitioner.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Application for remote area exemption for R-type diagnostic imaging services for a medical practitioner form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/hw065-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").
