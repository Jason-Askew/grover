# Centrelink Business Online Services

Your business can use our online services to help us deliver some Centrelink payments and services.

Business Hub replaces Centrelink Business Online Services

[Business Hub](/business-hub) is replacing Centrelink Business Online Services (CBOS). If you’re eligible, start using it now.

## on this page

-   [When to use CBOS](#a1)
-   [What you can do in CBOS](#a2)
-   [How to register](#a3)
-   [Who can help](#a4)

## When to use CBOS

You should use CBOS if:

-   you aren’t eligible to use Business Hub
-   Business Hub doesn’t include the payments and services you need to manage.

## What you can do in CBOS

You can use CBOS to:

-   manage [Centrepay](/centrepay-for-businesses) deductions
-   manage [Income Management](/income-management-organisations) deductions
-   verify customer rent details with us using the [Electronic Verification of Rent](/electronic-verification-rent-for-businesses) service
-   confirm a customer’s entitlement to a rebate, service, fee reduction or concession using the [Centrelink Confirmation eService](/centrelink-confirmation-eservices-cces-for-businesses)
-   upload documents and files for [Tasmanian Freight Equalisation Scheme (TFES)](/tasmanian-freight-equalisation-scheme) claims.

If you are an Australian Disability Enterprises (ADE) employer, you can report employee earnings directly to us using the [Employer Reporting service](/employer-reporting-service).

Information about [supported employment](https://www.dss.gov.au/disability-employment-programs/supported-employment) for people with disability is on the Department of Social Services website.

[Log in to CBOS](https://business.centrelink.gov.au/LoginServices/source/VANguard/ValidateBAN.jsp?finalURL=http%3A%2F%2Fbusiness.humanservices.gwy%2FLoginServices%2FAuthenticate.do)

## How to register

You can register for the following services:

-   [Centrepay for businesses](/centrepay-for-businesses)
-   [Centrelink Confirmation eServices](/centrelink-confirmation-eservices-cces-for-businesses)
-   [Electronic Verification of Rent](/electronic-verification-rent-for-businesses)
-   [Income Management](/income-management-organisations).

Once approved for one of these, you can use the [Business Online Services User Details form](/sa445) to manage user access.

## Who can help

If you need help, contact us through the [Business and employer contact information](/business-and-employer-contact-information) for the service you want to use.
