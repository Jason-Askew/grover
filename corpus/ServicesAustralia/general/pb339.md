# Growth hormone paediatric - somatrogon - initial grandfather authority application form (PB339)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment with somatrogon for paediatric patients with short stature with slow growth (SSSD) or short stature with biochemical growth hormone deficiency (BGHD) under the section 100 Growth Hormone Program.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).
