# Healthcare Identifiers Service application to deactivate or retire a contracted service provider organisation form (HW048)

Use this form to deactivate or retire a Contracted Service Provider (CSP) organisation record within the Healthcare Identifier (HI) Service.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Healthcare Identifiers Service application to deactivate or retire a contracted service provider organisation form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/hw048-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").
