# How to manage your payment

What you need to do and the options you have to manage your payment.

You can read more about:

-   changes to your circumstances.
-   someone to act on your behalf.

## View and print your CAPS payment history online

You can view and print your payment history for the last 5 years using your Medicare online account through myGov.

### Steps to view and print your CAPS payment history online

Follow these steps if you’re using your Medicare online account through myGov:

1.  Sign in to myGov.
2.  Select **Medicare**.
3.  Select **History and statements**.
4.  Select **CAPS Payment history**.
5.  Select **Download history (PDF)** to print history.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au/moasso/sps/oidc/rp/moa/kickoff/mygov?target_link_uri=%2Fmoaonline%2F%23%2Fextlnk%2Fcaps)

You can also use the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app).
