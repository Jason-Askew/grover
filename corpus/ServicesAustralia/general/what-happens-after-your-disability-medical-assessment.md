# What happens after your assessment

We’ll write to you to let you know the outcome of your claim or medical review.

If you want a copy of your report, call our [Disability, Sickness and Carers line](#toolbar-phone). We’ll let you know if you need to make a formal [Freedom of Information](/freedom-information) request.

Sonic HealthPlus aren’t able to provide a copy of your Disability Medical Assessment (DMA) report to you, this can only be provided by us.

## If you have questions

You can [call our Disability, Sickness and Carers line](#toolbar-phone) if you have questions about your assessment.

Contact numbers available on this page.

### Disability, sickness and carers line

Use this line if you need help with your Disability Support Pension, Special Disability Trusts, Mobility Allowance, Carer Payment, Carer Allowance or Ex-Carer Allowance (Child) Health Care Card, Health Care Card or Pensioner Concession Card.

[132 717](tel:132717)

Monday to Friday 8 am to 5 pm

There are other ways you may want to [contact us](/contact-us).
