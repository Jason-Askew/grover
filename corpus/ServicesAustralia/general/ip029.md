# Practice Incentives Program Indigenous Health Incentive patient withdrawal of consent form (IP029)

Use this form to withdraw your consent to participate in the Practice Incentives Program (PIP) Indigenous Health Incentive (IHI).

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Practice Incentives Program Indigenous Health Incentive patient withdrawal of consent form](/sites/default/files/2024-10/ip029-2410en-f.pdf "PDF document").

This form needs to be completed and signed by the patient withdrawing their consent from the PIP IHI.
