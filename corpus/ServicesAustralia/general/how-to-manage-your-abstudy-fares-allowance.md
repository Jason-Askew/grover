# How to manage your allowance

Your options and obligations for ABSTUDY Fares Allowance.

When you get the allowance you need to let us know if:

-   you need to [change or cancel your travel](/changing-travel-weve-arranged-under-abstudy-fares-allowance?context=22701)
-   you [miss travel](/penalties-for-travel-youve-missed-under-abstudy-fares-allowance?context=22701) we’ve booked for you
-   your [circumstances change](/change-circumstances-when-you-get-abstudy-fares-allowance?context=22701).

There are also [other responsibilities](/your-responsibilities-when-you-get-abstudy-fares-allowance?context=22701) you need to meet. If your parents or guardians travel with you they have [responsibilities](/parent-or-guardian-and-supervisor-responsibilities-when-you-get-abstudy-fares-allowance?context=22701) too.

Changes to travel we've arranged
