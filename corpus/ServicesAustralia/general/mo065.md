# COVID-19 Vaccine Claims Scheme cost calculation form (MO065)

Use this form to outline losses or expenses if you’re making a claim made under the COVID-19 Vaccine Claims Scheme.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [COVID-19 Vaccine Claims Scheme expenses form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/mo065-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Before you complete this form, you must check:

-   if [you or the person you’re claiming for are eligible under the scheme](/who-can-get-support-under-covid-19-vaccine-claims-scheme)
-   what proof you need.

Find out more about the [COVID-19 Vaccine Claims Scheme](/covid-19-vaccine-claims-scheme).
