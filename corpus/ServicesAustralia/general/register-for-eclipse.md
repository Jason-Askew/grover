# Register for ECLIPSE

We have streamlined the way patients pay their bills and claim benefits from Medicare and their private health insurer.

It offers a secure connection between us and:

-   health professionals
-   public and private hospitals
-   billing agents
-   private health insurers
-   the Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA).

It includes direct communication in the one transaction between health professionals, us and private health insurers.

Information about registering for Medicare Online and Electronic Claim Lodgement and Information Processing Service Environment (ECLIPSE) to lodge electronic claims.

1.  Check with your software developer that your operating system and software is compatible.
2.  Register for online claiming by receiving a minor ID from your software developer.
3.  Register for Provider Digital Access (PRODA)  individual and organisation accounts.
4.  Submit online claiming provider agreement form.
5.  Link your PRODA organisation account with a service in PRODA called Medicare Online/ECLIPSE/DVA/AIR.
