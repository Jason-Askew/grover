# Application for recognition as a specialist or consultant physician form (HW077)

Use this form to apply for recognition as a specialist or consultant physician so you can provide services to private patients that attract Medicare Benefits at the specialist or consultant physician rates.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Application for recognition as a specialist or consultant physician form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/hw077-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Use this form if you’re a registered medical practitioner and either:

-   an Australian citizen
-   a permanent resident
-   a temporary resident
-   a New Zealand citizen.

You can also apply for recognition as a specialist or consultant physician using Health Professional Online Services (HPOS).

When applying online, you’ll need a [PRODA](/provider-digital-access-proda) account to use [HPOS](/hpos).
