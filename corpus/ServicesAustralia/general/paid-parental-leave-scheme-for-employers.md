This information about Paid Parental Leave is for employers. If you’re an employee, you can find out more about [Parental Leave Pay](/parental-leave-pay).

The Paid Parental Leave scheme includes Parental Leave Pay. It’s a payment that helps eligible working parents take time off to care for a newborn or recently adopted child.

If you’re an employer looking for more information, we have [resources for the scheme](/resources-for-paid-parental-leave-scheme?context=23121) including the Employer Toolkit available to help you.
