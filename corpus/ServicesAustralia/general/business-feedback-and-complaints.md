# Business feedback and complaints

As a business you can give a compliment. You can also make a complaint or offer a suggestion to help us improve our service.

## on this page

-   [Centrelink and Medicare](#centrelinkmedicare)
-   [Child Support](#childsupport)

This includes all of the following:

-   businesses
-   schools
-   educational organisations
-   financial institutions
-   peak bodies.

If you’re a [customer](/complaints-and-feedback) or [health professional](/health-professionals-feedback-and-complaints) you can send us your feedback too. Send your feedback or make a complaint using the specific options for you.

## Centrelink and Medicare

Use either of these online forms to give us feedback or make a complaint about Centrelink and Medicare:

-   [Centrelink Feedback](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/apps/custonline_feedback/) online form
-   [Medicare Feedback](https://feedback.humanservices.gov.au/mcasite_feedback/feedback/feedbackBasePage.jsf?wec-appid=feedback&wec-locale=en) online form.

Once you’ve selected the type of feedback you’d like to give, select **Third Party Organisation** at Step 3. Then enter your business, school or organisation details under **Organisation name**.

Or call the [complaints and feedback](/business-and-employer-contact-information?context=60075) line.

## Child Support

Employers and financial institutions can send us feedback about Child Support. To do this you can either:

-   use your [Child Support Business Online Service](https://services.csa.gov.au/) - once you sign in, select **Messages** then **Feedback**
-   call the [feedback and complaints](/business-and-employer-contact-information?context=60075) line.
