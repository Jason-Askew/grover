# How much you can get

You’ll get the allowance with your regular payment each fortnight.

Your situation

Fortnightly payment amount

Single

$7.00

Part of a couple

$3.50 each

You don’t pay tax on Pharmaceutical Allowance, even if you get it as an advance payment.
