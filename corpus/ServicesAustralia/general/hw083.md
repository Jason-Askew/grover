# Request to revoke or reissue a NASH PKI certificate form (HW083)

Use this form to revoke or reissue your current National Authentication Service for Health (NASH) Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) Certificate.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Request to revoke or reissue a NASH PKI certificate form](/sites/default/files/2025-07/hw083-2507en-f.pdf "PDF document").

You can use this form to **request to revoke** or **reissue** a NASH PKI Organisation Certificate.

You can use this form to **reissue** your certificate if both of the following apply:

-   your organisation is a network organisation
-   you cannot [request a new certificate](/apply-for-national-authentication-service-for-health-nash) in [HPOS](/hpos).
