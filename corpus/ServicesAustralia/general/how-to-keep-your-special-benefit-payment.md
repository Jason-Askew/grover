# How to keep your payment

Find out what you need to do to keep getting Special Benefit.

A [change in circumstances](/change-circumstances-when-you-get-special-benefit?context=22281) can affect your payment. You need to tell us about changes for you and your partner within 14 days.

If you’re working, to make sure we can pay you the right amount, you’ll need to [report your employment income](/employment-income-reporting?context=22281).

If you get Special Benefit and can’t work or study for a short time, you may need to give us a [medical certificate](/getting-medical-certificate-for-special-benefit?context=22281).

You need to tell us if you [travel outside Australia](/travel-outside-australia-rules-for-special-benefit?context=22281). Depending on the reason for your travel, your Special Benefit payment may stop when you leave.
