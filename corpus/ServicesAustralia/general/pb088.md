# Crohn’s disease adult - continuing authority application form (PB088)

Use this form to apply to continue PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine for adult patients with severe Crohn’s disease.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Biological medicine refers to:

-   adalimumab
-   infliximab
-   upadacitinib
-   ustekinumab
-   vedolizumab.

Please note: You don’t need to complete this form if you use the [Online PBS Authorities](/apply-for-pbs-authority?context=20#accordion1) system.

Download and complete the [Crohn’s disease adult - continuing authority application form](/sites/default/files/2025-11/pb088-2512en-f.pdf "PDF document").
