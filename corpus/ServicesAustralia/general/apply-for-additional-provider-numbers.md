# Apply for additional provider numbers

You can apply for an additional provider number online or by completing a form.

You need to apply for an additional provider number if you work:

-   in a new practice location
-   for a new service at the same location.

If you work in a new health profession or discipline, you’ll need to [apply for an initial Medicare provider number](/apply-for-initial-provider-and-prescriber-numbers?context=20).

Apply online

You can apply online for an additional Medicare provider number if you’re any of the following:

-   Ahpra-registered allied health professional with limited or general registration
-   non-medical health professional - nurse practitioner, midwife, dentist, dental practitioner or optometrist
-   [medical intern who trained in Australia or New Zealand](/medical-intern-application-for-provider-or-prescriber-number?context=20)
-   non-Ahpra registered allied health professional.

You can also apply online for an additional provider number in the same location if you’re a Medicare Urgent Care Clinic (UCC) providing UCC services.

If you’re a medical practitioner, you may be able to [apply for additional provider numbers online](/medical-practitioner-application-for-additional-provider-numbers?context=20).

If you’re eligible, you can apply for an additional provider number online through [Health Professional Online Services (HPOS)](/hpos?context=20).

When you get your additional provider number wait 2 business days before you submit claims with the new number.

To apply for an additional provider number:

1.  Log in to Provider Digital Access (PRODA) to use HPOS.
2.  Select **My details**, then **My provider numbers**.
3.  Select **Create a new provider location** and read the privacy note.
4.  Submit answers to required questions.

[Log in to PRODA](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au:5447/sso/sps/oidc/rp/HPOSWEB/kickoff/proda1)

Your bank details for the new location will be pending if someone you’ve nominated as a delegate creates a new location on your behalf.

You can manage your provider numbers, banking details, practice details and locations in HPOS.

Learn more about [provider eligibility](https://hpe.servicesaustralia.gov.au/provider-eligibility.html) on the Health Professional Education website.

If you can’t apply online

If you can’t apply online, you’ll need to use one of the following forms:

-   [Application for a Medicare provider number and, or prescriber number for a medical practitioner form](/hw019?context=20)
-   [Application for a Medicare provider number and, or prescriber number for allied health and non-medical health professionals form](/hw093?context=20)
-   [Application for a Medicare provider number and/or PBS prescriber number for a midwife or nurse practitioner form](/hw088?context=20).

Send the completed form and your other documents to [Medicare provider](/health-professionals-contact-information?context=20#hpos).
