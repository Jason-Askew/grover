# How to set it up

You can set up and use your Child Support online account through myGov.

You need to be an existing Child Support customer to link Child Support to myGov.

First, you need to go to the myGov website to [create a myGov account](https://my.gov.au/en/create-account/).

Then, link Child Support by following these steps.

1.  Sign in to your [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) account and select **View or link services**.
2.  Select the **Child Support** link button.
3.  Follow the prompts to link your Child Support online account to myGov.

You can read the [help pages on myGov](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help) to learn how to link Child Support to your myGov account or call the [Child support enquiry line](#toolbar-phone).

If you’re overseas or have limited internet access, you’ll need to call the [Child support enquiry line](#toolbar-phone). We’ll help you create a Child Support online account.

Contact numbers available on this page.
