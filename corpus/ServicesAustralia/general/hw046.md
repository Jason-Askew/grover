# HI Service - application to add, replace or remove a contracted service provider officer form (HW046)

Use this form to add, replace, or remove a Contracted Service Provider officer record within the Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [HI Service - application to add, replace or remove a contracted service provider officer form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/hw046-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").
