# Privacy

We respect your right to privacy.

We’ll treat any information you give us in line with our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).

We may refer you to a support service. They can only pass on information if you’ve agreed or if it’s required or authorised by law.

Read about how we manage your personal information and [your right to privacy](/your-right-to-privacy).
