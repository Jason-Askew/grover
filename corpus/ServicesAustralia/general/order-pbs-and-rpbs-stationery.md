# Order PBS and RPBS stationery

Use Health Professional Online Services (HPOS) to order Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) and Repatriation Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (RPBS) stationery.

To order PBS or RPBS stationery through HPOS, you must also have an [individual Provider Digital Access (PRODA) account linked to HPOS](/hpos?context=20).

How pharmacies and hospitals can order PBS stationery

To order stationery:

1.  Log in to PRODA to access your HPOS account.
2.  Select **My programs**.
3.  Select **PBS stationery online ordering**.

[Log in to PRODA](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au:5447/sso/sps/oidc/rp/HPOSWEB/kickoff/proda1)

In HPOS you can:

-   place a new stationery order
-   view order details
-   re-order a previous order once it is dispatched or complete.

Before you place the order, check your delivery details are correct. If they’re not, you can select a different registered practice location or enter a preferred mailing address.

Once you place an order, you’ll get a confirmation message and order number. Use your order number to [manage your PBS and RPBS stationery](/manage-your-pharmaceutical-benefits-scheme-pbs-and-repatriation-pharmaceutical-benefits-scheme-rpbs?context=20).

To make an order, you’ll need to link to HPOS using either your Pharmacy Approval Number or Hospital Provider Number. If you’re already linked for another service, you’ll need to link your Pharmacy Approval Number or Hospital Provider Number as an additional identifier.

If you’re having issues placing your online order, read [how to fix errors when ordering online](/manage-your-pharmaceutical-benefits-scheme-pbs-and-repatriation-pharmaceutical-benefits-scheme-rpbs?context=20#accordion2).

How prescribers can order PBS stationery

To order online:

1.  Log in to PRODA to access your HPOS account.
2.  Select **My programs**.
3.  Select **PBS stationery online ordering**.

[Log in to PRODA](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au:5447/sso/sps/oidc/rp/HPOSWEB/kickoff/proda1)

In HPOS you can:

-   place a new stationery order
-   view order details
-   re-order a previous order once it is dispatched or complete.

Before you order, check your delivery address fields are correct. If they’re not, you can select a different registered practice location or enter a preferred mailing address.

Once you place an order, you’ll get a confirmation message and order number. Use your [order number to manage your order](/manage-your-pharmaceutical-benefits-scheme-pbs-and-repatriation-pharmaceutical-benefits-scheme-rpbs?context=20).

If you have no access to a computer or the internet, please [contact us](/health-professionals-contact-information?context=20).
