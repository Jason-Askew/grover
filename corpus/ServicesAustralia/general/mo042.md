# Statutory declaration - Midwife Professional Indemnity Scheme - Online claim form (MO042)

Use this form to support a Midwife Professional Indemnity Scheme (MPIS) online claim application.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Statutory declaration - Midwife Professional Indemnity Scheme - Online claim form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/mo042-2501en-f.pdf "PDF document").

A person authorised to represent your organisation must complete and sign this form.

If the statutory declaration is not provided with each new online claim application a Commonwealth Contribution Indemnity Scheme payment cannot be made.
