# Which countries have agreements with us

Australia has agreements with a number of countries to share responsibility for social security coverage.

Find information about agreements [in your language](/information-your-language).

Under these agreements you may be able to claim a:

-   Centrelink payment if you live in Australia but are visiting an agreement country
-   Centrelink payment if you live in an agreement country
-   Centrelink payment if you live in an agreement country but are visiting Australia
-   payment from an agreement country if you’re in Australia
-   payment from an agreement country if you don’t meet the residence or social insurance rules and you’ve lived in Australia.
