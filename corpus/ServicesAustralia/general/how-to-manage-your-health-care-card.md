# How to manage your card

Your options and obligations to hold a Health Care Card.

[

Replace a lost or stolen card

If your Pensioner Concession Card or Health Care Card is lost, stolen or ruined, we can post you a new card if you need one.


](/replace-lost-or-stolen-pensioner-concession-card-or-health-care-card?context=21981)

[

Digital card

Have your concession or health care card with you everywhere you take your smart mobile device.


](/digital-card?context=21981)

[

Change of circumstances

You need to tell us about any changes to your circumstances when you hold a Health Care Card.


](/change-circumstances-if-you-hold-health-care-card?context=21981)

[

Travel outside Australia

The travel rules of your main payment affect your Health Care Card when you travel outside Australia.


](/travel-outside-australia-rules-for-health-care-card?context=21981)
