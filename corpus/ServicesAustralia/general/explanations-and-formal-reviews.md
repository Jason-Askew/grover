## If you want more information or disagree with a decision

You can ask us to explain our decision about your Centrelink payments, Medicare debt or child support. You can also ask for a review of a decision by doing any of the following:

-   [writing to us](/write-to-us?context=64107)
-   [calling us](/phone-us?context=64107) on your regular payment line
-   visiting us at a [service centre](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/).

Read more about:

-   [explanations and formal reviews of a Centrelink decision](/explanations-and-formal-reviews-centrelink-decision?context=64107)
-   [objections to child support decisions](/objections-to-child-support-decisions?context=64107)
-   [reviews and appeals about your Medicare debts or overpayments](/reviews-and-appeals-about-your-medicare-debts-or-overpayments?context=64107).

## If you have feedback or complaints

You can give us feedback or make a complaint at any time.

Read more about [feedback and complaints](/complaints-and-feedback?context=64107).

## If you’re claiming compensation from us

You may be able to claim compensation from us if you believe we’ve made a mistake that caused you either:

-   financial loss
-   injury
-   other harm.

Read more about [claiming compensation from us](/claim-compensation-from-us?context=64107).
