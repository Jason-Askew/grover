# Add your immunisation history statements to a digital wallet

How to add your COVID-19 and influenza (flu) immunisation history statement to your device for easy storage, and how to update your proof of vaccinations.

## on this page

-   [If you’re eligible for Medicare](#a1)
-   [If you’re not eligible for Medicare](#a2)
-   [If you need to update your proof in your digital wallet](#updateproofdigitalwallet)

You can add your COVID-19 and influenza (flu) immunisation history statement to your digital wallet. This is either your Apple Wallet or Google Wallet.

How you add your statement to your digital wallet depends on whether you’re eligible for Medicare or not.

If you’ve had any additional vaccines remember to update your proof in your digital wallet.

## If you’re eligible for Medicare

You can add your proof of vaccinations to your Apple Wallet or Google Wallet using either:

-   the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app)
-   your Medicare online account through myGov using a browser on your mobile phone or tablet.

If you’re using an iOS device, your software must be iOS 9.0 or later and can use either Safari or Chrome browsers. If you’re using an Android device, your software must be 5.0 and up and needs to use the Chrome browser.

### COVID-19 and influenza (flu) immunisation history statement

If you’re using the myGov app:

1.  Select **Services**, then **Medicare**.
2.  Select **Proof of vaccinations**.
3.  Select your **name**, then **View COVID-19 and influenza (flu) statement**.
4.  Select either **Add to Apple Wallet** or **Add to Google Wallet**.

If your Medicare online account is linked to myGov:

1.  Sign in to myGov using the mobile app or internet browser on your mobile phone or tablet.
2.  Select **Medicare** then **View proof** in Proof of vaccinations.
3.  Select **View history** then select your **name**.
4.  Select either **Add to Apple Wallet** or **Add to Google Wallet**.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au/moasso/sps/oidc/rp/moa/kickoff/mygov?target_link_uri=%2Fmoaonline)

If you don’t have a myGov account or a Medicare online account you’ll need to [create them](/create-online-account).

## If you’re not eligible for Medicare

You can add your COVID-19 and influenza (flu) immunisation history statement to your digital wallet using the Individual Healthcare Identifiers service (IHI service) through myGov.

If you’re using the IHI service through myGov:

1.  Sign in to your myGov account using a browser on your device.
2.  Select **Individual Healthcare Identifiers service**.
3.  Select **View proof** in Proof of vaccinations.
4.  Select **View history** in Immunisation history.
5.  Select either **Add to Apple Wallet** or **Add to Google Wallet**.

[Sign in to myGov](https://my.gov.au/)

## If you need to update your proof in your digital wallet

If you’ve had additional vaccines, you’ll need to update the statement saved in your digital wallet.

To do this, it is recommended to delete the old statement from your wallet first. Then, follow the steps for adding a statement to your wallet.
