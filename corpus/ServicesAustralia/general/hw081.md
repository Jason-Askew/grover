# Private health insurer registrations and updates for ECLIPSE form (HW081)

Use this form to register and update private health insurer details for the ECLIPSE and Manual claiming.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Private health insurer registrations and updates for ECLIPSE form](/sites/default/files/2024-09/hw081-2409en-f.pdf "PDF document").

ECLIPSE is the Electronic Claim Lodgement and Information Processing Service Environment.
