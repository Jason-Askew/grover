# Medicare Two-way claim form (MS001)

Use this form together with a Medicare claim form to submit your in-hospital and ancillary claims with Medicare and your private health fund.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Medicare Two-way claim form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/ms001-2507en-f.pdf "PDF document").

This form can also be used to claim gap benefits from your private health fund for in-hospital and ancillary services.

If your myGov account is linked to Medicare, you can submit your claim online.

To do this:

1.  Complete a [Medicare Two-way claim form](/ms001).
2.  Sign in to myGov.
3.  Attach an image of your original accounts and receipts if paid.
4.  Attach an image of the completed Medicare Two-way form.
5.  Submit your claim.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au/moasso/sps/oidc/rp/moa/kickoff/mygov?target_link_uri=%2Fmoaonline)

You can also use the [myGov app](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app).
