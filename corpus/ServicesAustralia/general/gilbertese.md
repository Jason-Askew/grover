# Te taetae inanon te taetae / Gilbertese

We provide information in your language to help you learn more about our payments and services.

A rairaki rongorongo n oin am taetae bwa ko aonga ni matata n aron ara bwakamwane ao ara tieweti nako.

Ngkana ko kabonganai ara assistive technology (ara burokuraem) ao e aki kona n reke rongorongo aika rairaki, ko kona n reitaki ma ngaira [n oin am taetae](/extra-help-when-calling-us).

If you are using assistive technology and cannot access our translated information, you can contact us [in your language](/extra-help-when-calling-us).
