# How to download and set up the app

You can download and set up the Express Plus Child Support mobile app on your mobile phone or tablet.

You need to have a Child Support online account and myGov account to use the app.

If you don’t, you’ll need to [set them up](/how-to-set-up-your-child-support-online-account).

You’ll then need to link your Child Support online account to myGov. You can use the [myGov online help guides](/mygov-online-help-guides).

[

Download the app

When you’ve set up your Child Support online account, you can download and use the Express Plus Child Support mobile app.
