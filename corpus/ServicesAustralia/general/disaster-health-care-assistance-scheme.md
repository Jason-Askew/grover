# Disaster Health Care Assistance Scheme

Help with out of pocket health costs you have from an act of terrorism or natural disaster the scheme covers.

-   for specific international act of terrorism and natural disaster events
-   covers health care provided in Australia you need directly due to the event.
    

[

Who can get it

If you’re an Australian resident, you may be able to get help with reasonable health related out of pocket costs.


](/who-can-get-disaster-health-care-assistance?context=22486)

[

What it covers

Under the scheme, we can help you with reasonable out of pocket costs for health care you get in Australia.

How to claim
