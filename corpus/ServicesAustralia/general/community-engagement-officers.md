# Community Engagement Officers

We can help you get information about payments and services if you’re having a difficult time due to a vulnerability or complex issue.

## on this page

-   [How we can help](#a1)
-   [Where to find us](#a2)

Community Engagement Officers are here to help you if you’re having trouble accessing our services. We can help you access information, payments and services.

## How we can help

We work with community organisations to help you access the services you need.

We can help you to:

-   get access to payments and services
-   connect with other support services you need.

## Where to find us

You can find us working with community organisations in places such as:

-   mental health facilities
-   general crisis and support services
-   specialist accommodation services
-   youth services
-   drug and alcohol services
-   family and domestic violence services
-   organised meeting places.

If you need support now, our [social workers](/social-work-services) can help you. They can help with information, support and short term counselling.
