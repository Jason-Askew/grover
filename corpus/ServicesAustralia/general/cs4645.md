# Statement of financial details for debt repayment form (CS4645)

Use this form to provide a statement of your financial details so that we can work out a payment arrangement for you to pay your overdue child support.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Statement of financial details for debt repayment form](/sites/default/files/2025-05/cs4645-2505en-f.pdf "PDF document").

It is important that you provide full details of your income, expenses, assets and liabilities to help us decide the most appropriate debt repayment amount.
