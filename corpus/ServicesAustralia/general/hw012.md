# HI Service - My Health Record system - application to register a contracted service provider organisation record form (HW012)

Use this form to register a Contracted Service Provider with the Healthcare Identifiers Service or the My Health Record system.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [HI Service - My Health Record system - application to register a contracted service provider organisation record form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/hw012-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").

You can also request a National Authentication Service for Health (NASH) Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) Certificate for your organisation.

You must read and agree to the terms and conditions of use when applying for a NASH PKI certificate. These are on pages 10 and 11 of this form.
