# How to get help

Get help using the Express Plus Child Support mobile app.

## on this page

-   [Reset your PIN](#a1)
-   [Forgotten myGov password or username](#a2)

## Reset your PIN

If you’ve forgotten your myGov PIN, select **Forgot PIN** on the **Sign in with myGov** screen.You’ll need to enter your myGov password. Then, enter the security code or answer your secret question. Then, create a new myGov PIN and sign in.

Don’t give your PIN to anyone. Keep it in a safe place.

## Forgotten myGov password or username

If you’ve forgotten your myGov password or username, you can reset them at the [myGov website](https://my.gov.au/).
