# Medicare Easyclaim enquiry form (HW059)

Use this form where a claimant has not received their Medicare benefit that was claimed via Medicare Easyclaim at that practice.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Medicare Easyclaim enquiry form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/hw059-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").

This form is to be completed by health professionals.
