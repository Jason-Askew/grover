# Verification of Terminal Illness form (SA495)

Use this form to verify your patient’s terminal illness when they’re applying for Disability Support Pension (DSP).

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Verification of Terminal Illness form](/sites/default/files/2025-02/sa495-2502en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Only complete it if you haven’t completed the verification section in the Claim for DSP for a Terminal Illness form.

For DSP, a terminal illness means the average life expectancy for a person with this condition is less than 2 years.
