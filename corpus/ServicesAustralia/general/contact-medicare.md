## Contact us

You can either [call us](#toolbar-phone) or [write to us](/write-to-us?context=64107#medicare) for help. You can't enquire for a person over 14 years of age unless you have their consent.

We can also help if you don't speak English or you're deaf, have hearing loss or speech disability. Read more about [extra help when calling us](/extra-help-when-calling-us?context=64107).

## Give us your feedback

We want to hear about your experience using our services. We’ll use your feedback to improve our products and services.

Read more about [complaints and feedback](/complaints-and-feedback?context=64107).
