# MyMedicare Incentives Review of decision form (IP034)

Use this form if you disagree with a program decision made under MyMedicare Incentives.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [MyMedicare Incentives Review of decision form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/ip034-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").

You must upload the form and supporting documents through HPOS messages within 28 days of the decision.
