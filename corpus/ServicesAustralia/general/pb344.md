# Paroxysmal nocturnal haemoglobinuria - iptacopan or pegcetacoplan continuing or returning authority application form (PB344)

Use this form to apply to continue or return to PBS-subsidised treatment with iptacopan or pegcetacoplan for paroxysmal nocturnal haemoglobinuria.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.
