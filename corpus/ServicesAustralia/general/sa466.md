# Claim for Disability Support Pension form (SA466)

Use this form to claim for Disability Support Pension.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Claim for Disability Support Pension form](/sites/default/files/2025-10/sa466-2511en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Read about [claiming Disability Support Pension](/how-to-claim-disability-support-pension) to ensure this is the right form for you.

You will also need these forms:

-   [Income and Assets form](/sa369)
-   [Consent to disclose medical information form](/sa472)
-   [DSP Medical Evidence Requirements Checklist](/sa473).
