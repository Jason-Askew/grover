# Become a PBS approved supplier

Pharmacists, medical practitioners and hospital authorities can apply for online claiming for the Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS).

## on this page

-   [Pharmacists](#a1)
-   [Medical practitioners](#a2)
-   [Hospital authorities](#a3)

To apply for online claiming, complete and submit the [Notification of Australian Business Number and reference identification for tax treatment purposes and recipient created tax invoice agreement form (HW055)](/hw055?context=20).

Use this form for each premises and agree to the terms and conditions to register for online claiming for Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS).

## Pharmacists

You’ll find information for pharmacists and those who want to get a new pharmacy, relocate a pharmacy or manage a pharmacy’s arrangements. Read more about [PBS pharmacists](https://www.health.gov.au/our-work/pbs-approved-suppliers/pharmacists) on the Department of Health, Disability and Ageing website.

## Medical practitioners

You’ll find information for medical practitioners on becoming an approved supplier of PBS-subsidised medicines in an area that does not have a pharmacy. Read more about becoming an [approved PBS supplier for medical practitioners](https://www.health.gov.au/our-work/pbs-approved-suppliers/medical-practitioners) on the Department of Health, Disability and Ageing website.

## Hospital authorities

You’ll find information for public and private hospitals on how to become an approved supplier of PBS-subsidised medicines or highly specialised drugs. Read more about becoming an [approved PBS supplier for hospital authorities](https://www.health.gov.au/our-work/pbs-approved-suppliers/hospital-authorities) on the Department of Health, Disability and Ageing website.
