# What help is available

Grieving the death of someone in your family is a tough thing to go through. There are many things to consider. We have payments and services that may help you.

For help with Medicare, you can call the [Medicare program line](/phone-us#medicaregeneral). Let us know if you need an interpreter and we'll arrange one for free.

There are different ways we can help after the death of a loved one.

If you or the person who died are getting a payment from us, we need to know as soon as possible. This is so we pay the right amount and no debt is incurred.
