# Tax time

Find out what you need to know and do at tax time, if you get a payment or service from us.

Tax time marks the end of the financial year. What you need to know and do will depend on your circumstances. This includes if you got Medicare, Centrelink payments, or have a child support case.

The financial year runs from 1 July to 30 June.
