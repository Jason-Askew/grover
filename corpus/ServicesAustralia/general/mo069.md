# Midwife Professional Indemnity Scheme - Application for payment form (MO069)

Use this form to apply for a Midwife Professional Indemnity Scheme Level 1 or Level 2 Commonwealth contribution.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Midwife Professional Indemnity Schemes - Application for payment form](/sites/default/files/2025-07/mo069-2507en-f.pdf "PDF document").
