# Healthcare Identifiers Service - Application to register a new entity form (HW013)

Use this form to register your entity with the Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [HI Service - application to register a new entity form](/sites/default/files/2025-10/hw013-2511en-f.pdf "PDF document").

You can also request a National Authentication Service for Health (NASH) Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) Certificate for your entity.

You must read and agree to the terms and conditions of use when applying for a NASH PKI certificate. These are on pages 7 and 8 of this form.
