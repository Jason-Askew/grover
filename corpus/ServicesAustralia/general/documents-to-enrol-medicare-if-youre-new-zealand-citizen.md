# Documents you need if you’re a New Zealand citizen

You need to provide documents that prove your identity and residency.

You can enrol in Medicare if you’re a New Zealand citizen. You’ll need to give us both:

-   a New Zealand passport for each person you want to enrol
-   2 documents that prove your family live in Australia dated within the last 6 months.

## Proving you live in Australia

You can prove you live in Australia by giving us either:

-   2 documents from Australia
-   one document from Australia and one from where you last lived.

You don’t need to get your documents certified.

If you don’t have these documents, [tell us](/phone-us#medicaregeneral). We’ll talk to you about other options.
