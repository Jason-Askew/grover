## If we contact you

From time to time we’ll contact you. If you’ve set up your myGov Inbox we can send you an SMS or email to let you know there’s a new letter. Otherwise we’ll send it through the post.

Learn how to [set up online account notifications](/set-your-notifications-for-centrelink-letters-online?context=22641).

We’ll contact you before a child you care for is about to turn 16 years, or 18 years for a child with a terminal illness. If you continue to provide care, you’ll need to [transfer Carer Allowance from child to adult](/transfer-carer-payment-and-carer-allowance-from-child-to-adult?context=21811) to keep your payment.

We may also contact you and ask you to do a review to check if you can keep getting Carer Allowance.

## How to cancel Carer Allowance

To cancel your Carer Allowance you can either:

-   call us on the [Disability, sickness and carers line](#toolbar-phone)
-   visit your local [service centre](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/).

Contact numbers available on this page.

### Disability, sickness and carers line

Use this line if you need help with your Disability Support Pension, Special Disability Trusts, Mobility Allowance, Carer Payment, Carer Allowance or Ex-Carer Allowance (Child) Health Care Card, Health Care Card or Pensioner Concession Card.

[132 717](tel:132717)

Monday to Friday 8 am to 5 pm

There are other ways you may want to [contact us](/contact-us).
