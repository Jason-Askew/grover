# Application for approval, amendment or renewal of an approved pathology practitioner form (HW009)

Use this form to apply for or renew acceptance as an Approved Pathology Practitioner under section 23DC of the Health Insurance Act 1973.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Download and complete the [Application for approval, amendment or renewal of an approved pathology practitioner form](/sites/default/files/2025-11/hw009-2511en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Pathology practitioners should use this form.
