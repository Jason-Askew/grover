[

How it works

An Assurance of Support is a legal agreement you make with us to help someone get a visa to live in Australia.


](/how-assurance-support-works?context=22051)

[

Who can be one

To give an Assurance of Support you must meet certain guidelines.


](/who-can-be-assurer-for-assurance-support?context=22051)

[

How long it lasts

The Assurance of Support period depends on the type of visa you apply for. It can last for 1, 2, 4 or 10 years.


](/how-long-assurance-support-lasts?context=22051)

[

How to apply

How you apply to give an Assurance of Support depends on your circumstances.


](/how-to-apply-to-give-assurance-support?context=22051)

[

When you can’t support an assuree

We may pay income support to the assuree during the Assurance of Support period if you can't support them, or they can't accept support from you.


](/when-you-cant-support-assuree-with-assurance-support?context=22051)
