# How to manage your payment

Your options and obligations for Newborn Upfront Payment and Newborn Supplement.

We know that the way you use your money changes in your lifetime. We have tips and information to help you at different times [manage your money](/manage-your-money).

[

Changing your claim to Parental Leave Pay

You can change from Newborn Upfront Payment and Newborn Supplement to Parental Leave Pay if you think it's a better option for you.


](/how-to-change-from-newborn-upfront-payment-and-newborn-supplement-to-parental-leave-pay?context=22186)

[

Change of circumstances

You need to tell us if your circumstances change when you get Newborn Upfront Payment and Newborn Supplement.


](/change-circumstances-when-you-get-newborn-upfront-payment-and-newborn-supplement?context=22186)

[

Travel outside Australia

The Family Tax Benefit travel rules affect your Newborn Upfront Payment and Newborn Supplement when you travel outside Australia.


](/travel-outside-australia-rules-for-newborn-upfront-payment-and-newborn-supplement?context=22186)

[

Someone to act for you

You can ask someone to act for you with Medicare, Centrelink, aged care or Child Support. You can authorise them to speak to us, update your details and get payments for you.


](/someone-to-act-for-you-with-medicare-centrelink-aged-care-or-child-support?context=22186)
