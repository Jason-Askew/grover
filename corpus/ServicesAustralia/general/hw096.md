# HI Service - application to register a healthcare support service provider form (HW096)

Use this form to register a healthcare support service provider with the Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [HI Service - application to register a healthcare support service provider form](/sites/default/files/2025-10/hw096-2511en-f.pdf "PDF document").
