# Youth Disability Supplement

An extra payment if you’re a young person with disability and on an income support payment.

[

Who can get it

You’ll automatically get the Youth Disability Supplement if you’re under 21, and you get Disability Support Pension (DSP).


](/who-can-get-youth-disability-supplement?context=22341)

[

How to get it

You don’t need to apply for Youth Disability Supplement.


](/how-to-get-youth-disability-supplement?context=22341)

[

How much you can get

How much Youth Disability Supplement you can get depends on how much your main payment is. We pay the supplement with your income support payment fortnightly.


](/how-much-youth-disability-supplement-you-can-get?context=22341)
