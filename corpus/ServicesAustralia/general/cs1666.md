# Child support agreement form (CS1666)

Use this form to make a child support agreement, ask us to accept an agreement, replace or end an existing agreement, or end an existing child support liability.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Child support agreement form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/cs1666-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Either party to the agreement can submit this form.
