# Aged care entry record form (AC021)

Use this form to tell the us that a care recipient has entered either permanent residential care or commenced receiving a Home Care Package on or after 1 July 2014.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Aged care entry record form](/sites/default/files/2025-11/ac021-251119en-f.pdf "PDF document").

An authorised person of the approved provider must complete and sign this form within 28 days of the care recipient’s entry or commencement date.

This information will not need to be provided again in the monthly claim form, unless the care recipient’s circumstances change.
