# Income Management and BasicsCard in Playford and the APY Lands of South Australia

You can no longer start Income Management in Playford and the APY Lands of South Australia.

If you are new to Income Management, you will start on [enhanced Income Management](/enhanced-income-management-and-smartcard) instead.

If you are already on Income Management in Playford and the APY Lands of South Australia, you can choose to move to enhanced Income Management an enhanced Income Management account and a SmartCard.

If you choose not to move, you will stay on Income Management and can keep using your BasicsCard if you have one. But if your circumstances change, you might need to move to enhanced Income Management later.
