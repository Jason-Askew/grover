# PBS Safety Net claim for payment form (PB241)

Use this form if you are an approved supplier claiming a payment for issuing a PBS Safety Net card.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete either the PDF or Word version of the:

-   [PBS Safety Net claim for payment form](/sites/default/files/pb241-2010en-f.pdf "PDF document")
-   [PBS Safety Net claim for payment form](/sites/default/files/pb241-2010en.doc "Word document").

PBS is the Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme.

We require you to keep a copy of the completed form that you submit to us for your records.

We will only accept typed claim forms. We will return handwritten, incomplete or poor quality forms to the pharmacy.
