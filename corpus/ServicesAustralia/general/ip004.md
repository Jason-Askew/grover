# Practice Incentives Program Procedural General Practitioner Payment application form (IP004)

Use this form to apply for the Procedural General Practitioner Payment if your practice is already registered for the PIP.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Practice Incentives Program Procedural General Practitioner Payment application form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/ip004-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").

PIP is the Practice Incentives Program.

Only use this form if your practice is already registered for PIP.
