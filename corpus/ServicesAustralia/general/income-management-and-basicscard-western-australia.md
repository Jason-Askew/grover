# Income Management and BasicsCard in Western Australia

You can no longer start Income Management in Western Australia.

If you are new to Income Management, you will start on [enhanced Income Management](/enhanced-income-management-and-smartcard) instead.

If you started Income Management before 4 September 2023, choose the area where you live for more details.
