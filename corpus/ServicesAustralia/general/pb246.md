# Ulcerative colitis paediatric - change or recommencement authority application form (PB246)

Use this form to apply to change or recommence PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine for paediatric patients with moderate to severe ulcerative colitis.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Biological medicine refers to adalimumab or infliximab.
