# Malti / Maltese

We provide information in your language to help you learn more about our payments and services.

Aħna nipprovdu informazzjoni bil-lingwa tiegħek biex ngħinuk titgħallem aktar dwar il-ħlasijiet u s-servizzi tagħna.

Jekk qed tuża teknoloġija ta’ għajnuna għal dawk b’diżabilità u ma tistax ikollok aċċess għall-informazzjoni maqluba bil-lingwa tiegħek, tista’ tikkuntattjana [bil-lingwa tiegħek](/extra-help-when-calling-us).

Qlibna b’diversi lingwi [formoli internazzjonali](/international-forms-for-individuals) li tista’ tuża jekk toqgħod barra l-Awstralja. Tista’ tuża dawn il-formuli biex tikklemja ħlas minn Centrelink. Tista’ tużahom ukoll biex tapplika għal assessja ta’ child support (sapport għat-tfal).

If you are using assistive technology and cannot access our translated information, you can contact us [in your language](/extra-help-when-calling-us).

We have translated [international forms](/international-forms-for-individuals) you can use if you live outside Australia. You can use these forms to claim a Centrelink payment. You can also use them to apply for a child support assessment.
