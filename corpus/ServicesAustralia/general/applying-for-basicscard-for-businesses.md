# Applying

To use BasicsCard as a payment method for your customers, we must approve you as a BasicsCard business.

## on this page

-   [Steps to apply to use the BasicsCard payment method](#a1)
-   [Application outcome](#a2)

To apply, follow these steps:

## Steps to apply to use the BasicsCard payment method

1\. Before you start

Before you start download, print and read:

-   [BasicsCard Merchant Terms](/sites/default/files/2023-08/basicscard-merchant-terms.docx "Word document") - the terms you will need to agree to in order to accept the BasicsCard
-   [BasicsCard Special Conditions for Taxi-cab and Minibus](/sites/default/files/2025-08/basicscard-special-conditions-for-taxi-and-minibus-200825.docx "Word document") - specific to taxi-cab and minibus merchants
-   [BasicsCard Merchant Approval Framework](https://www.dss.gov.au/our-responsibilities/families-and-children/programmes-services/welfare-conditionality/income-management/basicscard-merchant-approval-framework) - policy guidelines on the Department of Social Services website.

2\. Complete the [Application form](/ss460), making sure you:

-   complete all relevant questions
-   complete the terminal details section, and
-   read the agreement to participate and sign the application.

3\. Submit

Email your completed BasicsCard Application form to [impact.basicscard@servicesaustralia.gov.au](mailto:impact.basicscard@servicesaustralia.gov.au).

## Application outcome

We’ll let you know the outcome of your application.

Once approved, we’ll activate your EFTPOS terminals. We’ll use the information from your application to do this. You can then accept the BasicsCard as a form of payment.
