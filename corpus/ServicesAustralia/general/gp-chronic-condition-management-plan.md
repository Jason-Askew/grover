# GP chronic condition management plan

Steps to create and manage a GP chronic condition management plan (GPCCMP).

## Create and manage a GPCCMP

**Step 1**. Find out the [requirements for a GPCCMP](/requirements-for-chronic-condition-management-plan?context=20).

We have information on:

-   health professional eligibility
-   patient eligibility
-   billing requirements
-   referrals to allied health professionals.

**Step 2**. Provide the service to your patient.

**Step 3**. Bill services using the correct Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS) item number.

You can learn about [billing chronic condition management plan items](/mbs-billing-rules-for-gp-chronic-condition-management-plans?context=20).

You can check eligibility and look up item numbers through the [MBS items online checker in HPOS](/check-mbs-item-numbers?context=20). Learn about the relevant MBS item descriptions on the [MBS Online](http://www.mbsonline.gov.au/) website.

**Step 4**. Review and manage the [plan](/requirements-for-chronic-condition-management-plan?context=20#accordion7).
