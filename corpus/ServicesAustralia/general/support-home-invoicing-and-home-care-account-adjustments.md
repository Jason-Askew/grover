# Support at Home invoicing and Home Care account adjustments

On 1 November 2025, the Support at Home program replaced the Home Care Packages program.

The Transition to Support at Home eKit has the following files for you or your software developer:

-   Home care account adjustment template
-   sample invoice CSV upload files
-   sample invoice CSV download file
-   explanations of the structure and file elements for uploading and downloading
-   reference data file that includes claim validation data and full listings, including codes, of Support at Home services, items, wraparounds and health professional types
-   example budget drawdown scenarios reflecting funding use of quarterly budget, Commonwealth unspent funds and home care account funds
-   sample payment statement CSV and PDF download files
-   delegate approval (late claiming evidence list).
