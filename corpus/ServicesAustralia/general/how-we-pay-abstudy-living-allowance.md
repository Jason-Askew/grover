# How we pay it

If you can get ABSTUDY Living Allowance, who we pay it to depends on the student’s circumstances.

## on this page

-   [Students 18 or older](#a1)
-   [Apprentices](#a2)
-   [High school students aged 16 or 17 and living at home](#a3)
-   [High school students and living away from home](#a4)

We’ll let you know who we’ll pay Living Allowance to when you claim.

In some situations, parents and guardians might be able to choose who we pay it to.

## Students 18 or older

We can pay ABSTUDY straight to you, if you are one of the following:

-   a student 18 or older
-   a student under 18 and independent
-   a high school student at a non-school provider
-   a higher education or a masters or doctorate student.

## Apprentices

If you’re doing an Australian Apprenticeship, we can pay ABSTUDY straight to you.

## High school students aged 16 or 17 and living at home

If you’re 16 or 17 and a dependent high school student living at home, we pay ABSTUDY to either:

-   your parent or guardian
-   the student, if requested by their parent or guardian
-   the student, if their parent or guardian uses it for other purposes, like gambling or alcohol.

## High school students and living away from home

### Under 16

For dependent high school students under 16 who live away from home, we may pay ABSTUDY to:

-   their parent or guardian
-   their boarding school
-   their private board provider, if requested by their parent or guardian
-   their [boarding hostel](/hostels-with-active-agreement-with-abstudy?context=45541) if it has an active ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement.

### 16 or older

For dependent high school students aged 16 or older who live away from home, we may pay ABSTUDY to:

-   their parent or guardian
-   the student, if requested by their parent or guardian
-   their boarding school
-   their private board provider, if requested by their parent or guardian
-   their [boarding hostel](/hostels-with-active-agreement-with-abstudy?context=45541) if it has an active ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement.
