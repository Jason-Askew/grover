# Assignment of benefit Medicare bulk bill Webclaim form (DB020)

Use this form in conjunction with HPOS Medicare Bulk Bill Webclaims only. It cannot be submitted to us for manual processing.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Assignment of benefit Medicare bulk bill Webclaim form](/sites/default/files/db020-2006en-f.pdf "PDF document").

This form is interactive. It has 2 copies, one for the health professional and one for the patient.
