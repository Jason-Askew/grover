# Claiming as a secondary or primary student

Complete the following steps to claim ABSTUDY Living Allowance as a secondary or primary school student. Before you start, check if you can get it.

## Steps to claim ABSTUDY Living Allowance as a secondary or primary student

1.  ### 1\. Get your documents ready
    
    You’ll need to get some documents ready to help answer some of the questions in the claim. We will need your Tax File Number, bank account details, and course details.
    
2.  ### 2\. Make your claim
    
    If you are a secondary or primary school student, you can claim the allowance by either:
    
    -   calling us on the [ABSTUDY line](#toolbar-phone) and we'll help you
    -   printing and completing the [claim for ABSTUDY](/sy019) or [claim for ABSTUDY remote areas](/sy092).
    
    To submit your claim form, follow these steps:
    
    1.  Check you’ve answered all questions.
    2.  Sign and date the claim form.
    3.  Attach any supporting documents.
    4.  Submit your claim to us.
    
    You can submit your claim form in 1 of the following ways:
    
    -   by post to
        
        Services Australia  
        Student Services  
        Reply Paid 7804  
        Canberra BC ACT 2610
        
    -   in person at a [service centre](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/).
    
    #### Outcome of your claim
    
    We’ll let you know the result of your application. We’ll send a message to your [myGov Inbox](/centrelink-letters-online).
    
    If you don't get electronic letters, we'll send you a letter in the mail.
    
    If we need more details we’ll ask you for them.
    
    You can ask us to [review our decision](/explanations-and-formal-reviews-centrelink-decision?context=262661) if you think we’ve made a mistake.
