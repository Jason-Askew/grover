# Ulcerative colitis adult - initial authority application form (PB127)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine for adult patients with moderate to severe ulcerative colitis or etrasimod for patients with moderate to severe ulcerative colitis.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Biological medicine refers to:

-   adalimumab
-   etrasimod
-   golimumab
-   infliximab
-   ozanimod
-   tofacitinib
-   upadacitinib
-   ustekinumab
-   vedolizumab.
