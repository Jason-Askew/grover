### All other documents

To upload a document, follow these steps:

1.  Select **Upload documents** from your home screen, then **Upload document**.
2.  Select either **Centrelink form** followed by **Scan barcode** or **Other document** followed by the category that best suits your document, then:
    -   **Take a photo**, **Upload a document** or **Add a photo from library** if you use an Apple device
    -   **Upload a photo** or **Upload a document** if you use an Android device.
3.  When your documents or photos are ready, select **Submit**.

To check the documents you’ve uploaded, select **Upload documents** from your home screen.

Copies of documents you upload will only be available to download or view online for 24 hours. You can check the document type, how it was uploaded, as well as the date and time of the upload for 2 years. Hiding sensitive details helps protect your personal information. If you need copies of those documents, you can [contact us](/personal-information-releases?context=64107) to request them.

For more help, read our [upload documents online guide](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app-help-upload-documents?context=64107) for the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app.

## What documents you can upload

The total size of all files for a document upload must be under 10MB. Each file must also be in one of the following formats:

-   pdf
-   png
-   jpeg.

You can include up to 10 files for each upload.

Make sure you remove the password protection on your document before you upload it.

Please make sure all images you upload are appropriate.

Don’t upload any images that:

-   are distressing, intimidating or offensive
-   are of the human body as evidence of physical injuries, or to support a claim
-   could be considered illegal, as we may report them to the relevant authorities.
