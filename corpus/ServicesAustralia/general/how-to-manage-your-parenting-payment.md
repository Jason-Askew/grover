# How to manage your payment

Your options for managing your Parenting Payment.

There are some things you can do to manage your Parenting Payment.

You may get part of your payment [as an advance](/advance-payment?context=22196).

Some people can get income support payments [paid each week](/weekly-payment-option?context=22196) instead of each fortnight.

We can help you access your money if your [bank account is overdrawn](/overdrawn-bank-account?context=22196).

Keep in mind there are rules about how long you can get Parenting Payment when you [travel outside Australia](/travel-outside-australia-rules-for-parenting-payment?context=22196).

You can arrange regular deductions from your Centrelink payments to pay bills. Read more about [Centrepay](/centrepay).

You can also get help to [manage your money](/manage-your-money) and [other help](/getting-other-help-when-recently-unemployed) from support services.
