# Centrelink Confirmation eServices (CCeS) for businesses

Your business can use Centrelink Confirmation eServices to confirm a customer's eligibility for a concession, rebate or service you provide.

To use Centrelink Confirmation eServices (CCeS) you must have a contract with us. You’ll also need to get consent from your customers to check their details with us.

To find out if CCeS is right for you, read about [how it works](/how-centrelink-confirmation-eservices-for-businesses-works?context=23236).

Read more about [how to apply](/how-to-apply-to-use-centrelink-confirmation-eservices-for-businesses?context=23236), including the policy, terms and procedural guide.
