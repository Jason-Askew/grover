# What it can help you with

The Refugee Servicing Network can help you find out about payments.

We can also help you:

-   get a Medicare card
-   talk to someone in your language
-   attend free seminars about our payments and services
-   apply for payments and exemptions
-   find work.

To find out more, visit a [service centre](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/). You can call the [Multilingual Phone Service](#toolbar-phone) to speak to us in your language about Centrelink payments and services.

Contact numbers available on this page.
