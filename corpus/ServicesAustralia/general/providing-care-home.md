# Providing care at home

Services for older Australians who need care to remain living independently at home.

From 1 November 2025, the Support at Home Program replaced the Home Care Packages Program and Short-Term Restorative Care Programme.

You can use the [Aged Care Provider Portal (ACPP)](/aged-care-provider-portal?context=20) to do business with us.

Support at Home Program for aged care providers
