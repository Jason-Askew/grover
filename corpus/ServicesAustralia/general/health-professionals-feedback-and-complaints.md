# Health professionals feedback and complaints

Health professionals can make a complaint, give a compliment or offer a suggestion to help us improve our service.

If you are a [customer](/complaints-and-feedback) or [business](/business-feedback-and-complaints) you should send your feedback or make a complaint using the specific options for you.

You can provide feedback or make a complaint [online](https://feedback.humanservices.gov.au/mcasite_feedback/feedback/feedbackBasePage.jsf?wec-appid=feedback&wec-locale=en) or over the phone 1800 132 468.
