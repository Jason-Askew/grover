# How to apply

You must complete an application to use Centrepay.

## on this page

-   [Step 1: read our documents](#a1)
-   [Step 2: complete the application form](#a2)
-   [Step 3: submit forms](#a3)
-   [Step 4: after you apply](#a4)

If approved, your business can start using Centrepay under the new Terms of Use.

## Step 1: read our documents

Download and read the [Centrepay Terms of Use and Centrepay Policy for Businesses](/centrepay-policy-terms-and-guides?context=23256) documents.

These documents tell you about Centrepay including all the following:

-   what you should consider before applying
-   the requirements your business must meet
-   the obligations of Centrepay businesses
-   that Centrepay is free and voluntary for Centrelink customers
-   that customers must provide consent for all deductions.

You must make sure your business is able to comply with the obligations under the Terms of Use, and Policy before applying.

We suggest you get legal advice in relation to the contents of these documents.

## Step 2: complete the application form

You must download, read, and complete the [Centrepay - Business Application form](/sa389).

## Step 3: submit forms

You can submit your forms and other documents by email or post to our [Centrepay for businesses team](/business-and-employer-contact-information#centrepaybusinesses).

## Step 4: after you apply

We may contact you and ask for more details in relation to your business application.

Once we assess your application, we’ll send you a letter advising you of the outcome. This letter includes details to help you get started with Centrepay.

### Contact us

Call the [Centrepay for businesses team](/business-and-employer-contact-information#centrepaybusinesses) if you have more Centrepay questions.

If you aren’t satisfied with the decision we’ve made, you can send us a complaint either by:

-   calling the [Centrepay for businesses team](/business-and-employer-contact-information#centrepaybusinesses)
-   submitting a written request within 10 business days of our decision.
