# Our payments affected by compensation

Some Centrelink payments are affected by compensation or similar payments from benefits or schemes.

Some payments from us are affected by compensation or similar payments.

These include all the following income support payments and concession cards:

-   Age Pension 
-   Austudy 
-   Carer Payment 
-   Disability Support Pension 
-   Farm Household Allowance 
-   JobSeeker Payment 
-   Parenting Payment 
-   Special Benefit 
-   Youth Allowance 
-   Low Income Health Care Card
-   Pensioner Education Supplement, when paid with an income support payment.

You can read a full list of [compensation affected payments](https://guides.dss.gov.au/social-security-guide/1/1/c/250), including historical payments we no longer pay, in the Social Security Guide on the Department of Social Services website.
