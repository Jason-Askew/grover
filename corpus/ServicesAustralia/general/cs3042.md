# Representative authority form (CS3042)

Use this form to authorise another person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf about your child support case.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Representative authority form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/cs3042-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").

The person you authorise must be 18 or older.
