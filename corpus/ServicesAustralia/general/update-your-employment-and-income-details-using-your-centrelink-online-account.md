# Update your employment and income details online

You can update your employment and income details using your online account.

You can view or update:

-   [employment income](/centrelink-online-account-help-report-employment-income)
-   deductions for [Centrepay](/centrelink-online-account-help-add-new-centrepay-deduction) and [tax](/centrelink-online-account-help-deducting-tax-from-your-payment)
-   [income and assets including banking, shares and other assets](/centrelink-online-account-help-manage-your-income-and-assets-details)
-   [money you owe](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app-help-manage-money-you-owe)
-   [advance payments](/centrelink-online-account-help-manage-your-advance-payments).

You may also [cancel a current payment](/cancelling-your-payment-online).
