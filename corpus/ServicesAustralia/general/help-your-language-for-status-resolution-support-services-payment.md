# Help in your language

If you don’t speak English you can get help from us in your language.

## on this page

-   [Centrelink](#a1)
-   [Medicare](#a2)
-   [Child support](#a3)

You can read [information in your language](/information-your-language).

## Centrelink

For Centrelink payments and services, you can call our [multilingual phone service](/phone-us#languages).

We also have [multicultural service officers](/multicultural-service-officers) who can help you with payments and services.

## Medicare

For help with Medicare, you can call the [Medicare program line](/phone-us#medicaregeneral). Let us know if you need an interpreter and we’ll arrange one for free.

## Child support

For help with Child Support, you can call the [Child Support Enquiry line](/phone-us#csgeneralenquiries). Let us know if you need an interpreter and we’ll arrange one for free.
