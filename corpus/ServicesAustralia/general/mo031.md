# Statutory declaration ROCS - Medical practitioner who has ceased private or all remunerated medical practice due to retirement form (MO031)

Use this form to declare for the Run-Off Cover Scheme (ROCS) that you're a medical practitioner who has ceased private or all remunerated medical practice due to retirement.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Statutory declaration ROCS - Medical practitioner who has ceased private or all remunerated medical practice due to retirement form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/mo031-2501en-f.pdf "PDF document").

The ROCS only applies to claims or incidents first notified on or after 1 July 2004.
