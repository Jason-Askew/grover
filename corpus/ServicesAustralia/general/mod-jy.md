# Parent or Guardian details - for the BASE tax year and CURRENT tax year for dependent Youth Allowance or ABSTUDY customers form (MOD JY)

Use this form to provide income details of a parent or guardian if you are a dependent claiming or receiving Youth Allowance or ABSTUDY.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Parent or Guardian details - for the BASE tax year and CURRENT tax year for dependent Youth Allowance or ABSTUDY customers form](/sites/default/files/2023-07/modjy-2307en-f.pdf "PDF document").

The parent or guardian of the person claiming or receiving Youth Allowance or ABSTUDY should complete this form.

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, sign in now to submit your form and any supporting documents.
