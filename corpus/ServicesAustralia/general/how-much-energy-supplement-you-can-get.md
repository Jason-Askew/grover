# How much you can get

How much Energy Supplement you get depends on your circumstances and the payment you get.

If you have a debt, we may use your Energy Supplement payments to repay it. We don't count Energy Supplement as income. We don't tax or index it.

The rate you get depends on your and your partner’s circumstances and the payment or services you get.
