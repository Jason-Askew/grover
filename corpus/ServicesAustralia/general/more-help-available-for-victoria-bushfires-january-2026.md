# More help available for Victoria Bushfires, January 2026

Published: 9 February 2026

If you’ve been affected by this disaster, we have Australian Government Disaster Recovery Payment (AGDRP) to help you.

![](/sites/default/files/styles/dhs_news_article_image/public/2023-11/15296b-2311.jpg?itok=hrDydttS)

AGDRP is a lump sum payment to help you if you’ve been directly affected by this disaster.

AGDRP has been extended to the following areas:

-   Acheron in Murrindindi Local Government Area (LGA)
-   Bullioh in Towong LGA
-   Crooked River in Wellington LGA
-   Meredith in Golden Plains LGA
-   Mount Mercer in Golden Plains LGA
-   Thornton in Murrindindi LGA.

Disaster Recovery Allowance is also available for people who’ve lost income because of this disaster.

Learn about your eligibility for [Victoria Bushfires, January 2026](/vic-bushfires-jan-2026).
