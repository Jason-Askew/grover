# How much you can get

The amount of Remote Area Allowance you can get depends on if you’re single or in a couple.

-   [Income and assets tests](#a1)

-   [Reviews](#a3)

If you're

The fortnightly payment is

Single

$18.20

Couple

$15.60 each

If you have any dependent children, we’ll pay you an extra $7.30 per fortnight for each one.

This is a tax free allowance.

## Income and assets tests

You don’t have to meet an income or asset test.

## Zone tax offset

If you get the allowance, you need to reduce dollar for dollar the zone tax offset you’re claiming in your income tax return. Find out more about [zone tax offset](https://www.ato.gov.au/individuals-and-families/income-deductions-offsets-and-records/tax-offsets/zone-and-overseas-forces-tax-offsets) on the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) website.

## Reviews

We’ll review your allowance if you change address.
