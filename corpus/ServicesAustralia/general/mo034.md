# Statutory declaration ROCS - Medical practitioner who has not engaged in either private or remunerated medical practice during the preceding 3 years form (MO034)

Use this form to declare for the Run-Off Cover Scheme (ROCS) that you're a medical practitioner who hasn't engaged in either private or remunerated medical practice during the preceding 3 years.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Statutory declaration ROCS - Medical practitioner who has not engaged in either private or remunerated medical practice during the preceding 3 years form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/mo034-2501en-f.pdf "PDF document").

The ROCS only applies to claims or incidents first notified on or after 1 July 2004.
