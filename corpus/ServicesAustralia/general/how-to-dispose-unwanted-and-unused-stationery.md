# How to dispose of unwanted and unused stationery

If you need to dispose of unwanted or unused Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) stationery, we can help.

You must dispose of unwanted and unused your Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) stationery using a secure method.

## How to dispose of unwanted and unused stationery

You can securely destroy unwanted or unused stationery by placing it in a secure bin or using a shredder.

If you can’t destroy the stationery yourself, you can email Print Media Group. Write Secure destruction in the subject line and email to [sapbs@pmg.com.au](mailto:sapbs@pmg.com.au).

In your email, you’ll need to provide the following:

-   prescriber’s name
-   pick-up address
-   contact name and number
-   number of cartons ready for collection
-   reason you need the stationery destroyed
-   any other relevant details to help Australia Post collect your stationery.

Print Media Group will reply within one business day to confirm that your request has been actioned.
