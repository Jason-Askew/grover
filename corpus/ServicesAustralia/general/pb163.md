# Growth hormone paediatric - continuing authority application form (PB163)

Use this form to apply to continue PBS-subsidised treatment with somatropin or somatrogon for paediatric patients with severe growth hormone deficiency under the section 100 Growth Hormone Program.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.
