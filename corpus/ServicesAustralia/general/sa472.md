# Consent to disclose medical information form (SA472)

Use this form to confirm that you consent to your treating health providers disclosing relevant information about your disability or medical conditions to us.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Consent to disclose medical information form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/sa472-2412en-f_1.pdf "PDF document").

This form is used to support your [claim for Disability Support Pension form](/sa466).
