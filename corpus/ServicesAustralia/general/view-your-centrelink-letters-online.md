# View your letters

There are a few ways you can view your Centrelink letters online.

You can view your Centrelink letters online using:

-   your [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) Inbox
-   the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app).

Your letters stay available online for up to 2 years. You can:

-   print them
-   save them to your computer
-   save them to the vault in your app.
