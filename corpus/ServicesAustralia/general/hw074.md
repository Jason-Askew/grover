# General practitioner 90 day pay doctor cheque scheme application form (HW074)

Use this form if you are a general practitioner registering for the General Practitioner 90 Day Pay Doctor Cheque Scheme.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [General practitioner 90 day pay doctor cheque scheme application form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/hw074-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").
