# Payment rates on ABSTUDY

If you get ABSTUDY the Energy Supplement amount you get depends on your and your partner’s circumstances and the payment or services you get.

## on this page

-   [ABSTUDY 22 and over](#a1)
-   [ABSTUDY under 22](#a2)
-   [ABSTUDY under 22 with Youth Disability Supplement](#a3)
-   [Payment options](#a4)

If you get an ABSTUDY Masters and Doctorate Living Allowance, you’ ll get $75 of Energy Supplement each quarter. This will be in March, June, September and December.

## Payment options

If you get ABSTUDY Living Allowance, you’ll usually get Energy Supplement fortnightly. We pay it with your regular payment. You may also be able to get Energy Supplement quarterly.

You can change how often you get your payment at any time by [calling us](/phone-us) on your regular payment line.
