# Electronic Verification of Rent for businesses

A free online service for housing providers to manage tenant rent details directly with us. You can use it to tell us each time there’s a change in a tenant’s rent.

Your business can verify and update a tenant’s rent amount using Electronic Verification of Rent (EVoR) each time there’s a change in rent. To find out if EVoR is right for you, read about [how it works](/how-electronic-verification-rent-for-businesses-works?context=23176).

To use EVoR you must have a contract with us. You’ll also need to get consent from your tenants before you change their rent details with us. Read more about [who can use it](/who-can-use-electronic-verification-rent-for-businesses?context=23176).

Read about [how to apply](/how-to-apply-for-electronic-verification-rent-for-businesses?context=23176) for EVoR, including the policy, terms and procedural guide.
