# Aged care application for the dementia and cognition supplement in home care provided before 1 November 2025 form (AC014)

Use this form to advise us that a care recipient within your aged care service is eligible to receive the Dementia and Cognition Supplement.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Aged care application for the dementia and cognition supplement in home care provided before 1 November 2025 form](/sites/default/files/2025-10/ac014-2511en-f.pdf "PDF document").

This form must be completed and signed by an authorised person of the approved provider.

To submit this form, register a Dementia and Cognition Supplement event on the [Aged Care Provider Portal](/aged-care-provider-portal) (ACPP). You can then upload the form and any additional documents.

If the ACPP is not available, return the completed form and supporting documents by following the instructions on the form.
