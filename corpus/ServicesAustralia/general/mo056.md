# Run off Cover Indemnity Scheme - medical certificate permanent disability form (MO056)

Use this form when a Medical Indemnity Insurer or Medical Defence Organisation determines an insured medical practitioner is eligible to participate in the Run off Cover Indemnity Scheme (ROCS) due to permanent disability.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Run off Cover Indemnity Scheme - medical certificate permanent disability form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/mo056-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Use this form if the medical practitioner has temporarily or permanently ceased from providing medical services due to permanent disability.

A medical practitioner other than the medical practitioner being assessed for eligibility under the ROCS must complete this form.

ROCS is the Run off Cover Indemnity Scheme.
