# Details of your child’s care arrangements form (FA012)

Use this form to provide information about your child’s care arrangements.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Details of your child’s care arrangements form](/sites/default/files/2024-06/fa012-2407en-f.pdf "PDF document").
