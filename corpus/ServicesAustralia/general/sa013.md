# Request for ophthalmologist/optometrist report form (SA013)

Use this form if you are claiming Age Pension or Disability Support Pension on the basis of permanent blindness.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Request for ophthalmologist/optometrist report](/sites/default/files/2023-10/sa013-2310en-f.pdf "PDF document").

An ophthalmologist or optometrist must complete this form.
