# Crohn's disease paediatric - initial authority application form (PB085)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine for paediatric patients with Crohn’s disease.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Biological medicine refers to adalimumab and infliximab.

Please note: You don’t need to complete this form if you use the [Online PBS Authorities](/apply-for-pbs-authority#accordion1) system.

Download and complete the [Crohn’s disease paediatric - initial authority application form](/sites/default/files/2025-08/pb085-2509en-f.pdf "PDF document").
