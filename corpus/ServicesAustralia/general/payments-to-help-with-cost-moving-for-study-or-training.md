# Payments to help with the cost of moving for study or training

Published: 3 February 2026

You could get extra payments from Centrelink if you need to move away from home to study, train or do an Australian Apprenticeship.

![news item](/sites/default/files/styles/dhs_news_article_image/public/2020-02/13587-d1-0460.jpg?itok=jRDYzJyr)

If you get one of the following payments:

-   Youth Allowance for students and apprentices
-   Austudy
-   ABSTUDY.

You may be eligible for further payments to help with the cost of moving for study or training.

They can be used for expenses like travel, rent and living costs.

You need to make a claim for some of these payments to get them.
