# Self service

You can manage most of your Medicare, Centrelink and Child Support information with us online or by phone self service.

## on this page

-   [Online help guides](#onlinehelpguides)
-   [Service availability and changes](#service)
-   [Service centre or Agents and Access Points](#a3)

If you have a myGov account [sign in to myGov](https://my.gov.au/) now to manage your information.

## Online help guides

Guides to help you use myGov, Medicare, Centrelink and Child Support online accounts and Express Plus mobile apps. Read more about our [online help guides](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps?context=64107).

## Service availability and changes

Our apps are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. There may be short breaks for Child Support digital services updates between 2 am and 5 am AEST.

You can find out if our services are unavailable by going to [customer service changes](/customer-service-changes?context=64107).
