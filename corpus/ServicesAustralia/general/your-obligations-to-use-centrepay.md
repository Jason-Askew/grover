# Your obligations

Approved Centrepay businesses must comply and act in line with the Terms of Use and Policy for Businesses.

Your Centrepay obligations are clearly detailed in the following documents:

-   the Centrepay Terms of Use
-   the Centrepay Policy for Businesses
-   your approval letter to use Centrepay
-   any additional conditions we impose on your business
-   the Deduction authority form.
