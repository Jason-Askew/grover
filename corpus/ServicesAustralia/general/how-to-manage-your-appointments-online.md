# How to manage your appointments online

If you’re a Centrelink customer, you can book, reschedule and cancel some of your appointments online.

## on this page

-   [Using your Centrelink online account through myGov](#a1)
-   [Using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](#usingexpressplus)

## Using your Centrelink online account through myGov

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, you can manage your appointments online.

To do this:

1.  Sign in to myGov.
2.  Select **MENU** from your homepage.
3.  Select **Documents and appointments**, then **Appointments** and **Manage appointments**.

You can then select one of the following:

-   **Book appointment** to book a new appointment
-   **View details**, then **Reschedule appointment** to change your appointment
-   **View details**, then **Cancel appointment** when an appointment can be cancelled online.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

## Using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app

To manage your appointments using the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app), follow these steps:

1.  **Sign in** to the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app).
2.  Select **Appointment** from the home screen.

You can then select one of the following:

-   **Book appointment** to book a new appointment
-   **Reschedule appointment** to change your appointment
-   **Cancel appointment** when an appointment can be cancelled online.

Your appointment details will show on your home screen.

To add or update your appointment in your device’s calendar, select **Appointment**, then **Add to calendar**.

If you can’t manage your appointment online, you’ll need to [phone us](/phone-us#jobseekers) on your regular payment line.
