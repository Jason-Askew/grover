# Register or update for aged care web services form (AC027)

Use this form to register or amend details for third party software built on aged care web services.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Register or update for aged care web services form](/sites/default/files/2025-10/ac027-2511en-f.pdf "PDF document").

An authorised person for an approved aged care service must complete and sign this form.

Read more about [setting up your aged care web services software](/set-up-aged-care-web-services-software).
