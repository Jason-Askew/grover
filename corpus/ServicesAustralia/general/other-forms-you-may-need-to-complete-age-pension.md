# Other forms you may need to complete

When you make your claim for Age Pension, we may ask you to give us more information.

You may need to complete some extra forms. Your claim will tell you which ones.

Your situation

Forms you may need to complete

[Income and Asset information](/sa369)

You're self-employed or have a business

[Business details](/mod-f)

You have a private company

[Private Company details](/mod-pc)

You have a private trust

[Private Trust details](/mod-pt)

You have had compensation or damages

[Compensation and damages](/mod-c)

You want someone else to deal with us for you
