# Manual identity verification for Provider Digital Access form (HW080)

Use this form if you could not verify your identity online for your Provider Digital Access (PRODA) account or your name has changed.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Manual identity verification for Provider Digital Access form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/hw080-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").
