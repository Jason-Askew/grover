# Turn notifications on and off

You can turn notifications for the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app on or off from your device.

## on this page

-   [Turn on notifications](#a1)
-   [Turn off notifications](#a2)

When you subscribe to notifications, you’ll get reminders for tasks you need to complete.

You’ll be subscribed initially when you:

-   accept the notification offer when you first sign in to the app using an Apple device; or
-   first sign in to the app using an Android device.

You’ll only get notifications from us when you’re connected to the internet.

## Turn on notifications

To turn on notifications after initially signing in, go to **Settings** in your device, then **Notifications** and select **Centrelink** from the list then:

-   on your Apple device, switch **Allow Notifications** to **ON**
-   on your Android device, switch **Show notifications** to **ON**.

## Turn off notifications

To turn off notifications go to **Settings** in your device, then **Notifications** and select **Centrelink** from the list then:

-   on your Apple device, switch **Allow Notifications** to **OFF**
-   on your Android device, switch **Show notifications** to **OFF**.
