# Additional support available for Victoria Bushfires, January 2026

Published: 20 January 2026

If you’ve been affected by this disaster, we have Australian Government Disaster Recovery Payment (AGDRP) to help you.

![](/sites/default/files/styles/dhs_news_article_image/public/2026-01/15296g-2601-emergency.jpg?itok=AKDAaAvW)

AGDRP is a lump sum payment to help you if you’ve been directly affected by this declared disaster.

AGDRP has been extended to the following areas:

-   Cobram in Moira Local Government Area (LGA)
-   Fawcett in Murrindindi LGA
-   Koriella in Murrindindi LGA
-   Maintongoon in Murrindindi LGA
-   Mitre in Horsham LGA
-   Mitre in West Wimmera LGA
-   Walwa in Toowong LGA
-   Yarroweyah in Moira LGA.

Disaster Recovery Allowance is also available for people who’ve lost income because of this disaster.

Learn about your eligibility for [Victoria Bushfires, January 2026](/vic-bushfires-jan-2026).
