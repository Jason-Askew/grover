# Centrelink Medical Certificate form (SU415)

Use this form if you’re a medical practitioner and your patient can’t meet their requirements because they are temporarily incapacitated due to a medical condition.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Centrelink Medical Certificate form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/su415-2501en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Only medical practitioners may complete and sign this form.

You can also [complete a Centrelink Medical Certificate using Health Professional Online Services (HPOS)](/complete-medical-certificate-for-centrelink#accordion1). This helps us assess your patients request faster.
