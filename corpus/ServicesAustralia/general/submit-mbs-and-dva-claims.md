# Submit MBS and DVA claims

You can submit Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS) and Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA) claims through ECLIPSE, Medicare Easyclaim, Medicare Online and Health Professional Online Services (HPOS).

There are multiple channels you can use to submit Medicare claims and Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA) claims. You can do these claims manually or electronically.
