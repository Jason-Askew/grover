# What criteria you need to meet to get a higher rate

To get a higher rate of your ABSTUDY payment there are certain criteria you need to meet.

Criteria you may need to meet depends on your situation.

[

Your travel time or access

You may get a higher rate of ABSTUDY if travel to study or work is impossible or unreasonable. You must also live away from home because of this.


](/your-travel-time-or-access-criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy?context=45541)

[

Your local government school has a limited program or facilities

You may get a higher rate of ABSTUDY if your local government secondary schools are limited program schools. You must also live away from home to study because of this.


](/criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy-if-your-local-government-school-has-limited-program-or?context=45541)

[

You’re doing an approved special course

You may get a higher rate of ABSTUDY Living Allowance if you live away from home to do an approved special course.


](/approved-special-course-criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy?context=45541)

[

You have a disability, health condition or special education need

You may get a higher rate of ABSTUDY Living Allowance if you have a disability or health condition. You must also live away from home because of this.


](/criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy-if-you-have-disability-health-condition-or-special-education?context=45541)

[

Your home conditions are unsuitable

You may get a higher rate of ABSTUDY Living Allowance if you live away from home because of your home conditions.


](/criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy-if-your-home-conditions-are-unsuitable?context=45541)

[

Your parents move or are away a lot because of work

You may get a higher rate of ABSTUDY Living Allowance if your parents move a lot for work. You must also live away from home to study because of this.


](/criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy-if-your-parents-move-or-are-away-lot-because-work?context=45541)

[

You get a scholarship

You may get a higher rate of ABSTUDY Living Allowance if you live away from home because you’re offered a scholarship.


](/criteria-to-get-higher-rate-abstudy-if-you-get-scholarship?context=45541)
