# Work Health and Safety policies

Our Work Health and Safety (WHS) policies outline our WHS obligations to people who interact with us.

The policies apply to staff, contractors, other workers and customers.

They outline the objectives and duties of Services Australia as well as the duties of all:

-   staff and other workers
-   officers
-   other people.

We commit to complying with our WHS obligations set out in the:

-   [_Work Health and Safety Act 2011_](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/C2011A00137)
-   [Work Health and Safety Regulations 2011](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/F2011L02664)
-   [Codes of Practice](https://www.comcare.gov.au/scheme-legislation/whs-act/codes-of-practice)
-   [HR Policy Statement: Work Health and Safety](/sites/default/files/2024-11/work-health-and-safety-policy-statement.pdf "PDF document")
-   [HR Policy Statement: Work Health and Safety](/sites/default/files/2022-06/work-health-and-safety-policy-statement.docx "Word document")
-   [HR Policy Statement: Respectful Workplaces and Complaints](/sites/default/files/2024-11/managing-complaints-policy-external.pdf "PDF document")
-   [HR Policy Statement: Respectful Workplaces and Complaints](/sites/default/files/2024-11/managing-complaints-policy-external.docx "Word document").
