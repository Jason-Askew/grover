# Lea Faka-Tonga / Tongan

We provide information in your language to help you learn more about our payments and services.

‘Oku mau ‘oatu ‘ae ngaahi fakamatala ‘ihe ho’o lea Fakafonua ke tokoni ki ho’o ‘ilo fekau’aki mo ‘emau ngaahi vahe kehekehe mo ‘emau faifatongia ngaue.

Kapau ‘oku ke ngaue’aki ha ngaahi tokoni fakatekinolosia pea ‘ikai ke malava ‘o hu atu kihe fakamatala kuo ‘osi fakatonulea’i, pea ke fetu’utaki mai leva kia mautolu ‘i [ho’o lea fakafonua](/extra-help-when-calling-us).

If you are using assistive technology and cannot access our translated information, you can contact us [in your language](/extra-help-when-calling-us).
