# Claim for ABSTUDY Remote Areas form (SY092)

Use this form to claim ABSTUDY Remote Areas Allowance if you are a secondary school student from a remote community who needs to live away from home to attend school.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Claim for ABSTUDY Remote Areas form](/sites/default/files/2024-07/sy092-2407en-f.pdf "PDF document").
