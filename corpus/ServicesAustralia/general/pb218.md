# Moderate to severe hidradenitis suppurativa - initial, change or recommencement authority application form (PB218)

Use this form to apply for initial, changing or recommencing PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine for moderate to severe hidradenitis suppurativa.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Biological medicine refers to adalimumab, bimekizumab and secukinumab.
