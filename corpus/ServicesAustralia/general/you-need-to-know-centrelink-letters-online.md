# You need to know

How Centrelink letters online works.

[

Who can get letters online

You can get Centrelink letters online if you have linked your Centrelink online account to myGov.


](/who-can-get-centrelink-letters-online?context=22641)

[

What you can do

When you get Centrelink letters online you can view, print or save them.


](/what-you-can-do-with-centrelink-letters-online?context=22641)

[

Terms and conditions

If you choose to get Centrelink letters online, we'll stop sending most letters to your mailing address.


](/centrelink-letters-online-terms-and-conditions?context=22641)

[

Security and privacy

We take your online security seriously.


](/security-and-privacy-for-centrelink-letters-online?context=22641)

[

Your mailing address

You should make sure your mailing address is up to date, even if you get Centrelink letters online.


](/your-mailing-address-when-you-get-centrelink-letters-online?context=22641)
