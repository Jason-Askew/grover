# Simplified Billing or ECLIPSE adjustment claim form (HW023)

Use this form to adjust a previously processed claim which needs to be amended with new or altered information.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Simplified Billing or ECLIPSE adjustment claim form](/sites/default/files/2025-09/hw023-2508en-f.pdf "PDF document").

This form should be used by medical practices, providers, private health insurers and billing agents.

ECLIPSE is the Electronic Claim Lodgement and Information Processing Service Environment.
