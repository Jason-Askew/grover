# Information you need to know about your claim for Age Pension and Pension Bonus form (CI006)

Information about your claim for Age Pension and Pension Bonus.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You should read this information before you start your claim for Age Pension and Pension Bonus.
