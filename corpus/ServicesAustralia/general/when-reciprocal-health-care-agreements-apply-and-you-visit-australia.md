# When you visit Australia

Information for visitors to Australia on Reciprocal Health Care Agreements.

[

Visiting from Belgium

If you’re visiting Australia from Belgium, you may be eligible for medical care under Medicare while you’re here.


](/reciprocal-health-care-agreements-visiting-from-belgium?context=22481)

Visiting from Finland

Visiting from Italy

If you’re visiting Australia from Italy, you may be eligible for medical care under Medicare for a period of 6 months while you’re here.
