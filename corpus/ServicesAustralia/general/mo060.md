# ROCS - midwife medical certificate for permanent disability form (MO060)

Use this form if a Professional Indemnity Insurer has determined that an insured midwife is eligible to participate in the Midwife Professional Indemnity (Commonwealth Contribution) Scheme for Run-off Cover.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [ROCS - midwife medical certificate for permanent disability form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/mo060-2507en-f.pdf "PDF document")

Use this form if the midwife has temporarily or permanently ceased from providing medical services due to permanent disability.

ROCS is the Run off Cover Indemnity Scheme.
