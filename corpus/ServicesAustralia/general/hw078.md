# Request for pay group link form (HW078)

Use this form to request a pay group link so you can have Medicare benefit cheques made payable to another payee associated with the practice or another address.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Request for pay group link form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/hw078-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Medicare benefit cheques are usually made payable to the provider at their location address.
