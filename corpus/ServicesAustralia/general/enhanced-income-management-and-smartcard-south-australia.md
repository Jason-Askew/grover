# Enhanced Income Management and SmartCard in South Australia

Manage your money using enhanced Income Management in some parts of South Australia. It will help you meet essential household needs and expenses for you and your family.

Enhanced Income Management and SmartCard are available in these areas of South Australia:

-   Ceduna region
-   Greater Adelaide region
-   Playford region
-   Anangu Pitjantjatjara Yankunytjatjara (APY) Lands.

It is not available if you live in other areas of South Australia.

Select the area where you live for more details about enhanced Income Management and SmartCard.
