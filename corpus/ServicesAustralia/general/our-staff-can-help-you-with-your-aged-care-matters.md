# Our staff can help you with your aged care matters

Published: 28 January 2026

We offer free face to face appointments with our service centre staff and Aged Care Specialist Officers (ACSO).

![](/sites/default/files/styles/dhs_news_article_image/public/2023-10/15176-210705d-999-182.jpg?itok=fm3GngjC)

Watch the video to find out how you can get help with:

-   general information about aged care from a Service Officer
-   in-depth detailed information from an Aged Care Specialist Officer (ACSO).

[Transcript](/sites/default/files/2025-12/transcript-services-australia-aged-care-service-offer.docx "Word document") | [Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RWh4LeZKuk8)

To book a Service Officer appointment you can:

-   use the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app
-   your Centrelink online account through myGov
-   call the Aged Care Line
-   visit a service centre.

Call the [Aged Care Line](/phone-us) or visit a service centre to make an appointment with an ACSO.
