# Activities that don’t count as work

Some activities won’t count as part of the work test for Parental Leave Pay.

Work doesn’t include any of the following:

-   unpaid work, including volunteer work
-   unpaid leave, including unpaid maternity leave
-   income support programs that include a work component
-   study, including study through a scholarship or other award of financial aid
-   paid out leave entitlements, such as paid out maternity leave or other leave entitlements. This also includes leave paid out from a redundancy.
