## Placing an order

When placing an order, tell us:

-   your full name and position
-   your organisation or business name
-   your postal and street address and postcode
-   your email details and fax and telephone numbers including area codes
-   the name, product number and quantity of each product you need.

Place your order by either:

-   calling 1800 067 307
-   emailing [publications.production@servicesaustralia.gov.au](mailto:publications.production@servicesaustralia.gov.au)
-   faxing 02 6260 2770.
