# Centrelink phone self service

Offers a range of options for you to access and update your information over the phone. You can use it 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

[

What the options are

There are 2 options for phone self service.


](/what-are-centrelink-phone-self-service-options?context=22646)

[

How to register

You can register for phone self service over the phone or in person.


](/how-to-register-for-centrelink-phone-self-service?context=22646)

[

When you call

When you call, have both your Customer Access Number (CAN) and PIN, if you haven't created a voiceprint.


](/when-you-call-centrelink-phone-self-service?context=22646)
