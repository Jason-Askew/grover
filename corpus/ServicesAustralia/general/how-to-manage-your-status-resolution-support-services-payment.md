# How to manage your payment

Your options and obligations for the Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) payment.

If you’re getting the Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) payment, there are some things you need to do while you’re getting this payment. This includes the following:

-   tell us about a [change in your circumstances](/change-circumstances-if-you-get-status-resolution-support-services-payment?context=22626)
-   report any [income you or your partner get from work](/how-to-report-your-status-resolution-support-services-payment-income?context=22626).

If you need help, you can get [help in your own language](/help-your-language-for-status-resolution-support-services-payment?context=22626).

You can also authorise someone to speak to us on your behalf.

[

Change of circumstances

You need to tell us as soon as possible if there’s a change to any of the details you gave us. Changes can affect your payment.


](/change-circumstances-if-you-get-status-resolution-support-services-payment?context=22626)

[

Report your income

If you or your partner get any income from work, you need to tell us. This is so we can pay you the right Status Resolution Support Services payment (SRSS) amount.


](/how-to-report-your-status-resolution-support-services-payment-income?context=22626)

[

Someone to deal with us for you

You can authorise someone to speak to us about your Status Resolution Support Services payment (SRSS).


](/someone-to-deal-with-us-about-your-status-resolution-support-services-payment?context=22626)

[

Help in your language

If you don’t speak English you can get help from us in your language.


](/help-your-language-for-status-resolution-support-services-payment?context=22626)
