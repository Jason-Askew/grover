# Request for information about Medicare card details accessed form (MS044)

Use this form to request information about searches made for your Medicare card details by health professionals using our online services.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Request for information about Medicare card details accessed form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/ms044-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").
