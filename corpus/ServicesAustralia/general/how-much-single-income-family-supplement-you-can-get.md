# How much you can get

How much Single Income Family Supplement (SIFS) you can receive depends on your situation.

It’s an annual payment of up to $300.

How much you’ll receive depends on your situation. This includes your household income and the number of days you were caring for an eligible child.

Use [Payment Finder](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/apps/clkonline_cof/payment-service-finder/payments-finder) to estimate how much SIFS you may be eligible for.
