# Update your family and child care details online

You can update your family and child care details through your online account.

You can view or update:

-   the addition of a newborn child to complete your claim for Paid Parental Leave or Family Tax Benefit (FTB)
-   [your child’s education details for FTB](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-childs-education-details-for-family-tax-benefit)
-   child care details and payments
-   [family income estimate and payment choices](/centrelink-online-account-help-update-your-family-income-estimate)
-   [non-lodgement of tax return](/centrelink-online-account-help-advise-non-lodgement-tax-return)
-   return to work details.

## Child Care Subsidy (CCS)

You can view or update:

-   [allocated hours for CCS](/hours-subsidised-child-care-for-child-care-subsidy)
-   attendance record for CCS
-   [withholding percentage for CCS](/centrelink-online-account-help-change-your-withholding-percentage-for-child-care-subsidy)
-   recognised participation for CCS
-   [payment balancing for CCS](/balancing-child-care-subsidy)
-   apply for [Additional Child Care Subsidy](/additional-child-care-subsidy).

You can also [confirm your child’s enrolment details for CCS](/centrelink-online-account-help-confirm-your-childs-enrolment-details-for-child-care-subsidy).
