# Aged Care Provider Portal - register, amend or remove users form (AC004)

Use this form to register, amend or remove users for the Aged Care Provider Portal if your organisation doesn’t have an Organisation Administrator.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You do not need to use this form if your service has an Organisation Administrator. Organisation Administrators can add and link staff with existing ‘A numbers’ to their service.

Download and complete the [Aged Care Provider Portal - register, amend or remove users form](/sites/default/files/2025-10/ac004-2511en-f.pdf "PDF document").

An authorised person of the approved service must complete and sign this form.

Read more about the [Aged Care Provider Portal](/aged-care-provider-portal).
