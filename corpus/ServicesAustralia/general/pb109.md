# Rheumatoid arthritis - initial authority application form (PB109)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment with an originator biological medicine for adult patients with severe active rheumatoid arthritis.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Biological medicine refers to:

-   abatacept
-   adalimumab
-   baricitinib
-   certolizumab pegol
-   etanercept
-   golimumab
-   infliximab
-   tocilizumab
-   tofacitinib
-   upadacitinib.
