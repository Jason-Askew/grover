# Extended support available for Victoria Bushfires, January 2026

Published: 23 January 2026

If you’ve been affected by this disaster, we have Australian Government Disaster Recovery Payment (AGDRP) and Disaster Recovery Allowance (DRA) to help you.

![](/sites/default/files/styles/dhs_news_article_image/public/2026-01/15296d-2601-emergency.jpg?itok=eNu_9RUx)

AGDRP is a lump sum payment to help you if you’ve been directly affected by this declared disaster.

AGDRP has been extended to the following areas in Towong Local Government Area (LGA):

-   Berringama
-   Bungil
-   Koetong
-   Shelley
-   Thologolong.

DRA is a short term allowance to help you if you’ve lost income as a direct result of this disaster.

You can get this payment for up to 13 weeks.

DRA has been extended to the following LGAs:

-   Benalla
-   Campaspe
-   Macedon Ranges
-   Wodonga
-   Yarra Ranges.
