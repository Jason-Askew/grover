# Rheumatoid arthritis - change or recommencement authority application form (PB247)

Use this form to apply to change or recommence PBS-subsidised treatment with an originator biological medicine for adult patients with severe active rheumatoid arthritis.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

You can also use this form for patients reclassified from paediatric severe active juvenile idiopathic arthritis or systemic juvenile idiopathic arthritis.

Biological medicine refers to:

-   abatacept
-   adalimumab
-   baricitinib
-   certolizumab
-   etanercept
-   golimumab
-   infliximab
-   tocilizumab
-   tofacitinib
-   upadacitinib.
