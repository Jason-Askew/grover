# Find us

Find one of our service centres, agents or access points. We can help with payment and service information for Medicare, Centrelink and Child Support.

Easter public holidays closures

Our service centres, agents, access points and most call centres close for the Easter public holidays.

This means you’ll need to do your business with us online or by phone self-service.

We’ll be closed on:

-   Good Friday, 3 April 2026
-   Easter Monday, 6 April 2026.

Read more about [public holiday reporting and payment dates](/public-holiday-reporting-and-payment-dates).

## Using online services

You can now do all of these things online through [myGov](https://my.gov.au/):

-   [confirm your identity](/how-to-prove-your-identity-with-centrelink)
-   get a [Centrelink Reference Number (CRN)](/centrelink-customer-reference-number-crn?context=64107)
-   create a [Centrelink online account](/centrelink-online-account)
-   link your Centrelink online account to myGov.

You don’t need to visit a service centre or call us to do any of these.

## Visiting us

For some services, you can now [book an appointment](/book-appointment?context=64107) at your chosen service centre.

We can [help veterans and their families at our service centres](/support-for-veterans?context=64107).

Check [customer service changes](/customer-service-changes?context=26266) to see if there is an extension or interruption to our service delivery.

[Find a service centre near you](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/)

## Visiting you

We have staff who travel to rural and regional areas in mobile services centres to help customers with our services and payments. Check [where and when our mobile service centres](/when-and-where-to-find-mobile-service-centres) are travelling to see if they’re visiting your community soon.
