# Application for bulk bill claim adjustment form (DB018)

Use this form when applying for a bulk bill claim adjustment for assigned Medicare benefits where the original date of service is less than 2 years old.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Application for bulk bill claim adjustment form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/db018-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").
