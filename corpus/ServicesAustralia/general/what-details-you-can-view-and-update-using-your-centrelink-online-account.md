# What details you can view and update online

You can view, update or add information using your online account.

You donâ€™t always need to call or visit us to change your Centrelink details. You can use your online account to do this.

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, sign in now and select **My details** to update your details.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

You can also use the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app).

You can also complete the following reviews:

-   academic reassessment
-   caring details
-   Carer Allowance income
-   Special Benefit
-   confirm or update employment income
-   study load declaration
-   weekly payments
-   Healthy Start for School
-   [income stream](/centrelink-online-account-help-complete-your-income-stream-review)
-   consent or decline the release of your information.
