## When your circumstances change

You must tell us if you do any of the following by calling the [Disability, sickness and carers](/phone-us?context=64107) line or by visiting a [service centre](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/). You:

-   stop giving care to the person you get Carer Allowance for
-   start sharing the care with someone else
-   stop sharing the care with someone else and become the main carer
-   and your current partner’s combined adjusted taxable income for the previous financial year was $250,000 or more
-   or your partner are 60 or more and get an account-based income stream.

You can tell us the following online or by calling or visiting us. You:

-   change or correct your name
-   change address
-   [travel outside Australia](/travel-outside-australia-rules-for-carer-allowance?context=21811)
-   have a [relationship change](/relationship-changes), for example you become partnered or separated.

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, sign in and select **My details**.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

If you don’t have a myGov account or a Centrelink online account you can [create them](/create-online-account).

## When circumstances change for the person you care for

You must tell us if any of the following applies to the person you provide care for by calling the [Disability, sickness and carers](/phone-us?context=64107) line or by visiting a [service centre](/find-us?context=64107). They:

-   need less care
-   move into a care facility, such as an aged care home
-   die.

You can tell us the following online or by calling or visiting us. They:

-   change or correct their name
-   stop being a dependent child
-   travel outside Australia.

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, sign in and select **My details**.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

If you don’t have a myGov account or a Centrelink online account you can [create them](/create-online-account).

To check who you’re getting a payment for, go to **My Payments** in your Centrelink online account.

## What happens if you don’t tell us

If you don’t tell us about changes in your life, we may pay you too much. If this happens you’ll have to [pay the money back](/how-to-repay-money-you-owe-to-centrelink).

If you deliberately don’t tell us about changes, you could be [committing fraud](/avoid-committing-fraud?context=21811).
