# Card renewal

You can renew your Ex-Carer Allowance (Child) Health Care Card over the phone.

Call the [Disability, sickness and carers line](#toolbar-phone) and give your details over the phone. We can renew your card straight away.

You’ll need to tell us that you are still doing full time study.

If you don’t want to give your details over the phone you will need to [submit a new claim](/how-to-claim-ex-carer-allowance-child-health-care-card?context=22691).

Contact numbers available on this page.

### Disability, sickness and carers line

Use this line if you need help with your Disability Support Pension, Special Disability Trusts, Mobility Allowance, Carer Payment, Carer Allowance or Ex-Carer Allowance (Child) Health Care Card, Health Care Card or Pensioner Concession Card.

[132 717](tel:132717)

Monday to Friday 8 am to 5 pm

There are other ways you may want to [contact us](/contact-us).
