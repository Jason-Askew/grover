# Change of circumstances

You need to tell us if your circumstances change when you get Newborn Upfront Payment and Newborn Supplement.

## on this page

-   [What to tell us](#a1)
-   [How to tell us](#a2)

## What to tell us

You need to tell us if any of the following happens:

-   you [change or correct your name](#changename)
-   you change your address
-   your [relationship changes](/relationship-changes), for example if you become partnered or separated
-   your or your partner’s income changes
-   you don’t have to lodge a tax return
-   your child leaves your care.

You also need to tell us if any of the following happens:

-   your child’s work or study load changes
-   you [leave Australia](/payments-while-outside-australia?context=22186)
-   [your care arrangements change](/changing-your-childs-care-arrangements).

You need to tell us about a change in your circumstances within 14 days. If you don’t, we may pay you too much and you may have to [pay back the money](/how-to-repay-money-you-owe-to-centrelink).

## How to tell us

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov you can tell us about changes to your circumstances online. To do this, sign in to myGov and select **My details**.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

You can also tell us using either:

-   the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app)
-   [Centrelink phone self service](/centrelink-phone-self-service).

Read more about what you can do using [self service](/centrelink-phone-self-service).

If you don’t have access to a self service option please call the [families line](#toolbar-phone).
