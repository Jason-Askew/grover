# Acute lymphoblastic leukaemia first line - dasatinib - initial (induction and consolidation) authority application form (PB271)

Use this form to apply for initial (induction and consolidation) PBS-subsidised therapy with dasatinib in the first-line treatment of acute lymphoblastic leukaemia.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Download and complete the [acute lymphoblastic leukaemia first line - dasatinib - initial (induction and consolidation) authority application form](/sites/default/files/2025-05/pb271-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").
