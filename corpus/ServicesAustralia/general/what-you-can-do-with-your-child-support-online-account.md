# What you can do

You can use your Child Support online account to check payment details and keep your personal details up to date.

## on this page

-   [Checking payment details](#a1)
-   [Updating your details](#a2)
-   [Using your account overseas](#a3)
-   [Using the Express Plus Child Support mobile app](#a4)

## Checking payment details

You can use your online account to:

-   check when your payments are due and how much you'll get
-   check how much you owe
-   calculate your support payments with the [Child Support Estimator](https://processing.csa.gov.au/estimator/About.aspx)
-   update [income for a current](/child-support-online-account-help-update-current-income) or [previous financial year](/child-support-online-account-help-update-income-for-previous-financial-year)
-   request child support [deductions from your employer](/child-support-online-account-help-request-employer-deductions)
-   request [private collection](/private-collect?context=21911) of payments
-   see past payments
-   submit documents.

## Updating your details

You can keep your details up to date. You can use your online account to update your:

-   personal and contact details
-   bank details
-   income estimate
-   nominated representative details
-   direct payments made or received
-   employer details
-   care arrangements for your children.

You can also use it to:

-   apply for a [Child support assessment](/child-support-assessment)
-   add a child or relevant dependant to an assessment
-   apply to extend the assessment for a child turning 18
-   advise of a death
-   view and print letters from us in your myGov Inbox.
