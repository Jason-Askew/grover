## Chronic condition care plans

If your patient has a chronic medical condition, they may be eligible for services under any of the following:

-   General Practitioner chronic condition management plan (GPCCMP).
-   Mental Health Case Conferences (MHCC).

Chronic medical conditions are those that have been, or are likely to be, present for at least 6 months.

Learn about [GP chronic condition management plans](/mbs-billing-rules-for-gp-chronic-condition-management-plans?context=20) and the rules for [billing MBS Items for GP chronic condition management plans](/gp-chronic-condition-management-plan?context=20).

## Continence Aids Payment Scheme (CAPS)

CAPS helps eligible people with permanent and severe incontinence with some of the costs of continence-related products.

Learn [how to help patients with CAPS](/help-patients-with-continence-aids-payment-scheme?context=20).

## Stoma Appliance Scheme (SAS)

The SAS provides free stoma appliances and products to people with a stoma.
