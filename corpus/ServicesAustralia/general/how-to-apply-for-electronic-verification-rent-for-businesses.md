# How to apply

Before you apply, you need to understand and agree to the EVoR policy, terms and procedural guide.

-   [Step 1: Before you apply](#a1)
-   [Step 2: Complete the application form](#a2)

## Step 1: Before you apply

Before you apply, download and read the [EVoR policy, terms and procedural guide](/electronic-verification-rent-policy-terms-and-procedural-guide?context=23176).

## Step 2: Complete the application form

To apply for EVoR, download and complete the [EVoR Business Application form](/sa436).

## Step 3: Complete the user details form

To complete your application you also need to complete the [Business Online Services - User details form](/sa445).

You can use it to register at least one of your staff to access [Centrelink Business Online Services](/centrelink-business-online-services) (CBOS) for EVoR.

If you're approved to use EVoR, any staff members who need to use CBOS must get their own access. They’ll need to complete the [Business Online Services - User details form](/sa445).

We'll provide log on IDs and password details for each user.

## Step 4: Submit forms

You can submit your forms and other required information by email or fax to our [Confirmation Services helpdesk](/business-and-employer-contact-information#confirmation).

## Step 5: After you apply

We may contact you to ask for more details about your application.

We'll send you a letter advising the outcome of your application.

If we approve your application, we’ll give you information about how to use EVoR. This includes how you should get a customer’s consent.

If we don’t approve your application and you think we’ve made a mistake, ask us to review our decision. To do this, contact us using the details in the letter we sent you.

Call the [Confirmation Services helpdesk](/business-and-employer-contact-information#confirmation) if you have further EVoR questions.
