# Application to register or amend a diagnostic imaging or radiation oncology practice form (HW061)

Use this form to register or amend the details of a diagnostic imaging or radiation oncology practice.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Download and complete the [Application to register or amend a diagnostic imaging or radiation oncology practice form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/hw061-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").

A separate form is required for each practice and must be signed by a proprietor or authorised representative.
