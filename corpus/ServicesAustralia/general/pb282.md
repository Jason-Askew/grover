# Juvenile idiopathic arthritis - for adult patients with onset prior to age 18 - change or recommencement authority application form (PB282)

Use this form to apply to change or recommence PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine for an adult patient with a documented history of juvenile idiopathic arthritis with onset prior to the age of 18.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Biological medicine refers to:

-   adalimumab
-   etanercept
-   tocilizumab.
