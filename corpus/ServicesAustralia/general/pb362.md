# Severe aplastic anaemia - eltrombopag - initial authority application form (PB362)

Use this form to apply for PBS-subsidised first-line treatment with eltrombopag for patients with severe aplastic anaemia.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

You can also use this form for initial PBS-subsidised second-line treatment with eltrombopag for patients with the same condition.
