# Application for a Medicare provider number and/or prescriber number for a nurse practitioner or midwife form (HW088)

Use this form to apply for an initial or additional Medicare Australia provider number or PBS prescriber number if you are an eligible midwife or nurse practitioner.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Application for a Medicare provider number and/or prescriber number for a nurse practitioner or midwife form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/hw088-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").

You must be an eligible midwife or nurse practitioner under Section 84AAF/84AAJ of the _National Health Act 1953_ to apply for a PBS prescriber number.
