# Palliative Care Schedule - opioid treatment authority application form (PB306)

Use this form to apply for increased quantities or repeats for PBS-subsidised treatment with opioids listed on the Palliative Care Schedule.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

This form is not for opioid items listed on the section 85 General Schedule.

Download and complete the [Palliative Care Schedule - opioid authority application form](/sites/default/files/2025-05/pb306-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").
