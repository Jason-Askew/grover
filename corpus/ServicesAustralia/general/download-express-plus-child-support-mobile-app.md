# Download the app

When you’ve set up your Child Support online account, you can download and use the Express Plus Child Support mobile app.

If the operating system on your device isn't up to date you can either:

-   download the latest software for your device
-   use your [Child Support online account](/child-support-online-accounts) through myGov.

To download to an iPad, change your search to **iPhone only** in the App Store.

Download the Express Plus Child Support app to your device.

 [![Download on the Apple App Store](/sites/default/files/2019-10/apple-appstore-available-medium.png)](https://itunes.apple.com/au/app/express-plus-child-support/id925638034)  [![Get it on Google Play Store](/sites/default/files/2019-10/android-app-on-google-play.png)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=au.gov.dhs.childsupport)

For the app to work, we need permission to access your:

-   phone number, this allows you to contact us while using the app
-   camera, this allows you to take photos of documents for upload.
