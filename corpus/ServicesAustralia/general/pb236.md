# Fistulising Crohn’s disease - change or recommencement (treatment break less than 5 years) authority application form (PB236)

Use this form to apply to change or recommence PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine for complex refractory fistulising Crohn’s disease, where recommencement is after a treatment break less than 5 years.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Biological medicine refers to adalimumab, infliximab and ustekinumab.

Please note: You don’t need to complete this form if you use the [Online PBS Authorities system](/apply-for-pbs-authority#accordion1).

Download and complete the [fistulising Crohn’s disease - change or recommencement (treatment break less than 5 years) authority application form](/sites/default/files/2025-11/pb236-2512en-f.pdf "PDF document").
