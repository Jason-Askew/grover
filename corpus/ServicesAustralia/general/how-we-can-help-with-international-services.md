# How we can help

You may be able to get a pension from Australia or another country under an agreement. You may also get your current payment if you're going overseas.

## on this page

-   [Social security agreements](#a1)
-   [Getting your foreign pension](#a2)
-   [Travel overseas](#a3)
-   [Languages](#a4)
-   [Contact us](#a5)

## Social security agreements

We can help you:

-   find out if you can get an Australian pension overseas
-   claim a pension from another country while you live in Australia.

Read about [international social security agreements](/social-security-agreements-for-international-services?context=22471).

## Getting your foreign pension

If you live in Australia and get a foreign pension, public holidays may cause a delay in getting your payment. Please call your overseas pension authority during their opening hours for information about their payments.

## Travel overseas

If you travel outside Australia it may affect your Centrelink payments.

## Languages

We have:

-   staff you can speak to in a range of languages
-   [interpreters](/information-your-language#interpretertranslation) you can use.

## Contact us

If you’re in Australia, call our [International services line](#toolbar-phone).

If you’re outside Australia either:

-   call one of our [international phone numbers](#toolbar-phone)
-   [write or fax us](/write-to-us).

Calls to some international phone numbers are free.

Contact numbers available on this page.
