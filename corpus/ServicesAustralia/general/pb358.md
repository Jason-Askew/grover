# Transthyretin amyloid cardiomyopathy - tafamidis - initial authority application form (PB358)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment or initial grandfathered PBS-subsidised treatment with tafamidis for patients with transthyretin amyloid cardiomyopathy.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.
