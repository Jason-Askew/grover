# Someone to deal with us for you

You can authorise someone to speak to us about your Status Resolution Support Services payment (SRSS).

You can authorise someone to speak about your payment and details with us. We will need your approval before we can give them your information.

To do this, you will need to complete the [Status Resolution Support Services Payment authorising a person or organisation to enquire on your behalf form](/su698).
