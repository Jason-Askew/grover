# Advice of a likely payment of debt form (CS4456)

Use this form to advise us whether an outstanding child support debt is likely to be paid for a company that is in administration or liquidation.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Advice of a likely payment of debt form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/cs4456-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").
