# How to manage your payment

Your options and obligations for Austudy.

Getting help managing regular deductions from your payment

You can arrange regular deductions from your Centrelink payments to pay bills. Read more about [Centrepay](/centrepay).

[

Change of circumstances

You must tell us if things change as it might affect your Austudy payment.


](/change-circumstances-could-affect-your-austudy?context=22441)

[

Employment income reporting

You must tell us the gross employment income you and your partner get so we can pay you the right amount.


](/employment-income-reporting?context=22441)

[

Prepare to transfer to Age Pension

If you get Austudy and you’re Age Pension age, you can apply to transfer to Age Pension.


](/prepare-to-transfer-to-age-pension-if-you-get-austudy?context=22441)

[

Travel outside Australia

There are rules about when you can get Austudy when you travel outside Australia.


](/travel-outside-australia-rules-for-austudy?context=22441)

[

Someone to act for you

You can ask someone to act for you with Medicare, Centrelink, aged care or Child Support. You can authorise them to speak to us, update your details and get payments for you.


](/someone-to-act-for-you-with-medicare-centrelink-aged-care-or-child-support?context=22441)

[

Overdrawn bank account

You can get help to access your money if you have an overdrawn bank account.


](/overdrawn-bank-account?context=22441)

[

Online review of study details

We may send a letter to your myGov inbox asking you to do an online review of your study details.


](/online-review-study-details?context=22441)

[

Cancelling your payment online

You may be able to cancel your own payment online. This means you don’t need to visit a service centre or call us.


](/cancelling-your-payment-online?context=22441)
