# Customer service changes

Sometimes there may be changes to our service delivery. This can be because of a natural disaster, public holiday or system maintenance.

## Face to face

### Temporary service centre closures

You can manage most of your business with us [online](/self-service) or by [calling us](/phone-us).

**Northern Territory**

[Hermannsburg](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/findnearest.asp?locationid=543843&submittopage=locatorprofile.asp&log=1), 9 Raberaba Circuit, Hermannsburg NT 0872.

**Queensland**

[Fortitude Valley](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/findnearest.asp?locationid=557710&submittopage=locatorprofile.asp&log=1), 728 Ann Street, Fortitude Valley QLD 4006.

### Easter public holidays closures

Our service centres, agents, access points and most call centres close for the Easter public holidays.

This means you’ll need to do your business with us online or by phone self-service.

We’ll be closed on:

-   Good Friday, 3 April 2026
-   Easter Monday, 6 April 2026.

Read more about [public holiday reporting and payment dates](/public-holiday-reporting-and-payment-dates).
