# Change of circumstances

We need to know about changes that could stop you from having a Commonwealth Seniors Health Card.

## on this page

-   [How and when to tell us](#a1)
-   [What happens if you don’t tell us](#a2)

You need to tell us if any of the following happens:

-   you [change or correct your name](#changename)
-   your personal circumstances change
-   you change your address
-   your income changes
-   you return to work
-   you don’t need to lodge a tax return
-   there are changes in care for your dependent children
-   you’re [leaving Australia](/travel-outside-australia-rules-for-commonwealth-seniors-health-card?context=21966).

## How and when to tell us

You need to tell us if your circumstances change within 14 days.

You can’t tell us about any of these changes online:

-   income changes
-   returning to work
-   that you don’t need to lodge a tax return.

To tell us about all other changes you can:

-   update your circumstances online
-   call us on the [Centrelink Older Australians Line](#toolbar-phone)
-   visit a [service centre](https://findus.servicesaustralia.gov.au/)

To do this, sign in to myGov using the link below and select **My details** from the top of the page.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

If you don’t have a myGov account or a Centrelink online account and want to use online services, you’ll need to [create an online account](/create-online-account).

### Changing or correcting your name

It’s important to tell us if you [change your legal name](/how-to-update-your-name-with-centrelink).

## What happens if you don’t tell us

If you don’t tell us about changes in your life, that could affect your eligibility for a card, we could charge you with fraud.

Find out [how to avoid fraud](/avoid-committing-fraud?context=21966).
