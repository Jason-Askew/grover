# Protocol for Lightweight Authentication of Identity (PLAID)

The PLAID protocol is cryptographically stronger, faster and more private, than most or all equivalent protocols currently available either commercially or via existing standards.

## Services Australia Reference Implementation

[PLAID Reference Implementation - Services Australia](/plaid-reference-implementation-services-australia?context=22) **version 8.04** is available for download.

The Protocol for Lightweight Authentication of Identity (PLAID) smartcard authentication protocol:

-   provides access to public PLAID protocol information, activities and standards
-   explains Services Australia's role and policy to industry and government domestically and internationally
-   ensures that the Australian Commonwealth Intellectual Property is clearly defined and publicly available
-   assists industry and governments in the commercialisation of PLAID through development of a COTS (commercial, off the shelf) product.
