# Lymphoma - small lymphocytic lymphoma

The Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) subsidises idelalisib for patients with small lymphocytic lymphoma (SLL).

## on this page

-   [Patient eligibility](#a1)
-   [Authority applications](#a2)
-   [More information](#a3)

## Patient eligibility

The PBS subsidises treatment with idelalisib under the _National Health Act 1953_, section 85 for patients with SLL.

Patients must be eligible for the [PBS](/pharmaceutical-benefits-scheme) and meet the relevant restriction criteria.

The [Schedule of Pharmaceutical Benefits](https://www.pbs.gov.au/pbs/home;jsessionid=j9866ap5cxd4xambews72vmw) on the [PBS website](http://www.pbs.gov.au/pbs/home) outlines the restrictions for prescribing idelalisib.

## Authority applications

### Applying for initial treatment

To apply for initial authority approval to prescribe PBS-subsidised idelalisib to treat SLL either:

-   use the [Online PBS Authorities](/apply-for-pbs-authority?context=20) in HPOS
-   call the [PBS authority approvals line](/health-professionals-contact-information?context=20#pbsauthority).

### Applying for continuing treatment

To apply for continuing authority approval to prescribe PBS-subsidised idelalisib to treat SLL either:

-   use [Online PBS Authorities](/apply-for-pbs-authority?context=20) in HPOS
-   call the [PBS authority approvals line](/health-professionals-contact-information?context=20#pbsauthority).
