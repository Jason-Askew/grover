# Severe asthma paediatric - change or continuing authority application form (PB385)

Use this form to apply to change PBS-subsidised treatment with dupilumab or omalizumab, or to continue PBS-subsidised treatment with dupilumab for patients 6 to under 12 years with uncontrolled severe asthma or uncontrolled severe allergic asthma.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.
