# Disability Support Pension Medical Evidence Checklist for treating health professionals form (SA478)

Treating health professionals can use this form to help patients provide medical evidence. It helps to check that there is relevant evidence for a DSP claim or medical review.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Disability Support Pension Medical Evidence Checklist for treating health professionals form](/sites/default/files/2026-01/sa478-2601en-f.pdf "PDF document").
