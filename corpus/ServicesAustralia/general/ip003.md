# Practice Incentives individual general practitioner or nurse practitioner details form (IP003)

Use this form to register, cease or amend details for a general practitioner or nurse practitioner with a PIP or WIP - Practice Stream practice.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Practice Incentives individual general practitioner or nurse practitioner details form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/ip003-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").

PIP is the Practice Incentives Program.

WIP - Practice Stream is the Workforce Incentive Program - Practice Stream.
