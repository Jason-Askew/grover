# View and manage an existing authority

View, amend or cancel an existing authority using the Online PBS Authorities (OPA) system on Health Professional Online Services (HPOS).

You can search for existing authorities you’ve previously requested and check patient history and previous application details.

To do this use any of the following:

-   a Medicare number
-   your patient’s details
-   an Authority Prescription Number, Authority Approval Number or Authority Unique ID.

Access the Online PBS Authorities system through either:

-   upgraded clinical and prescribing software
-   Health Professional Online Services (HPOS).

You can access this service in HPOS with a [Provider Digital Access (PRODA](/provider-digital-access-proda)) account. If you don’t have one, [register for a PRODA account](/how-to-register-for-individual-proda-account). Then follow the steps to set up your HPOS access.

View existing authorities in HPOS

To view your patient’s existing authority records, follow these steps:

1.  Log on to HPOS and select **PBS authorities** from the My programs menu.
2.  Select **Search authorities**, then enter any of the following:
    -   your patient’s Medicare number
    -   your patient’s details
    -   an authority number, authority approval number or authority unique ID.
3.  After entering information, select **Search**.
4.  Select **View** action link to see the details of an authority application.

[Log in to PRODA](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au:5447/sso/sps/oidc/rp/HPOSWEB/kickoff/proda1)

Amend existing authorities in HPOS

You can change an authority record that hasn’t yet been dispensed. You **can’t** amend an authority record that has a status of Awaiting Assessment or where the original prescription has been supplied. However you can stop the supply of any outstanding repeats.

If you change your patient’s details, the PBS item details, or restriction information, your authority will be reassessed.

To amend your patient’s existing authority prescriptions, follow these steps:

1.  Log on to HPOS and select **PBS authorities** from the My programs menu.
2.  Select **Search authorities**, then enter any of the following:
    -   your patient’s Medicare number
    -   your patient’s details
    -   an authority number, authority approval number or authority unique ID.
3.  After entering information, select **Search**.
4.  Select **View** action link to view the details of an authority record or to block remaining repeats from being dispensed.
5.  Select the **Amend** action link, then **Edit** to make any of the following changes:
    -   patient details
    -   PBS item details
    -   restriction details
    -   questions and answers previously selected
    -   attach supporting documentation
    -   Authority Prescription Number.
6.  Once you have made the necessary amendments or changes, select **Submit**.

[Log in to PRODA](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au:5447/sso/sps/oidc/rp/HPOSWEB/kickoff/proda1)

Cancel existing authorities

You can cancel an approved authority that hasn’t yet been dispensed. You **can’t** cancel authority that have already had an original prescription supplied, however you can stop the supply of any outstanding repeats.

To cancel your patient’s existing authority prescription, follow these steps:

1.  Log on to HPOS and select **PBS authorities** from the My programs menu.
2.  Select **Search authorities**, then enter any of the following:
    -   your patient’s Medicare number
    -   your patient’s details
    -   an authority number, authority approval number or authority unique ID.
3.  After entering information, select **Search**.
4.  Select **Amend** action link on the Search Results section of the Authority Search page.
5.  Select **Cancel Application**.

[Log in to PRODA](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au:5447/sso/sps/oidc/rp/HPOSWEB/kickoff/proda1)

Block an authority in HPOS

You can block the supply of any outstanding repeats of an approved authority that has had an original prescription supplied.

Only the original prescriber can block an authority record. You won’t see the blocked authority straight away as it’s updated overnight in the PBS claims system. The system displays the date from when the block is in place. You’ll need to call [PBS authority approvals](/health-professionals-contact-information?context=20#pbsauthority) to unblock an authority.

To block your patient’s existing authority prescription, follow these steps:

1.  Log on to HPOS and select **PBS authorities** from the My programs menu.
2.  Select **Search authorities**, then enter any of the following:
    -   your patient’s Medicare number
    -   your patient’s details
    -   an authority number, authority approval number or authority unique ID.
3.  After entering information, select **Search**.
4.  Select **View** from the Search Results section on the Authority Search page.
5.  Select **Block**.

[Log in to PRODA](https://www2.medicareaustralia.gov.au:5447/sso/sps/oidc/rp/HPOSWEB/kickoff/proda1)
