# Notice of withdrawal of application for a child support assessment form (CS1669)

Use this form to withdraw an application for assessment of child support at any time before the Child Support Registrar accepts the application.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Notice of withdrawal of application for a child support assessment form](/sites/default/files/2023-11/cs1669-2310en-f.pdf "PDF document").
