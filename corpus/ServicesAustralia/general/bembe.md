# Bembe

We provide information in your language to help you learn more about our payments and services.

Tulapeela ifyebo mu citundu cenu pakumyafwilishako ukwishibilapo nafimbi pa malipilo ne milimo yesu.

Ngacakuti mulebomfya ifibombelo fya kumyafwilishako elyo mwafilwa ukusanga ifyebo fyesu ifyayalwilwa mu citundu cenu, kuti mwatutumina [mu citundu cenu](/extra-help-when-calling-us).

If you are using assistive technology and cannot access our translated information, you can contact us [in your language](/extra-help-when-calling-us).
