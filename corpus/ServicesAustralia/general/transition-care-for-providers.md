# Transition care for providers

Transition care program provides support to people in aged care services.

Transition care is for older Australians who need a different care approach to residential or support at home services.

You can read about [Transition care services](https://www.health.gov.au/our-work/transition-care-programme) on the Department of Health, Disability and Ageing website.

You can use the [Aged Care Provider Portal (ACPP)](/aged-care-provider-portal?context=20) to do business with us.
