# Confirm your relationship status

Learn how to confirm your relationship if you or your partner make an online claim.

If your partner is making a claim for a payment, we need to know their relationship status. This means they may need you to confirm your relationship status with us.

If they ask you to do this, there are 2 options available, depending on the situation. The online claim will tell your partner which option you can use.

You can use either:

-   the [Partner Confirmation Logon](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/authsvc/policy/tac&portal=DMODP) service online
-   the [Partner details form](/mod-p?context=60029) that both you and your partner need to complete and return.
