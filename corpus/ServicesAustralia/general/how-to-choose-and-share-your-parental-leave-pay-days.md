Parental Leave Pay is a flexible payment that can be shared between parents.

You can take your Parental Leave Pay days in a way that works for you. If you have a partner, there are a number of days reserved for them to use.

You can take your Parental Leave Pay days as any of the following:

-   a single block
-   multiple smaller blocks
-   single days
-   smaller blocks combined with single days.

You can [choose how to take your payment](/choose-how-to-take-your-parental-leave-pay?context=64479). Learn about how you can [share your payment](/sharing-your-parental-leave-pay?context=64479).

If you’ve already submitted your claim, you can [change your nominated days](/changing-your-nominated-days-for-parental-leave-pay?context=64479).

If you share your Parental Leave Pay days with someone else, you can take some days at the same time. There are some [exemptions that apply to this limit](/exemptions-to-limit-for-taking-days-parental-leave-pay-same-time?context=64479).
