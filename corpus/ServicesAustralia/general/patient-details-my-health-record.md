# Patient details in My Health Record

Health professionals can access patient details in My Health Record. There are steps you need to take to set this up.

There are some things you as a health professional need before you can access your patient’s My Health Record. This includes all the following:

-   Set up your [access to HPOS](/connect-to-hpos?context=20).
-   Read and agree to the terms and conditions of using HPOS.
-   Register [on the organisational register](/register-organisation-register-hpos?context=20).

Learn more about [how to access your patient’s details in My Health Record](/access-your-patients-details-my-health-record?context=20).
