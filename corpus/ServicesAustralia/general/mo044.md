# Statutory declaration ROCS - Midwife who has not engaged in a private practice during the preceding 3 years form (MO044)

Use this form to declare for the Midwife Professional Indemnity Run-Off Cover Scheme (MPIROCS) that you’re an eligible midwife who hasn’t engaged in private practice during the preceding 3 years.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Statutory declaration ROCS - Midwife engaged in a private practice form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/mo044-2507en-f.pdf "PDF document").

The MPIROCS only applies to claims or incidents first notified on or after 1 July 2010.
