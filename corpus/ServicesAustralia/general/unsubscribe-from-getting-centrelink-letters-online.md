# Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe from getting Centrelink letters online. We'll send letters to your postal address instead.

## on this page

-   [How to unsubscribe](#a1)
-   [How to re-subscribe](#a2)

## How to unsubscribe

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, you can unsubscribe online.

To do this:

1.  Sign in to myGov.
2.  Select **My Details**, then **Letters and messaging**.
3.  Select **Electronic messaging and letters subscription** and follow the prompts.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

## How to re-subscribe

If you want to get your letters online again, you can re-subscribe at any time.

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, sign in now to re-subscribe.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

You can also use the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app).
