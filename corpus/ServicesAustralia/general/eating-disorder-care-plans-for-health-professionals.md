# Eating disorder care plans

Steps to create and manage an Eating Disorder Treatment and Management Plan (EDP).

## Create and manage an Eating Disorder Treatment and Management Plan (EDP)

**Step 1**. Find out the [requirements for an Eating Disorder Treatment and Management Plan](/requirements-eating-disorder-health-care-plan?context=20).

We have information on:

-   health professional eligibility
-   patient eligibility
-   billing requirements
-   referrals to allied health professionals.

**Step 2**. Provide the service to your patient.

**Step 3**. Bill services using the correct Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS) item number.

Read about [billing an Eating Disorder Treatment and Management Plans](/mbs-billing-rules-for-eating-disorder-treatment-and-management-plans?context=20).

Check eligibility and look up item numbers through the [MBS items online checker in HPOS](/check-mbs-item-numbers?context=20). Read about the relevant MBS item descriptions at [MBS Online](http://www.mbsonline.gov.au/) website.

You can [view a patientâ€™s care plan history in HPOS](/patient-details-hpos?context=20). Your patient can also view and track their care plan services through myGov.

**Step 4**. Review and manage the plan.
