# What can affect your payments

Understand what can affect your Parental Leave Pay.

A number of things can affect your Parental Leave Pay.

To get Parental Leave Pay, you must be on leave or not working. If you work for more than one hour, we consider this work.

Read more about [choosing how to take your payment](/choose-how-to-take-your-parental-leave-pay?context=64479).

There are rules about how long you can get Parental Leave Pay when you [travel outside Australia](/travel-outside-australia-if-you-get-parental-leave-pay?context=64479).

[

If you work on a Parental Leave Pay day

Parental Leave Pay is paid for days when you’re not working.


](/if-you-work-day-parental-leave-pay?context=64479)

[

If you’re self-employed and work on a Parental Leave Pay day

If you're self-employed, you must not work on any day you choose to get Parental Leave Pay.


](/if-youre-self-employed-and-work-parental-leave-pay-day?context=64479)

Effect on your existing payments and entitlements

Parental Leave Pay is part of your taxable income. It can affect your tax obligations and some payments and services.

Travel outside Australia

There are rules about when you can get Parental Leave Pay when you travel outside Australia.
