# Non-small cell lung cancer - crizotinib or entrectinib - initial authority application form (PB156)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment with crizotinib or entrectinib for Stage IIIB (locally advanced) or Stage IV (metastatic) non-small cell lung cancer.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.
