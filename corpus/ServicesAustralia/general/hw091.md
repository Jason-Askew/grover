# Simplified Billing manual claims batch header form (HW091)

Use this form to submit manual Simplified Billing claims only.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Simplified Billing manual claims batch header form](/sites/default/files/2025-09/hw091-2508en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Private health insurers or approved billing agents can use this form.
