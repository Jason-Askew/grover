# Payment and Service Finder

An online tool to see what payments and services you may be eligible for.

Payment and Service Finder can help you work out amounts of:

-   Centrelink payments, including pensions and allowances
-   Family Tax Benefit
-   Child care fee assistance.

To work out how much money you may get, answer the questions and choose a payment you want to estimate.

[Payment and Service Finder](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/apps/clkonline_cof/payment-service-finder/payment-service-finder-start)
