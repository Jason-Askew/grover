# Severe asthma - adolescent and adult - initial or recommencement after 12 months break authority application form (PB075)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine for patients 12 years or over with uncontrolled severe asthma.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

You can also use this form to apply to recommence PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine in a new treatment cycle following a mandatory 12-month break.

Biological medicine refers to:

-   benralizumab
-   dupilumab
-   mepolizumab
-   omalizumab.
