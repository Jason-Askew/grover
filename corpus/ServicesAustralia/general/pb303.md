# Pulmonary arterial hypertension - initial dual or triple therapy authority application form (PB303)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised dual or triple therapy treatment for pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH).

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Please note: You donâ€™t need to complete this form if you use the [Online PBS Authorities system](/apply-for-pbs-authority).

You can apply for dual or triple therapy PAH treatment with either:

-   one endothelin receptor antagonist (ERA) and one prostanoid
-   one ERA and one phosphodiesterase-5 inhibitor (PDE-5i)
-   one PDE-5i and one prostanoid
-   one ERA, one PDE-5i and one prostanoid.
