# Centrepay for businesses

A bill paying service for your customers to pay your business, direct from their Centrelink payments.

Customers can request eligible Centrepay deductions and we’ll send their payments to your business by secure electronic transfer.

## Notice of variation to Centrepay Procedural Guide and Policy and Terms

We‘ve varied the Centrepay Policy and Terms and Centrepay Procedural Guide for Businesses. Learn more about this and find relevant documents on [Centrepay Terms of Use and Policy](/centrepay-policy-terms-and-guides?context=23256).
