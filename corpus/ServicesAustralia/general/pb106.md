# Psoriatic arthritis - continuing authority application form (PB106)

Use this form to apply to continue PBS-subsidised treatment with a biological medicine for adult patients with severe psoriatic arthritis.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Biological medicine refers to:

-   adalimumab
-   bimekizumab
-   certolizumab pegol
-   etanercept
-   golimumab
-   guselkumab
-   infliximab
-   ixekizumab
-   risankizumab
-   secukinumab
-   tofacitinib
-   upadacitinib
-   ustekinumab.
