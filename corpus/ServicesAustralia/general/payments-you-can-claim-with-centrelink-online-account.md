# What payments you can claim online

You can claim most payments online.

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, sign in now to claim the payments or services listed below.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

Group

Payment type

Carers

Payments you can claim include:

-   [Carer Payment](/how-to-claim-carer-payment)
-   [Carer Allowance](/how-to-claim-carer-allowance).

Families

Payments you can claim include:

-   [Child Care Subsidy](/child-care-subsidy)
-   [Stillborn Baby Payment](/stillborn-baby-payment)
-   [Family Tax Benefit](/family-tax-benefit)
-   [Parental Leave Pay](/parental-leave-pay)
-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment)
-   [Single Income Family Supplement](/how-to-claim-single-income-family-supplement).

Job seekers

Payments you can claim include:

-   [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Youth Allowance](/youth-allowance).

Older Australians

Payments you can claim include:

-   [Age Pension](/age-pension)
-   [Transfer to Age Pension](/transfer-to-age-pension)
-   [Home Equity Access Scheme](/home-equity-access-scheme).

You can also apply for an [aged care calculation of your cost of care](/aged-care-calculation-your-cost-care).

People with disability

Payments you can claim include:

-   [Mobility Allowance](/mobility-allowance)
-   [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Essential Medical Equipment](/essential-medical-equipment-payment)
-   [Disability Support Pension](/disability-support-pension).

Students and trainees

Payments you can claim include:

-   [Youth Allowance](/youth-allowance)
-   [Austudy](/austudy)
-   [ABSTUDY Living Allowance](/abstudy-living-allowance)
-   [ABSTUDY Incidentals Allowance](/abstudy-incidentals-allowance)
-   [Pensioner Education Supplement](/pensioner-education-supplement).

Rural and remote Australians

You can claim [Farm Household Allowance](/farm-household-allowance).

Concession and health care cards

Cards you can claim include:

-   [Commonwealth Seniors Health Care Card](/commonwealth-seniors-health-card)
-   [Ex-Carer Allowance (Child) Health Care Card](/ex-carer-allowance-child-health-care-card)
-   [Foster Child Health Care Card](/foster-child-health-care-card)
-   [Low Income Health Care Card](/low-income-health-care-card).

You can also [renew your Low Income Health Care Card](/renewing-low-income-health-care-card).

You can find, estimate and compare payments using our [Payment Finder](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/apps/clkonline_cof/payment-service-finder/payments-finder).
