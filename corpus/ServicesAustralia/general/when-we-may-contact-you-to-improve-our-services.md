# When we may contact you

There may be times we engage with you to improve our services or for a payment accuracy review.

This is a different process to when we send you mail, [online letters](/centrelink-letters-online) or [electronic messaging](/electronic-messaging) about your payments.

Using research and surveys to improve our services

Payment Accuracy Reviews

We review your details to make sure you’re getting the right payment and payment amount. If you have a partner, we’ll also review their details.
