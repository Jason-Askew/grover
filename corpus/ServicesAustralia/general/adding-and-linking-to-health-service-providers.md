# Linking to health service providers

Learn how to add and link to health service providers in PRODA to access their services.

To access services for certain health service providers, you need to add and link to them in PRODA.

Read our online guides for:

-   [Adding and linking to Medicare Online (including ECLIPSE, DVA and AIR)](/adding-and-linking-to-medicare-online-proda?context=33786)
-   [Adding and linking to PBS Online](/adding-and-linking-to-pbs-online-proda?context=33786)
-   [Adding and linking to HPOS for PBS](/adding-and-linking-to-hpos-for-pbs?context=33786)
-   [Adding and linking to HPOS for AIR](/adding-and-linking-to-hpos-for-air?context=33786)
-   [Adding and linking to HPOS for Patient Verification (PV)](/adding-and-linking-to-hpos-for-patient-verification?context=33786)
-   [Adding and linking to HPOS for Organisation Register](/register-organisation-register-hpos#accordion1).

You’ll need to complete the linking process if you use a web services ready software product. If you use Medicare Online, check with your software developer if you need to do this.

You’ll also need to complete the linking process if you want to access PBS, AIR or Patient Verification through HPOS.

Linking establishes a relationship between your PRODA organisation and the health service provider you want to use.

You’ll need to have [certain information ready](/information-you-need-before-you-start?context=33786) before you start the adding and linking process in PRODA.
