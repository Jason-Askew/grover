# Employment Separation Certificate form (SU001)

You can ask your previous employer to use this form to certify important employment information about you.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

They can download and complete the [Employment Separation Certificate](/sites/default/files/2024-02/su001-240228en-f.pdf "PDF document") and either:

-   give it to you
-   submit online to us.

[Employers](/employment-separation-certificates-for-employers) can read more about Employment Separation Certificates.
