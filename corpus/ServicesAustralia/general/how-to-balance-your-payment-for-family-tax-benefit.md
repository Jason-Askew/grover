# How to balance your payment

We compare your income estimate with your actual income at the end of each financial year. This is how we check you got the right amount.

Call the [Centrelink Indigenous Call Centre](/centrelink-indigenous-call-centre?context=64107) to speak to us about Centrelink payments and services for free.

If you get Family Tax Benefit (FTB) or Child Care Subsidy (CCS), we need to balance your payments. We do this separately for each payment. Find out more about:

-   [balancing for CCS](/balancing-child-care-subsidy)
-   [balancing for FTB](/balancing-family-tax-benefit?context=22151).

The financial year runs from 1 July to 30 June. After 30 June, we confirm the amount of income you got during the financial year. We then compare this with the income estimate you gave us. We do this to check that we paid you the right amount. This is what we call balancing your payments.

Most families need to confirm their income so we can start the balancing process. Not everyone has to do the same thing for us to balance their payments.

If you got an income support payment we may [balance your FTB without you doing anything](/balancing-dates-if-you-were-single-and-got-income-support?context=22151).
