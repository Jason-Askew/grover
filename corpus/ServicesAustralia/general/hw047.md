# HI Service - application to amend a contracted service provider officer's details form (HW047)

Use this form to amend the personal details of a Contracted Service Provider (CSP) officer’s details within the Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [HI Service - application to amend a contracted service provider officer’s details form](/sites/default/files/2024-12/hw047-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").
