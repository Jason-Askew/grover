# Application for approval, amendment or renewal of an approved pathology authority form (HW011)

Use this form if you are a pathology authority applying for acceptance as an Approved Pathology Authority under section 23DF of the Health Insurance Act 1973.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Download and complete the [Application for approval, amendment or renewal of an approved pathology authority form](/sites/default/files/2025-09/hw011-2509en-f.pdf "PDF document").
