# How to manage your allowance

Your options and obligations when getting ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance higher rate for you or your child.

There are things you need to do to manage your allowance. This includes telling us about any:

-   [changes to study](/changes-to-study-if-you-get-abstudy-school-fees-allowance-home-rate?context=38071)
-   changes to your or your child’s circumstances.

You can also choose someone to deal with us on your behalf. This might be giving your child’s school or hostel [permission to enquire](/someone-to-act-for-you-with-medicare-centrelink-aged-care-or-child-support?context=38071#personpermittedtoenquire).

[

Changes to study

There may be penalties if a student changes or stops their study after we’ve paid School Fees Allowance higher rate.


](/changes-to-study-if-you-get-abstudy-school-fees-allowance-higher-rate?context=64831)

[

Change of circumstances

You need to tell us if your circumstances change when you get ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance higher rate.


](/change-circumstances-if-you-get-abstudy-school-fees-allowance-higher-rate?context=64831)

[

Someone to act for you

You can ask someone to act for you with Medicare, Centrelink, aged care or Child Support. You can authorise them to speak to us, update your details and get payments for you.


](/someone-to-act-for-you-with-medicare-centrelink-aged-care-or-child-support?context=64831)
