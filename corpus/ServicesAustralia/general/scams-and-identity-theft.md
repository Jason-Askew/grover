## Scams that pretend to be us

A scam is a trick designed to gain access to your money or personal information. Scammers pretend to be from Services Australia, including myGov, PRODA, Medicare, Child Support or Centrelink. They may send a text message, email, or social media message with a link to a fake myGov website that looks real.

If you enter your myGov sign in details on a fake website, scammers can use that information to sign in to your real myGov account. They can then try to steal your money or personal information. Read about [how scams work](/how-scams-work?context=60271).

This does not mean your myGov account has been hacked.

Hacking is when criminals force their way into an online system or network to control or steal data. We’re committed to protecting Australians from scams and identity theft. Read more about [how we protect your myGov account](https://my.gov.au/en/about/privacy-and-security/security/how-we-protect-your-mygov-account).

## If you’ve been scammed

Our staff can offer support and advice about how to protect yourself and your personal information. We can organise an interpreter when you call if you need help in your language.

Call our [Scams and Identity Theft Helpdesk](#toolbar-phone) immediately if you’ve:

-   opened a link in a suspicious text message, email or social media message
-   given someone your myGov sign in details or other personal information
-   visited a website that pretended to be us or downloaded a fake app.

Our staff can also support you if you’re affected by identity theft and either of the following apply to you:

-   you’ve had your identity documents or myGov, Centrelink or Medicare information exposed in a third-party data breach
-   someone is using your personal information to pretend to be you.

## If you see a Services Australia or myGov scam

To report a scam related to myGov or Services Australia, including Centrelink, Medicare, or Child Support, you can email [reportascam@servicesaustralia.gov.au](mailto:reportascam@servicesaustralia.gov.au).

Read more about [how to report a scam to us](/report-services-australia-or-mygov-scam?context=60271).
