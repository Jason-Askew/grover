# Private Trust form (MOD PT)

Use this form if in the last 5 years, you or your partner have been involved with or have transferred funds or assets to a private trust that has not been vested.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Private Trust form](/sites/default/files/mod-pt-2005en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Also use this form if in the last 5 years, you or your partner have made any type of gifts involving an active or vested private trust.

You do not need to complete this form if it is a Special Disability Trust or Complying Self Managed Superannuation Fund.
