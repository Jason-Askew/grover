# Planning long term

We have resources that can help you with long-term planning when you’re unemployed.

## on this page

-   [Help with claiming early](#a1)
-   [Help with paying bills](#a2)
-   [Help getting back to work](#a3)
-   [Help with a career change](#a4)
-   [Help with your mental health](#a5)

## Help with claiming early

You can start your [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment) or [Youth Allowance for job seekers](/youth-allowance-for-job-seekers) claim up to 13 weeks before your circumstances change. You can do this before:

-   you lose your job
-   your work hours reduce.

## Help with paying bills

When you’re unemployed, keeping on top of bills and other expenses can be stressful. We provide information that can help you [manage your bills](/manage-your-bills-and-money-when-recently-unemployed?context=60064).

## Help getting back to work

We have resources to help you look for work or re-enter the workforce when you’ve found your job.

Find information about how we can help when you’re [looking for work](/looking-for-work).

## Help with a career change

This might be a great time to learn a new skill or study. We have information to support you if you choose to:

-   [start further study at university or TAFE](/higher-education)
-   [start an apprenticeship or traineeship](/apprenticeship)
-   [retrain and improve your skills](/retraining).

## Help with your mental health

Being unemployed can be a stressful time. We have information to [help you with the cost of some mental health treatments](/mental-health-care-and-medicare?context=60092). We can [help you find support online and by phone](/mental-health-care-online-and-phone?context=60092).
