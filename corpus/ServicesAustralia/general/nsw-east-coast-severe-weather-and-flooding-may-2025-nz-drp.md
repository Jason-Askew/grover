# New Zealand Disaster Recovery Payment

A lump sum payment that was available to help eligible New Zealanders affected by New South Wales east coast severe weather and flooding that started in May 2025.

You had until 2 December 2025 to make a claim for the following Local Government Areas (LGAs):

-   Bellingen
-   Central Coast
-   Coffs Harbour
-   Dungog
-   Kempsey
-   Lake Macquarie
-   Maitland
-   Mid-Coast
-   Nambucca Valley
-   Newcastle
-   Port Macquarie-Hastings
-   Port Stephens.

Late claims will only be accepted in exceptional circumstances. To claim, call the [Emergency information line](/phone-us?context=64107#emergency).
