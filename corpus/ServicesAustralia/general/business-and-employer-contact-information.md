## National Business Gateway

The National Business Gateway is a national contact centre that works directly with the Australian business community. This helps deliver integrated services for Australian businesses.

They can help you:

-   use [Centrelink Business Online Services](/centrelink-business-online-services), issue log on IDs and reset passwords
-   complete forms, including questionnaires, work details reports, employment separation certificates and employer contact certificates
-   arrange an extension if you need more time to give us details we’ve asked for
-   find strategies and options to help you meet your reporting requirements
-   [retrench](/redundancy-information-for-employers) staff
-   refer you to services from other government departments and agencies.
