For Centrelink payments and services, you can call our [multilingual phone service](/phone-us#languages).

To get this payment all the following must apply. You must:

-   be caring for a newborn or recently adopted child
-   have met [the income test](/meeting-income-test-for-parental-leave-pay?context=64479)
-   not be working on your Parental Leave Pay days, except for allowable reasons
-   have met [the work test](/meeting-work-test-for-parental-leave-pay?context=64479)
-   meet the [residency rules](/residence-rules-for-parental-leave-pay?context=64479)
-   have registered or applied to register your child’s birth with your state or territory birth registry, if they’re a newborn.

There are additional requirements to get Parental Leave Pay. Read more about [who can get it](/who-can-get-parental-leave-pay?context=64479).

You must also claim Parental Leave Pay within the [claiming timeframes](/claiming-timeframes-for-parental-leave-pay?context=64479).
