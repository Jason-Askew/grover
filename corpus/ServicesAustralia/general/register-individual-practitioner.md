# Register as an individual practitioner

You need to register and set up access as an individual practitioner to Provider Digital Access (PRODA), Health Professionals Online Services (HPOS) and Healthcare Identifiers (HI) for health professionals.

As an individual health practitioner, you need to register for individual online accounts and set up access.

You need to register for an [individual account with Provider Digital Access (PRODA)](/how-to-register-for-individual-proda-account).
