# Court order submission

Once you have a child maintenance order from the court you can then notify us of the order.

## on this page

-   [Online](#a1)
-   [By fax](#a2)
-   [By post](#a3)
-   [Confirming your notification](#a4)

You’ll also need to send us a sealed copy of the court order. You can do this a few ways.

## Online

If your Child support online account is linked to myGov, sign in and select **Upload documents** to submit the order.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.csaonline.gov.au/csos_site/csos-base/basePage.jsf?wec-appid=cssr_app&wec-locale=en_US)

## By fax

Fax the order to 1300 309 949.

## By post

Send the order to us in the mail to:

Services Australia  
Child Support  
GPO Box 9815  
Canberra ACT 2610

## Confirming your notification

We'll confirm we’ve received your court order with you and the other parent.

Read more about [objections to child support decisions](/objections-to-child-support-decisions?context=64107).
