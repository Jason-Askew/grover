# Look out for Medicare replacement card scams

Published: 13 February 2026

Scammers are sending text messages to people saying their Medicare card needs to be replaced or has been suspended.

![](/sites/default/files/styles/dhs_news_article_image/public/2026-02/dhs1804d10m7a4084.jpg?itok=4uYVUz1Q)

These text messages are designed to trick you into giving away your personal and financial information.

The link takes you to a fake website that asks for your bank card details to pay the postage for a replacement card.
