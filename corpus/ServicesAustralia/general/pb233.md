# Spinal muscular atrophy paediatric - nusinersen or risdiplam - initial authority application form (PB233)

Use this form to apply for initial PBS-subsidised treatment with nusinersen or risdiplam for patients aged 18 years or under with spinal muscular atrophy.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.
