# Exemptions to the limits of days

You need to tell us if you have an exemption to the limits on Parental Leave Pay days.

You can [share your Parental Leave Pay](/sharing-your-parental-leave-pay?context=64479) with your partner or another parent. There is a limit on how many days you can take at the same time.

If you have a partner, some days are reserved for them to use. There is a limit on the number of days reserved for your partner, which depends on your childâ€™s date of birth or adoption.

There are some exemptions to these limits.

Exemptions to the limit for taking days at the same time

Exemptions to the reserved days limit
