# Kinyarwanda

We provide information in your language to help you learn more about our payments and services.

Dutanga amakuru mu rurimi rwawe kugufasha kwiga byinshi kubijyanye n'ibihembo n'ama serivisi.

Niba uriko urakoresha assistive technology (ubuhanga bwa teknolojia bufasha) kandi udashobora gushyikira amakuru yacu yahinduwe, urashobora gushyikirana natwe [mu rurimi rwawe](/extra-help-when-calling-us).

If you are using assistive technology and cannot access our translated information, you can contact us [in your language](/extra-help-when-calling-us).
