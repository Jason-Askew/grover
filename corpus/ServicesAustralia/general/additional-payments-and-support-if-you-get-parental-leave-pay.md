# Getting additional payments and support

As a new parent getting Parental Leave Pay, you may be able to get other payments and support.

## on this page

-   [If your child is with disability](#a1)
-   [Other support](#a2)

After your baby’s birth you may be able to get [financial support for the costs of child care](/accessing-child-care).

If you’re on a low income, you may be able to get both:

-   [Family Tax Benefit](/family-tax-benefit)
-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment).

## If your child is with disability

You may also be eligible for these payments and services:

-   [Carer Allowance](/carer-allowance)
-   [Carer Payment](/carer-payment)
-   [Child Disability Assistance Payment](/child-disability-assistance-payment)
-   [Essential Medical Equipment Payment](/essential-medical-equipment-payment)
-   [Youth Disability Supplement](/youth-disability-supplement).

## Other support

We also provide support with living costs such as:

-   a [health care card](/health-care-card) to help with visits to the doctor
-   [Telephone Allowance](/telephone-allowance) to help with home phone and internet costs
-   [Rent Assistance](/rent-assistance) if you get certain payments from us.

To find help for new parents in your area go to the [Service Finder](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/apps/clkonline_cof/payment-service-finder/service-finder).
