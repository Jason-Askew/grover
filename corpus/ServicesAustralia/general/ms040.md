# Request for Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme claims information form (MS040)

Use this form to request Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) claims information for individuals and families from more than 2 years ago.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Request for Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme claims information form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/ms040-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").

This form should only be used to request PBS claims information from more than 2 years ago.
