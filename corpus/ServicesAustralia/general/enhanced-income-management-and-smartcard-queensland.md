# Enhanced Income Management and SmartCard in Queensland

Manage your money using enhanced Income Management in some parts of Queensland. It will help you meet essential household needs and expenses for you and your family.

Enhanced Income Management and SmartCard are available in these areas of Queensland:

-   Bundaberg and Hervey Bay region
-   Cape York and Doomadgee region
-   Livingstone, Logan and Rockhampton.

It is not available if you live in other areas of Queensland.

Select the area where you live for more details about enhanced Income Management and SmartCard.
