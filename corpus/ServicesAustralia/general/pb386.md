# Severe asthma paediatric - dupilumab - initial grandfather authority application form (PB386)

Use this form to apply for initial grandfathered PBS subsidised treatment with dupilumab for patients with uncontrolled severe asthma who is/was aged 6 to under 12 years prior to initiating non-PBS subsidised treatment.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).
