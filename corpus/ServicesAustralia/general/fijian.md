# VakaViti / Fijian

We provide information in your language to help you learn more about our payments and services.

Keitou vakarautaka na itukutuku e vakadewataki e na nomu vosa me vukei iko mo vulica tale eso na ka me baleta na ilavo keitou sauma kei na neitou veiqaravi.

Kevaka o vakayagataka tiko e dua na ka se misini me vukei iko se assistive technology ka o sega ni rawa ni taura na neitou itukutuku sa vakadewataki, o rawa ni veitaratara mai e [na nomu vosa](/extra-help-when-calling-us).

If you are using assistive technology and cannot access our translated information, you can contact us [in your language](/extra-help-when-calling-us).
