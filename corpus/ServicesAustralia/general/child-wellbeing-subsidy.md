# The Child Wellbeing subsidy

You need to meet certain criteria to be eligible for the Additional Child Care Subsidy Child Wellbeing.

You must:

-   be eligible for Child Care Subsidy
-   care for a child who is vulnerable or at risk of harm, abuse or neglect.

This subsidy gives extra help to families where a child is in any of these situations:

-   vulnerable or considered to be at risk of harm, abuse or neglect
-   in formal foster care or formal kinship placement
-   in the care of the state, territory or the Minister.
