# Other Services Australia scams

There are other types of scams you should beware of that target Services Australia customers.

Read more about other types of [Active scams](/active-scams?context=60271).

[

Fake Services Australia social media profiles

Scammers are setting up fake social media profiles pretending to be us and other government agencies.


](/fake-services-australia-social-media-profiles?context=60271)

[

Fake charities and No Interest Loans scams

Scammers are posing as charities and No Interest Loans providers, such as Good Shepherd or Salvation Army. They then try to steal from people who need financial support.


](/fake-charities-and-no-interest-loans-scams?context=60271)

[

Fake disaster relief agencies

Scammers are posing as disaster relief agencies to take advantage of people affected by natural disasters.


](/fake-disaster-relief-agencies?context=60271)

[

Fake LinkedIn messages

Scammers are pretending to be employers on LinkedIn.


](/fake-linkedin-messages?context=60271)

[

National Disability Insurance Scheme and Centrelink scam messages

Scammers are pretending to be from Services Australia, Centrelink, myGov or the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS).


](/national-disability-insurance-scheme-and-centrelink-scam-messages?context=60271)
