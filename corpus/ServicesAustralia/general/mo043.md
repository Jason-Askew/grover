# ROCS - midwife medical certificate for maternity form (MO043)

Use this form if a Professional Indemnity Insurer has determined that an insured midwife is eligible to participate in the Midwife Professional Indemnity (Commonwealth Contribution) Scheme for Run-off Cover.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

A medical practitioner for the midwife who is being assessed for eligibility under Run-off Cover must complete this form.

ROCS is the Run-off Cover Indemnity Scheme.
