# Express Plus Centrelink mobile app help guides

If you need help with your Express Plus Centrelink mobile app, read the online guides.

You need to have a [Centrelink online account](/centrelink-online-account) and [myGov](https://my.gov.au) account to use the app. If you don’t have a myGov account, you’ll need to create one and link it to your Centrelink online account. If you need help read the [myGov online guides](/help-for-mygov-online-accounts-and-mobile-apps?context=64107) or [watch our Express Plus Centrelink mobile app video presentation](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app).
