# How to manage court ordered periodic child maintenance

Notify us within 14 days of a court order being made about child maintenance.

Once you have read about court orders for child maintenance the next steps are to:

1.  collect any supporting documents
2.  submit your court order to us with any supporting documents.

[

Court order submission

Once you have a child maintenance order from the court you can then notify us of the order.

Change of circumstances
