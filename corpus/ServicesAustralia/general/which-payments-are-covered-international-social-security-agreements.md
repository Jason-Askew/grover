# Which payments are covered

Agreements cover some payments from Australia and the other country.

## on this page

-   [Australian payments](#a1)
-   [Payments from other countries](#a2)

Read which [countries have agreements with us](/which-countries-have-international-social-security-agreements-with-australia?context=22476) and what they cover.

Find information about agreements [in your language](/information-your-language).

## Australian payments

Agreements may cover some of these Australian payments, including:

-   [Age Pension](/age-pension)
-   [Disability Support Pension](/disability-support-pension)
-   [Carer Payment](/carer-payment)
-   [Parenting Payment single](/parenting-payment)
-   [Double Orphan Pension](/double-orphan-pension).

## Payments from other countries

Agreements may cover some of these types of payments from other countries, including:

-   old age or retirement pensions
-   invalidity or disability pensions
-   survivor pensions for widows, widowers and orphans.
