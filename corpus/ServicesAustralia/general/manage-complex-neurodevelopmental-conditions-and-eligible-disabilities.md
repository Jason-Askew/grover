# Manage complex neurodevelopmental conditions and eligible disabilities

Manage assessment and treatment services for complex neurodevelopmental conditions and eligible disabilities.

## on this page

-   [How to manage assessment and diagnosis services](#a1)
-   [How to manage treatment services](#a2)

## How to manage assessment and diagnosis services

Step 1. Find out the [requirements for assessment services](/requirements-services-for-complex-neurodevelopmental-conditions-and-eligible-disabilities?context=20).

We have information on:

-   patient eligibility
-   billing requirements
-   treatment and management plan
-   referrals to health professionals.

Step 2. Provide the service to your patient.

Step 3. [Bill complex neurodevelopmental conditions and eligible disabilities](/mbs-billing-for-complex-neurodevelopmental-conditions-and-eligible-disabilities?context=20) services using the correct Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS) item number.

You can check eligibility and look up item numbers through the MBS items online checker in HPOS. Read about [the relevant MBS item descriptions](https://www.mbsonline.gov.au/) at MBS Online website.

Step 4. Review assessment of diagnosis.

## How to manage treatment services

Step 1. Find out the [requirements of assessment and treatment services for complex neurodevelopmental conditions and eligible disabilities](/requirements-services-for-complex-neurodevelopmental-conditions-and-eligible-disabilities?context=20).

We have information on:

-   billing requirements
-   treatment and management plan
-   referrals to health professionals.

Step 2. Provide the service to your patient.

Step 3. Bill services using the correct MBS item number.

You can check eligibility and look up item numbers through the [MBS items online checker in HPOS](/check-mbs-item-numbers?context=20). Read about [the relevant MBS item descriptions](https://www.mbsonline.gov.au/) at MBS Online website.

Step 4. Review and manage the plan.
