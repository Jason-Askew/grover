# Claim for ABSTUDY form (SY019)

Use this form if you are a secondary or tertiary student, or Australian Apprentice claiming ABSTUDY.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Claim for ABSTUDY form](/sites/default/files/2024-10/sy019-2410en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Read more about the requirements for claiming [ABSTUDY](/abstudy).
