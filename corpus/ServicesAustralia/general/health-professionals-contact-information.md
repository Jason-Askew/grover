# Contact

Health professionals can contact us online, by phone, email or post.

## on this page

-   [Contacting us online](#a1)
-   [Contacting us by phone, email or post](#a2)

If you’re a health professional contact us using one of the options on this page.

If you’re a [customer](/contact-us), corporate stakeholder, or [business](/business-and-employer-contact-information), contact us using the contact options for you.

## Contacting us online

[Health Professional Online Services (HPOS)](/hpos?context=20) provides secure and convenient online services for health professionals and administrators. Using this service may save you having to email, call or write to us.

## Contacting us by phone, email or post

### What the phone costs are

Call costs from a landline to a:

-   **13 number** from anywhere in Australia cost the same as your other local calls
-   **1800 number (Freecall™)** are free.

These numbers may cost more to call from a public or mobile phone.

Some of our phone lines are either closed or have limited operating times on public holidays. We recommend you make your call on standard business days.
