# HI Service - application to deactivate, reactivate or retire a healthcare organisation form (HW044)

Use this form to deactivate, reactivate, or retire a Healthcare Provider Identifier Organisation (HPI-O) record within the Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can upload or complete this form online through HPOS. Read more about [form upload in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Download and complete the [HI Service - application to deactivate, reactivate or retire a healthcare organisation form](/sites/default/files/2025-08/hw044-2508en-f.pdf "PDF document").
