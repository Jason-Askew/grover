# Profit and Loss Statement form (SU580)

Use this form if you are a sole trader, subcontractor or a partner in a partnership that has started new employment or a new business.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Profit and Loss Statement form](/sites/default/files/2024-04/su580-2403en-f.pdf "PDF document").

You should also use this form if your level of business activity or income from self-employment has changed.

You will need to complete a [Business details form](/mod-f) if you have not told Centrelink about this business before.
