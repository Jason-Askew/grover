# How to get help

You can get help to use your online account.

## on this page

-   [Online guides](#a1)
-   [Contact us](#a2)
-   [myGov](#a3)

## Online guides

We have [Centrelink online guides](/centrelink-online-account-help-guides) to help you with online accounts and mobile apps.

## Contact us

For help, call the [Centrelink online services support hotline](/phone-us#onlineservicescentrelink) and select **option 3**.

If you need help with your payment you can [call us](/phone-us) on your regular payment line.

## myGov

If you need help with myGov, you can:

-   go to the [myGov help pages](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help) on the myGov website
-   ask the [myGov](https://my.gov.au/) digital assistant
-   read our [myGov online guides](/mygov-online-help-guides)
-   call the [myGov online services support hotline](/phone-us#mygovhelp).
