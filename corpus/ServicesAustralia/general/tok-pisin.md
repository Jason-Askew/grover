# Tok Pisin

We provide information in your language to help you learn more about our payments and services.

Mipela i givim infomesen long tok ples bilong yu long helpim yu long save moa long ol peimen na sevis bilong mipela.

Sapos yu wok long yusim teknoloji bilong helpim yu na i no inap long kisim infomesen long tok ples bilong yu, yu ken toktok long mipela/salim mesej long mipela [long tok ples bilong yu](/extra-help-when-calling-us).
