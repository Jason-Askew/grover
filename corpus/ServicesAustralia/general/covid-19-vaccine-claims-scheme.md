# COVID-19 vaccine claims scheme

The COVID-19 Vaccine Claims Scheme closed on 30 September 2024. New claims are not being accepted.

If you submitted your claim on or before 30 September 2024, your claim will be assessed and finalised.

You can find more information about the [COVID-19 vaccine claims scheme](https://www.health.gov.au/our-work/covid-19-vaccine-claims-scheme) and [policy](https://www.health.gov.au/resources/publications/covid-19-vaccine-claims-scheme-policy) on the Department of Health, Disability and Ageing website.

The privacy and security of your personal information is important to us, and we protect it by law. Find out about [your right to privacy](/your-right-to-privacy).
