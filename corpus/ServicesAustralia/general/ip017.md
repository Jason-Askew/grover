# Practice Incentives Program Indigenous Health Incentive patient registration and consent form (IP017)

Use this form to register an eligible Indigenous patient in the Practice Incentives Program (PIP) Indigenous Health Incentive (IHI).

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Practice Incentives Program Indigenous Health Incentive - patient registration and consent form](/sites/default/files/2024-10/ip017-2410en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Only use this form if your practice or Indigenous health service is already registered for the PIP IHI.
