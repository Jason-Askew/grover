# Application for approval, amendment, renewal or cancellation of an approved collection centre form (HW035)

Use this form to apply for or cancel an Approved Collection Centre (ACC).

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Download and complete the [Application for approval, amendment, renewal or cancellation of an approved collection centre form](/sites/default/files/2024-11/hw035-2412en-f.pdf "PDF document").

You can approve, renew and cancel your pathology registration online through HPOS.
