# Enhanced Income Management and SmartCard in Western Australia

Manage your money using enhanced Income Management in some parts of Western Australia. It will help you meet essential household needs and expenses for you and your family.

Enhanced Income Management and SmartCard are available in these areas of Western Australia:

-   Goldfields region
-   Kimberley region
-   Kununurra and Wyndham region in the East Kimberley
-   Metropolitan Perth
-   Ngaanyatjarra (NG) Lands and Kiwirrkurra
-   Peel region.

It is not available if you live in other areas of Western Australia.

Select the area where you live for more details about enhanced Income Management and SmartCard.
