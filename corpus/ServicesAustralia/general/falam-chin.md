# Falam Chin

We provide information in your language to help you learn more about our payments and services.

Tangkapeknak le servicepawl kan tuah mi nan theihthiam ih nan zirnak dingah nan ṭawng in hi thu theihternak thu hi kan lo pe ding a si.

Techonology thilri na hmang nan thu hla theihnak let mi nan hmu thei lo a sile, [nan mai ṭawng in](/extra-help-when-calling-us) in run pehtlai thei a si.

If you are using assistive technology and cannot access our translated information, you can contact us [in your language](/extra-help-when-calling-us).
