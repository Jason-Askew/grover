# How to manage your payment

Your options and obligations for Disability Support Pension.

To find more information about your payments, go to **My payments** in your [Centrelink online account](/centrelink-online-account). There, you’ll be able to see information including how we work out your payment and how much we’ll pay you.

We have tips, tools and support to help you budget, deal with debt and [manage your money](/manage-your-money) during your lifetime.

You can arrange regular deductions from your Centrelink payments to pay bills. Read more about [Centrepay](/centrepay).
