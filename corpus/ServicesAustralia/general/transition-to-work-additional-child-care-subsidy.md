# The Transition to Work subsidy

You need to meet certain criteria to get Additional Child Care Subsidy Transition to Work.

## Eligibility

You must:

-   be eligible for [Child Care Subsidy](/child-care-subsidy)
-   meet the [transition to work requirements](/transition-to-work-participation-requirements-additional-child-care-subsidy?context=41866)
-   be within the time limit for each transition to work participation type
-   have a family income of $85,279 or less.

You must also be getting one of these payments:

-   [Parenting Payment](/parenting-payment)
-   [JobSeeker Payment](/jobseeker-payment)
-   [Disability Support Pension](/disability-support-pension)
-   [Youth Allowance](/youth-allowance)
-   [Carer Payment](/carer-payment)
-   [Special Benefit](/special-benefit)
-   [Austudy](/austudy)
-   [Farm Household Allowance](/farm-household-allowance)
-   a means tested [ABSTUDY](/abstudy) payment.

If you have [mutual obligation](/mutual-obligation-requirements?context=22196) or [participation requirements](/meeting-participation-requirements-if-youre-under-35) as part of your income support payment, you must also have either an active:

-   Job Plan
-   Participation Plan, if you get [Disability Support Pension](/disability-support-pension).
