# Authorising a person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf form (SS313)

Use this form to authorise a person or organisation in Australia to be your nominee so they can enquire or act on your behalf with Centrelink.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Authorising a person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf form](/sites/default/files/2023-08/ss313-2308en-f.pdf "PDF document").

## When to use this form

Use this form to authorise a person or organisation in Australia to be your nominee so they can enquire or act on your behalf with Centrelink.

If you and your proposed nominee both have Centrelink online account linked to myGov, you can sign in now to add a nominee online.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/nominee-arrangements/relationships-summary)

You can read our online guide for help to [add a nominee](/centrelink-online-account-help-add-nominee).

Read more about getting [someone to act](/someone-to-act-for-you-with-medicare-centrelink-aged-care-or-child-support) for you.
