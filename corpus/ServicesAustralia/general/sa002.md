# Claim for Age Pension and Pension Bonus form (SA002)

Use this form to claim Age Pension and Pension Bonus.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Before you start your claim for Age Pension and Pension Bonus, you should read both of the following:

-   [information you need to know about your claim for Age Pension and Pension Bonus form](/ci006)
-   [how to prepare to claim Age Pension](/how-to-prepare-to-claim-age-pension).

Once done, you can download, print and complete the [Claim for Age Pension and Pension Bonus form](/sites/default/files/2023-05/sa002-2305en.pdf "PDF document").

Remember to sign the form before you submit it with the [Income and Assets form](/sa369) and [supporting documents](/supporting-documents-for-age-pension).

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, sign in now to upload your form and documents.
