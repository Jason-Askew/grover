# Application for Post Separation Income to be Excluded form (CS4120)

Use this form to apply to exclude additional income from your child support assessment. This is income you earned after you separated from the child's other parent.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Application for Post Separation Income to be Excluded form](/sites/default/files/2023-11/cs4120-2310en-f.pdf "PDF document").

This must be income you have earned in addition to income you earned before your separation. For example it doesn't include any of the following:

-   income from a second job you had before separation
-   overtime if you were already doing overtime before separation
-   standard pay rises.
