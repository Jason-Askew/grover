# Application for a Medicare provider number or prescriber number for allied health, other primary health care, and non-medical health professionals form (HW093)

Use this form if you are applying for an initial or additional Medicare provider number or a PBS prescriber number or both.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Application for a Medicare provider number or prescriber number for allied health, other primary health care, and non-medical health professionals form](/sites/default/files/2025-10/hw093-2511en-f.pdf "PDF document").

You can also apply for Medicare provider numbers using Health Professional Online Services (HPOS).
