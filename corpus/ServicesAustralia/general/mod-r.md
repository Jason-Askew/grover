# Real estate details form (MOD R)

Use this form to let us know your share of a property's asset value and income.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Real estate details form](/sites/default/files/modr-2007en-f.pdf "PDF document").

Remember to sign it before you submit it with your supporting documents.

If your Centrelink online account is linked to myGov, sign in to upload your form and documents.

[Sign in to myGov](https://www.centrelink.gov.au/sso/sps/oidc/rp/clkcust/kickoff/mygov?return_uri=https://my.gov.au/en/myaccount/dashboard&Target=/apps/custonline_entry/online-services)

You can also use the [Express Plus Centrelink mobile app](/express-plus-centrelink-mobile-app).

Read about how to [submit your documents online](/upload-your-centrelink-documents-online?context=64107).

If you can't upload your form and supporting documents you can submit them at a [service centre](/find-us) or [by post](/write-to-us).
