# Medicare Compensation Recovery Notice of past benefits request form (MO026)

Use this form to get a copy of your Medicare history and care services statement, to find out what benefits and subsidies we will recover from your compensation.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

Download and complete the [Medicare Compensation Recovery Notice of past benefits request form](/sites/default/files/2025-06/mo026-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").

The injured person, claimant, authorised third party or the person paying your compensation can request a statement be issued. This form is the first step in requesting a [Notice of past benefits](/notice-past-benefits-for-medicare-compensation-recovery) under Section 21 of the _Health and Other Services (Compensation) Act 1995_.
