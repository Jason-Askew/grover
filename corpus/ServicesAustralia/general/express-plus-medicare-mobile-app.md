# Express Plus Medicare mobile app

The app retired on 1 November 2025. Use the myGov app to manage your Medicare information.

The Express Plus Medicare mobile app is no longer available.

You can continue to access all your Medicare services and [Digital Medicare card](/how-to-access-your-digital-medicare-card) securely through the myGov app. You can also access your Medicare online account through the myGov website.

Download the myGov app from the [myGov website](https://my.gov.au/en/about/help/mygov-app) today.
