# Atypical haemolytic uraemic syndrome (aHUS) - eculizumab or ravulizumab - recommencement authority application form (PB176)

Use this form to apply to recommence PBS-subsidised treatment with eculizumab or ravulizumab for aHUS.

How to use our forms

You must [download our forms and fill them in](/how-to-use-our-forms) so they are processed quickly. You can also electronically sign some of our forms. We have help available if you use assistive technology or need an interpreter.

You can [upload this form in HPOS](/upload-forms-hpos).

Download and complete the [atypical haemolytic uraemic syndrome (aHUS) - eculizumab or ravulizumab - recommencement authority application form](/sites/default/files/2025-05/pb176-2506en-f.pdf "PDF document").
